Employee Benefits

Union Pacific Technologies benefits program is an important part of your total compensation. Through its array of programs, the company offers many valuable and tax-effective programs designed to give you a solid base for financial protection.

Our Flexible Benefits Plan lets you tailor benefits to fit personal needs and those of your family. Other programs offer significant opportunities to accumulate capital, provide for income during retirement, assist in paying for higher education, and help locate service providers for different needs. Benefits include:
  • Medical offers you three medical options. Plans are comprehensive and cover a broad range of medical services and supplies. If you live in an area where a Health Maintenance Organization (HMO) is available, it is included as an additional option.
  • Retirement provides you with a lifetime income.
  • Thrift Plan (401K plan) allows you to build capital for your retirement years and includes company matching funds.
  • Dental covers most dental needs, from checkups and routine care to orthodontics for dependent children.
  • Wellness provides you and your family coverage for wellness checkups. It helps pay for eye and hearing exams, corrective lenses, and hearing aids.
  • Flexible Spending Accounts allow you to pay for certain health care and dependent care expenses on a before-tax basis.
  • Term Life and Accident Insurance is available for you and eligible dependents. In addition, you can also choose survivor income protection.
  • Long-Term Disability provides income until retirement if you become sick or disabled for an indefinite period of time.
  • Education Assistance provides financial help to defray the costs of company-approved higher education courses.
  • Child Care and Elder Care Referral assists you in identifying child-care and elder-care providers.
  • Matching Gifts offer company matching of your contributions to certain educational, cultural and medical institutions.
  • Employee Assistance offers confidential programs to assist employees and their families with substance abuse, family- and stress-related problems and other issues.
  • Other Benefits include short-term disability, business travel accident, merit scholarship, adoption assistance, vacations, and holidays.
Union Pacific reserves the right to change or discontinue these plans at any time.





The Application Process

If you are interested in UPT and your qualifications, skills, education and experience meet the requirements of one or more of the positions advertised, please email your resume and salary requirements to upt-careers@notes.up.com or send your resume to:

UPT Human Resources
7930 Clayton Road
St. Louis, MO 63117
c/o WEB

Your resume will be reviewed by representatives from our HR and technical staffs. If you are brought in for an interview, you will receive an application to be completed and returned to the HR department ASAP. HR will arrange further interviews if necessary. Once an offer is extended, HR will be in contact with you to arrange a pre-employment physical and drug screen, coordinate your first day on the job, and conduct an orientation regarding UPT's policies, benefits and environment.

Unsolicited resumes are not accepted. Only resumes in response to an advertised position are accepted.

The Hiring Process

Union Pacific Technologies interviews candidates during our fall and spring recruiting seasons -- the majority from college campuses -- for jobs in information systems related positions. Candidates fill out employment applications, which request information on education/degree(s), grade point average, and related experience.

Those who make it through the initial technical review are invited for an interview and location visit. From there, Human Department representatives notify finalists for the positions. It is the policy and practice of Union Pacific Technologies to provide a work environment free from discrimination. This policy extends to all terms and conditions of employment.
