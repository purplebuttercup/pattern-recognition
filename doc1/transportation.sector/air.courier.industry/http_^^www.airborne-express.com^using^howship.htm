How To Ship
How to Get Started
Call 1-800-AIRBORNE (1-800-247-2676) and we'll do the rest. We can open your account. Send you free shipping supplies. Schedule your packages for pickup. And more. Most important, our professionals can help you select the most time- and cost-effective shipping options, giving you maximum value for your shipping dollar.

Open an Account and Save Up to 32%
Open an account with Airborne Express and you can save up to 32% on your overnight express shipments alone. You'll receive our best shipping value, including special rates and exceptional service. Call 1-800-AIRBORNE (1-800-247-2676).

To order FREE shipping supplies...
Call 1-800-AIRBORNE (1-800-247-2676). Supplies are designed to make shipping convenient and include airbills, envelopes, boxes and tubes. Shipping today? Call early and your pickup driver can bring you whatever you need.

Shipping with Airborne Is As Easy As 1, 2, 3

  1. Call for pickup.
    Call 1-800-AIRBORNE (1-800-247-2676) to schedule a pickup. If you ship regularly, Airborne can arrange a daily pickup time for your added convenience.
  2. Use a drop box.
    For last-minute shipments, drop your package at one of Airborne's many conveniently located drop boxes. Call to confirm the latest drop-off time in your area.
  3. Bring us your package.
    For the latest possible drop off that still makes the nightly flight, bring your package to any Airborne Express terminal.
