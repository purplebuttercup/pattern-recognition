EDI: Electronic Data Interchange
Airborne Express has several Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) products to help increase efficiency and productivity in your transportation process. You can process packages, create management reports, produce invoices, track shipments, pay bills, transfer funds and more -- all electronically, saving you time and money.

LibraSM: Shipping and Tracking Hardware and Software
Save hours of data entry time and reduce errors by using LIBRA, Airborne's FREE computerized shipping system for volume shippers. LIBRA weighs, rates and labels each shipment in a matter of seconds. LIBRA also stores receiver addresses, prints invoices, generates management reports, tracks shipments and more. For overseas shipments, LIBRA INTERNATIONAL simplifies documentation preparation, forwards export declarations to U.S. Customs and cross-checks destination entries.

LIGHTSHIPTM TRACKER: Shipment Tracking Software
Track domestic and international shipments, provide proof of delivery and create shipping reports right at your PC with LIGHTSHIPTM TRACKER. Installs in minutes and performs with either Windows® or Windows 95®, and with a variety of modems.

LIGHTSHIPTM WORLD DIRECTORY: International Service Guide
The information you need to ship to more than 200 countries is right at your fingertips with LIGHTSHIPTM WORLD DIRECTORY. Access acceptable commodities, documentation requirements, transit times, maximum declared values, customs requirements and service and billing options. Installs in minutes and performs with Windows® or Windows 95®, and with a variety of modems.
