THANKSGIVING DAY HOLIDAY SCHEDULE

On Wednesday, November 26, 1997, preceding Thanksgiving Day, there will be normal pickup and delivery service.

EXPRESS SERVICE

  • Express packages picked up Wednesday, November 26, will be delivered Friday, November 28. Delivery by noon in primary delivery areas.

NEXT AFTERNOON SERVICE (NAS)

  • Next Afternoon packages weighing five pounds or less picked up Wednesday, November 26, will be delivered Friday, November 28. Delivery by 3:00 p.m. in primary delivery areas. Saturday delivery is not available with NAS.

SECOND DAY SERVICE (SDS)

  • Second Day packages picked up Wednesday, November 26, will be delivered Monday, December 1. Delivery by 5:00 p.m. in primary delivery areas. Saturday delivery is not available with SDS.

INTERNATIONAL EXPRESS SERVICE
Pickups made in the U.S. on Wednesday, November 26, will receive normal service to most countries:

  • Shipments arriving in the U.S. and clearing Customs on Wednesday, November 26, will be delivered Friday, November 28.
  • Shipments arriving in the U.S. and clearing Customs after Wednesday, November 26, will be delivered Monday, December 1.

DROP BOX SERVICE
There will be no Drop Box service on Thursday, November 27.

EMERGENCY SERVICE - 365 DAYS A YEAR
If you have a shipment that just can’t wait, Sky Courier, a subsidiary of Airborne Express, offers Same-Day/Best-Flight-Out Service 24 hours-a-day, 365 days a year. Call 1-800-336-3344 for either U.S. or International Express shipments.

24-HOUR COMMUNICATIONS CENTER
Although your local Airborne Express office will be closed on Thanksgiving Day, you may contact the Airborne Express 24-Hour Communications Center anytime by simply dialing 1-800-AIRBORNE (1-800-247-2676). If you’re shipping perishables over the holiday, please contact your local Airborne Express office to schedule these shipments.

Thank you for shipping with Airborne Express. Have a safe and happy holiday!
