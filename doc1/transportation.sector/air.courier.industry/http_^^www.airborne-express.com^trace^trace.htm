Airbill Number:

To track your shipment every step of the way, anytime, anywhere in the world, simply enter the airbill number you wish to track, click on the 'Track Shipment Now' button and receive detailed information about the status of your shipment in just seconds.

If you need more information call 1-800-AIRBORNE (1-800-247-2676) in the U.S.

Multi-Shipment Tracking Via E-mail

To track more than one shipment at a time, simply send an e-mail to track.it@airborne.com, listing up to 20 airbill numbers separated by commas. You'll receive a return e-mail with detailed information about the status of each of your shipments.

Additional Instructions

  • The airbill numbers must be in the message area of the e-mail, not the subject line.
  • Enter only airbill numbers with separating commas. No additional text is needed.
  • Your e-mail software must be correctly set up with your e-mail address as the default reply-to address or else you won't receive the shipment information back.
