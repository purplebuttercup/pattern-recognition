World Airways serves the air cargo industry three ways.

First, we charter cargo planes to other airlines for long-term service.

Second, we sometimes have cargo planes available to charter on short-term (one or few trips) to most anywhere in the world.

Third, we carry cargo in the cargo compartments of our passenger planes.


World has MD-11F, MD-11CF and DC-10-30CF freighter aircraft. The MD-11 carries 194,000 lbs up to 4,600 miles, while the DC-10 carries 143,000 lbs up to 3,900 miles.

For further information, call World Cargo at 800-CARGO-20.
