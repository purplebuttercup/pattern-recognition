MULTI-SHIPMENT TRACKING
Track up to 20 shipments at one time by simply sending an e-mail to track.it@airborne-express.com. List your airbill numbers separated by commas, and you'll receive a return e-mail with detailed information on the status of each of your shipments.

LIGHTSHIPTM TRACKER Tracking Software
Track domestic and international shipments, provide proof of delivery and create shipping reports right at your PC with LIGHTSHIPTM TRACKER. Installs in minutes and performs with either Windows® or Windows 95®, and with a variety of modems.

LIGHTSHIPTM WORLD DIRECTORY International Service Guide
The information you need to ship to more than 200 countries is right at your fingertips with LIGHTSHIPTM WORLD DIRECTORY. Access acceptable commodities, documentation requirements, transit times, maximum declared values, customs requirements and service and billing options. Installs in minutes and performs with Windows® or Windows 95®.

To get your free copy of LIGHTSHIPTM TRACKER or WORLD DIRECTORY just call 1-800-AIRBORNE (1-800-247-2676).

AIRBORNE GLOBAL POST
Airborne Express customers can receive as much as 30 percent off their international mail cost and receive transit times generally 1-2 days faster than standard international mail service. Airborne Express will pick up unsorted and unstamped packages, transfer them to the Airborne Express airport hub in Wilmington, Ohio, and ultimately to a mail facility in Folcroft, PA.

POSTAL CODE ROUTING by Airborne Express
In order to speed routing and improve accuracy in international shipping estimates, Airborne implemented a new routing system for international deliveries to the top 15 destination countries. Packages routed by city code now receive postal code designations similar to the U.S. Postal Service's ZIP code. Initially, Postal Code Routing will be available in: Argentina, Australia, Belgium, Brazil, Canada, Denmark, Finland, Germany, Mexico, The Netherlands, New Zealand, Sweden, Taiwan, Thailand and The United Kingdom. A total of 30 countries will be added to the system enabling Airborne to better access those markets currently receiving more than 80 percent of our international shipments.

For more information on our International Services please call 1-800-ABX-INTL (229-4685).
