Shipping Tips

  1. Always put a weight on your airbill.
  2. Use the packaging you get from your carrier -- it's free.
  3. Be sure to designate "letter" or "package" on airbill.
  4. Always address express shipments to a street address, never a P.O. Box.
  5. Never guess at a zip.
  6. Before you ship Saturday Delivery, make sure someone will be there to receive.
  7. Avoid over-insuring.
  8. Don't over pack.
  9. Shipping liquids or chemicals? Call your carrier first.
  10. Select the service level you need based on the required delivery time.
