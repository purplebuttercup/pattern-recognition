Contour Cutting Machine

This contour cutting machine is used for cutting two-dimensional contours out of a polystyrene block by means of a resistance wire. The two-dimensional contour is made by two CNC arbors, one moving the resistance wire in a vertical direction and the other moving the conveyor belt on which the polystyrene block is residing. The CNC contour data can be entered either directly to the contour cutting machine or on a separate CNC programming workstation which is not included in the scope of supplies.

The machine has non-motorized roller conveyors in front and in the back of the cutting bench to facilitate handling of the blicks during the cutting process.
