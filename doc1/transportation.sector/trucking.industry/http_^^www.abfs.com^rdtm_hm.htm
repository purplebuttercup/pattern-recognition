ABF Home Page
"Safe driving techniques must be used at all times. Anticipate what COULD happen and be ready for it."

After 24 years as a road driver, Harold Morris knows what safety is all about. Domiciled at ABF's St. Louis terminal, he has earned numerous safety awards.

One of Harold's major concerns is the young people who speed and follow too close -- two things that always seem to go hand in hand, he says. "They don't realize how long it's going to take that car to stop. They think it can stop in an instant, and my message to them is to please SLOW DOWN and back away from other traffic. They have to grow up fast when they get behind the wheel of a car. It's a big responsibility."

Harold is proud of the job he does as a driver and proud of the trucking industry. He feels the industry has contributed greatly to the wealth of this country, especially since the advent of the interstate highway system which has "allowed us to move freight speedily from border to border and coast to coast."
