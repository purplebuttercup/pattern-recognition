Picture Tube Disassembling Device
with room exhaust system for 15" to 32" picture tubes

This picture tube disassembling device enables the operator to open television and monitor screens automatically, in a safe and environmentally compatible manner, in order to sort and recycle the various components. The opening process itself generates neither dust nor noise and leaves no residues to be disposed of. In a single-shift operation, approx. 30,000 to 40,000 picture tubes can be opened per year, reaching a system availabilty of 95%.

The picture tube disassembling device is used for automatically disassembling picture tubes with a height-width ratio of 4:3 and a major diagonal of 30 to 80 cm (15 to 32 inch) into their component parts. It consists essentially of a basic frame (anadized aluminum profiles), a swivel table top, a pneumatically driven elevating platform, a vacuum-cleaned separation chamber, an electric band heater guide, a water cooling system, a transparent swivel door in the front made of acrylic glass with a thickness of 8 mm (0.32 inch), paneling, a supply of heating current and user-programmable PLC.

Principle dimensions of the disassembling device:
Width: 1680 mm (66.14 inch)
Height: 2200 mm (86.61 inch)
Depth: 1330 mm (52.36 inch)
Height of table top:   815 mm (32.09 inch)

This picture tube disassembling device meets all currently effective UVV and EC machine guidelines. It has been certified with the GS mark by the Berlin-Brandenburg TÜV (Technical Control Board of Germany) and the CE mark.

Option: Complete disassembly line for electronic scrap, including picture tube disassembling device.
