Caliber Technology is comprised of three primary groups:

Communications Services
Computer Services
Technology Applications

Communications Services

Communications Services designs, implements, and manages tools providing the flow of information among databases, computer platforms, and geographical locations. These include the network architecture to support data movement and delivery to the customer in acceptable time frames.

This group is also responsible for the design and management of the voice network. This function also includes end user computing, the Information Center, and local network development.

Computer Services

Computer Services provides technical support to and operations of mainframe computers at our data centers located in Akron, OH, and Pittsburgh, PA. This group also operates the network control and help desk functions that are staffed seven days a week, 24 hours a day. The facilities are secure and protected against power disruptions. Multiple Caliber companies have or plan to have equipment installed in these data centers.

Technology Applications

Technology Applications is responsible for the evaluation, development, maintenance, and support of electronic commerce facilities for the Caliber operating companies.

This group is also responsible for performing research and the ongoing evaluation of innovative technologies and processes that will provide a competitive advantage to the Caliber operating companies.
