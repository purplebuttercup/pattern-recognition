Machinery

Rougher

This rougher is used for preparing commercially available granulated EPS before processing it in foaming machines.

It runs discontinuously and achieves a specific gravity of approx. 12 to 30 grams/liter in one foaming operation.

In the rougher, raw EPS material is brought to a lower specific gravity by adding steam. If necessary, this process can be repeated several times to produce various qualities of the EPS material, up to impact sound quality.

The rougher can be run in manual or automatic mode as desired.
