RPS (Small-Package): serves shippers in the small-package market. RPS currently serves 100% of the U.S., Canada and Puerto Rico through a network of 370 terminals, including 26 hubs. RPS also offers services to 100% of Mexico as well as 28 European countries through joint cooperation agreements with Estafeta Mexicana, S.A. de C.V. and General Parcel Logistics GmbH, respectively.

Viking Freight (Regional Freight): a regional less-than-truckload (LTL) carrier that provides next- and second-day service to 10 western states as well as Alaska and Hawaii. Viking has a long tradition of posting an on-time service record that is consistently among the best in the regional LTL market.

Roberts Express (Expedited): the largest surface expedited carrier in North America. Roberts specializes in time-definite, door-to-door delivery of critical shipments in North America and Europe - anytime, anywhere, guaranteed.

Caliber Logistics (Logistics): a contract logistics provider which designs, implements and manages a broad range of customized logistics solutions for its customers. Caliber Logistics' services include transportation management, dedicated contract service, finished product distribution and just-in-time manufacturing support programs.

Each operating unit is supported by Caliber Technology, which provides integrated information services to customers, Caliber companies and others.
