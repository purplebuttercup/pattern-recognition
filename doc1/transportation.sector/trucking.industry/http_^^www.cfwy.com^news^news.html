REDWOOD SYSTEMS TO MANAGE LOGISTICS
FOR PHILIPS/LUCENT CONSUMER PRODUCTS

GUADALAJARA, Jalisco (Mexico), November 13, 1997 -- California-based Redwood Systems, a supply-chain logistics management company for multi-national corporations, will provide logistics solutions, computerized inventory tracking, warehousing management and trans-border transportation services for Philips Consumer Communications.
