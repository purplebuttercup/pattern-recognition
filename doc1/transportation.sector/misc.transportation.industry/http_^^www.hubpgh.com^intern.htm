Hub Group International - A "World-Wide" Logistics Company

Occupying the role of an international, intermodal, point to point transportation services company, "Hub Group International" provides worldwide logistics services to a broad range of U.S. and overseas importers and exporters. By virtue of our joint venture with Norton Lilly International, Inc. one of the U.S.'s largest and oldest full service steamship agencies, the Hub Group can provide a truly seamless and transparent multi-modal transportation product serving the global marketplace. The Hub Group's strength in managing domestic shipments coupled with Norton Lily's experience on the International side allows us to bring core competencies to bear on a very complicated and often frustrating endeavor.

Concentrating on the European, Asian and Latin American markets, Hub International can design cost competitive total transportation solutions, customized to meet all of your needs and requirements. Transacted with a single point of contact and under a single invoice, services may include foreign and domestic precarriage and oncarriage, ocean transportation services, warehousing and distribution, transloading, and shipment tracking.
Don't "miss the boat". Call the professionals at the Hub Group to explore the most convenient and practical approach to International shipping available anywhere.
