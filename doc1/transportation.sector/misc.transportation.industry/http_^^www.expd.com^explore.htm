Maps, Addresses and Phone Numbers

  • Infospace TM - Telephone yellow pages by subject, white pages, Government blue pages, toll-free numbers and fax numbers for U.S. and Canada.
  • MapQuest - Type in an address in the continental U.S. and get a street map of the local neighborhood. Zoom out to get a bird's eye view.
  • Switchboard TM - Type in a name of company or individual, city and state. Get a phone number and address information.

How to find other sites on the World Wide Web

There are many searchable indexes available on the Worldwide Web (WWW). Each is updated continually. Because the Internet is constantly changing and is so vast, no single index offers a comprehensive listing of the information available, nor are any two indexes identical. So, if you don't find what you are looking for in one index, choose another and try again.Click here to start your search.
