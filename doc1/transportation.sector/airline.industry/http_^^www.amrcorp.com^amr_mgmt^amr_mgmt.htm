AMR Investments, a wholly owned subsidiary of AMR Corporation, is an experienced provider of investment advisory services to institutional and retail markets.

  
 

 

 

AMR Airline Services provides passenger, ramp, cargo and other contract and management services to more than 200 leading airlines and airport authorities at about 60 locations throughout the world.

  
 

 

AMR Combs is the premier provider of corporate aviation services through a network of 14 FBOs (Fixed Base Operators) throughout North America.

  
 

 

AMR Global Logistics works with each client - in partnership - to develop and implement the most cost-effective methods to process and distribute their products or services.

  
 

 

AMR Training Group (AMRTG) provides a full range of training products and services, as well as extensive meeting facilities designed for the corporate marketplace.

  
 

 

Business JetSolutions is a jet ownership program for individuals and companies who need the flexibility and efficiency of a business jet, but who up to now thought the cost was out of reach.

  
 

 

TeleService Resources (TSR) is the world's largest provider of third-party, private label hotel and airline reservations, and one of the largest telemarketing operations in the United States.
