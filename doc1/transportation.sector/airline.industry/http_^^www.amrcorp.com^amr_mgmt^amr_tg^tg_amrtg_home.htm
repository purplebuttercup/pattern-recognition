AMR Training Group

AMR Training Group assists success-driven companies in meeting the challenges of operating in a rapidly changing marketplace. Drawing on methods American Airlines developed to train hundreds of thousands of employees, AMRTG offers its expertise to diverse companies around the globe.
Through skill-based instruction and accelerative learning techniques, AMRTG can help you meet your business goals by creating a high-performance work environment. Our suite of market-tested products are specificlly designed to increase employee performance and decrease training time and cost. We understand how training impacts productivity and profitability.

When working with your company, AMRTG will develop a custom-designed program to improve on-the-job results. We can help you define the skills employees need to position your business for success.
