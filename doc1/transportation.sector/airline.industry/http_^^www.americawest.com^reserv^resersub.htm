We proudly announce the release of Effortless Ticketing OnlineTM, a tool for consumers to shop flight schedules and fares, book reservations and issue tickets using the convenience of the Internet.

Simply select your travel cities, dates and number of passengers. Effortless Ticketing Online will show you flight schedules, fares and allow you to book your reservation and pay for your electronic tickets with just a few keystrokes.

If you'd prefer not to use Effortless Ticketing Online, you can still call your Professional Travel Agent, visit a City Ticket Office, or call America West Airlines at 1-800-2 FLY AWA.

We know you have a choice when selecting an airline for your air travel and we thank you for choosing America West Airlines.
