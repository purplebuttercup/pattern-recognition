The deHavilland Dash 8 incorporates many of the design features found on larger jet aircraft. Passengers enjoy a large cabin atmosphere with exemplary headroom, generous overhead storage and comfortable 2+2 seating. The Dash 8 is manufactured by Bombardier Regional Aircraft, the world's leading supplier of regional aircraft.
  • 37 Passengers
  • In-flight service
  • Lavatory
  • Ceiling 25,000 ft.
  • Cruising speed 287 m.p.h
  • Two Pratt & Whitney PW120 turbine engines
  • Pressurized
  • Overhead luggage compartments
