Mesaba Airlines' entire turbo-prop fleet will soon consist of new Saab 340 cabin class jet-props. This technologically-advanced aircraft manufactured by Saab Scania Aerospace features generous personal space and ample storage compartments. Additionally, passengers will enjoy reclining leather seats in a 2+1 configuration.
  • 34 Passengers
  • In-flight service
  • Lavatory
  • Ceiling 25,000 ft.
  • Cruising speed 300 m.p.h
  • Two General Electric CT-7 turbine engines
  • Pressurized
  • Overhead luggage compartments
  • Reclining leather seats
