The RJ Avroliner cross section is the most versatile and adaptable in its class. Mesaba will be the first US regional to operate a 2 class seating configuration, offering very comfortable 4-abreast first class seating and 5-abreast economy seating.

The RJ85 offers the baggage capacity and service standards demanded by passengers connecting to international services and it has the performance and economic flexibility to serve a complex and varied regional network. The RJ85 is part of the RJ Avroliner family - a family specifically developed to satisfy the needs of todays large regional carriers.

Economy Class

Technology where it counts!

Featuring Cat IIIA all-weather landing capabilities and digital avionics to Arinc 700 standard, the RJ Avroliner glass cockpit has been designed to exploit technology where it brings specific advantages to regional operators.

FADEC engine control, a unique feature for an aircraft in this category, offers the additional advantages of smooth power management and cooler starting temperatures.
