We give seniors a lift

Senior citizens are valued travelers at America West. That's why we offer seniors several ways to save when flying with us.

If you're age 62 and over, you can save 10% on most published fares. What's more, the 10% savings applies for one companion passenger of any age that's traveling with you on the same itinerary. It's our way of expressing thanks for choosing America West Airlines.

As a senior, you'll also like the many travel options we provide. America West serves more than 90 destinations across the 48 contiguous states, Canada and Mexico, giving you the chance to visit friends and relatives or jet off to exciting vacation spots. Imagine relaxing on a beach in sunny Mexico, fishing amid the scenic beauty of Canada, or strolling through the wine country of northern California. At America West, we can open the door to wonderful new adventures.

We take special pride in catering to our senior travelers, and we're pleased to say that seniors have noticed. Recently, readers of Arizona Senior World Newspapers, a Phoenix-based publication, voted America West as their favorite airline.

From our full service to our discount fares, America West is always glad to give seniors a lift.
