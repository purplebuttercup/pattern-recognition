Working for Change in Our Communities

    We understand that corporate responsibility extends far beyond gaining market share and increasing shareholder value. Being a good corporate citizen requires much more than providing superior service and products. It demands returning some of our good fortune back to the communities that help support Ultramar Diamond Shamrock Corporation.

    That's why we're committed to improving the quality of life and well-being of the people in each community where Ultramar Diamond Shamrock operates. Through ongoing involvement with a number of local, regional, and national charitable, non-profit organizations, Ultramar Diamond Shamrock is helping these communities face environmental and social challenges in each of 10 states and eastern Canada we call home. From financial contributions and employee volunteer time to in-store public education campaigns and donated resources, our company and its employees continue our proud tradition of giving a helping hand to neighbors and neighborhoods in need where our employees and customers live, work, and shop.
