About the Fueling Card Program

Crown provides commercial fueling with convenient stations, controls, reports, and savings. Go to our Apply Online secure page to start an account today. You will find our directions off the interstate helpful on the station directory. We are committed to providing outstanding customer service. See our Services page for a special offer if you Apply Online before December 31, 1997!

Our fleet cards require the use of Driver ID's (a PIN) and odometer entries at the time of the sale.  The card is assigned to a vehicle.  You receive monthly vehicle analysis reports and exception reports to show you who, what, when, and where you fueled. Updating your driver or vehicle list is as easy as calling our 24-hour customer service toll-free number.  You may select a Standard Card Activity Summary if you don't need detailed reports.  Thank you for your business. Enjoy your visit and feel free to give us your feedback on our program.

About Crown

Crown Central Petroleum Corporation, with headquarters in Baltimore, is a marketer/refiner of quality fuels. Our refineries in Houston and Tyler produce 152,000 barrels per day. The Colonial Pipeline and Texas Eastern Pipeline transports fuel for wholesale and retail sales to our 14 product terminals. CrownCen Marketing Company, a division, sells fuel in the Mid-Atlantic and Southeast through 340 retail stations. Our commercial fueling program began in 1986 and serves over 3300 accounts. You may download a complete list of our stations from the download area.
