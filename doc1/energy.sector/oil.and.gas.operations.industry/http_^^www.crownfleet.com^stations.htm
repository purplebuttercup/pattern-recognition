How Fuel Gets to Our Crown Stations

The Texas Eastern Pipeline serves the mid-west. Our La Gloria (Tyler) refinery fuels pass through this pipeline. We sell these fuels on a wholesale basis. The Colonial Pipeline serves the east coast. Our Pasadena (Houston) refinery fuels pass through this pipeline.We sell these fuels on a wholesale basis and through our 340 retail Crown and Fast Fare stations. We pull the fuel from our 14 terminals located along these pipelines and transport it to our stations by tanker trucks.
