Triton has discovered three natural-gas fields, the Samudra, Senja and Bumi East, on Block A-18 in the Gulf of Thailand in 1997. Another well drilled this year, the Bumi North-1, confirmed a significant northern extension of the Bumi Field. Overall, Triton and its partner have discovered seven fields on Block A-18. More information about activities in the Gulf of Thailand is available in the Malaysia-Thailand fact sheet and news releases section.
