Who To Call

Lone Star Gas Company operates the natural-gas distribution segment of ENSERCH Corporation. One of the largest distribution companies in the United States, Lone Star Gas serves 1.3 million customers ranging from large end-users to small residential customers in 550 cities and towns in Texas, providing gas service through 23,500 miles of pipeline to an area with about one-third of the state's population.
