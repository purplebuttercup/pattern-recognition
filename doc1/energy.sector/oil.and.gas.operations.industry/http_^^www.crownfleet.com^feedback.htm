Fleet Comments

This section shares our customers ideas and thoughts on fleet management. How do our customers put the Vehicle Analysis Report to work? How much have they saved? Read what others have said about Crown's Commercial Fueling.

General Services Corp.

Fleet Manager: Frank Zauner

General Services Corp. is a managing agent for 4600 apartments in the metro Richmond area. As Regional Property Manager, Frank Zauner oversees 130 personnel, including the fleet drivers. General Services has been a fleet customer since February 1990 and purchases about 5000 gallons of fuel per month. The fleet includes maintenance, investigations, grounds maintenance, and staff personnel. He shares his comments about fleet management below:

"Proactive fleet maintenance is a must. We have the drivers check the fluids daily. We developed a checklist for tires, lights, belts, hoses, etc. We try to instill a philosophy for the driver to treat the vehicles as if he owns it. What would he be doing if it were his vehicle? That kind of approach.

We like Crown for several reasons. Crown has always been price competitive. You have a quality gasoline, and your responsive to our needs. We love the monthly Vehicle Analysis Report. It's easy to read, tracks well, helps us watch the vehicles for preventive maintenance. We have to have quality vendors, and Crown is a quality vendor. Best of all, we find people with a smile at Crown."
