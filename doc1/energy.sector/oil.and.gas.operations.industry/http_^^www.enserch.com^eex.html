Company Profile

Enserch Exploration (NYSE:EEX) is among the nation's largest independent gas and oil exploration and production companies. The Company drills exporatory and development wells, operates producing wells and markets natural gas and oil. Enserch Exploration concentrates its efforts in Texas, other areas adjacent to the Gulf of Mexico and the shallow and deep-water areas of the Gulf of Mexico. At January 1, 1996, the Company's net proved reserves were estimated to be 1.36 trillion cubic feet of natural gas and 71.5 million barrles of oil and condensate. Combined EEX's proved reserves are equivalent to 1.79 trillion cubic feet of natural gas.
