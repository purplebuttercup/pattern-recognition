Today coal has become very distant from its end customers. It is delivered through electric outlets -- and most people go a lifetime without knowing most of their electricity comes from clean, reliable, domestic coal supplies.
But coal does not exist in a vacuum. It is part of a chain of economic value that includes a variety of links: mining, transportation, clean air technologies, electricity generation, power distribution and marketing and finally the end user.
