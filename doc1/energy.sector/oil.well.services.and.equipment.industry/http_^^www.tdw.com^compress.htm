ABOUT TIDEWATER COMPRESSION

Tidewater Compression Service, Inc. has been a leader in providing single source gas compression service to the oil and gas industry since 1969. Today, Tidewater Compression owns and operates one of the largest natural gas compressor fleets in the world - more than 470,000 horsepower and almost 3,000 units.

Tidewater Compression’s service personnel are some of the most experienced in the industry. Our salespeople are degreed engineers or technically oriented veterans who make the right recommendations. Our highly skilled maintenance personnel are always prepared and are available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Maintenance personnel receive constant training both in the classroom and in field seminars.

Maintenance personnel are supported by a computerized network of parts distribution centers that gets parts and maintenance personnel to your site promptly and efficiently. Tidewater Compression is the largest distributor and owner of Ajax Integral natural gas compressors worldwide.

Tide Air & Gas

At Tide Air & Gas, we are experts in designing and building air and gas compressor packages. Tide Air & Gas began as a full service compression company in 1971, providing unrivaled quality plus worldwide sales and service.

Tide Air & Gas specializes in custom and standard packaged air and gas compression systems for the natural gas, industrial, offshore, power generation, energy and petrochemical markets.

Our full staff of experienced engineers assists in designing custom solutions to your most challenging compression applications. Our engineers are armed with extensive international experience plus state-of-the-art computer aided drafting that enables them to efficiently design the best possible compression systems for a broad range of industries.

Our manufacturing facilities have a combined total of more than 30,000 square feet. The shops are well equipped and have heavy lifting capabilities. Our manufacturing shop is qualified to furnish construction to both U.S. and international standards.

Another important aspect of Tide Air & Gas is our service department. Our fully qualified technicians and mechanics are on call 24 hours a day to provide full mechanical and electrical service on everything we sell. They are available to provide help with installation, startup and service after the sale. Tide Air & Gas keeps an extensive inventory of replacement parts. We can quickly provide many parts for emergency repairs, dispatching them as needed 24 hours a day worldwide.

The Tide Air & Gas staff takes pride in selling, designing, building and servicing some of the best compression systems in the world. We are committed to professionalism in all of our dealings all over the world and are dedicated to providing the best customer service in our industry.

AIR COMPRESSOR PACKAGES

Oil free or lubricated, reciprocating or rotary, electric drive or engine drive, we package any type of system. Our air packages are operating in thousands of location around the world.

We utilize only the best manufacturers of air compressors such as Atlas Copco Z series oil free rotary compressors.

Our engineering department is well versed in complicated multi-compressor PLC control design and sophisticated piping, structural, pressure vessel, instrumentation and electrical design. If your project has demanding specifications and requires attention to detail, Tide Air & Gas is the packager for you.
