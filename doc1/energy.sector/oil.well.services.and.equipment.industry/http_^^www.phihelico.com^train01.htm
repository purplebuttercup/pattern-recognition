Petroleum Helicopters Inc.

Training Department

Lafayette, Louisiana

The Petroleum Helicopters Inc., (PHI) Training Department is housed in a 10,500 square foot facility in Broussard, Louisiana, USA. The Training Department staff includes fourteen(14) flight instructors, three(3) maintenance instructors, one(1) industrial safety instructor and one(1) administrative assistant. The staff has over one hundred and thirty (130) years of combined experience as instructors in the PHI Training Department. This staff of professionals helps maintain qualifications on over six hundred(600) pilots and four hundred and fifty(450) mechanics on various combinations of as many as twenty(20) different aircraft. This includes training for PHI pilots and PHI aircraft mechanics at different locations throughout the United States of America and the world. Knowledge is the best tool used to promote safety and PHI has a commitment to training which underlines the drive to, "Provide Worldwide Helicopter Services That Are Unsurpassed In Safety And Customer Satisfaction".
