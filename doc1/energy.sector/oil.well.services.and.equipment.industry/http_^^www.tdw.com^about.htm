ABOUT

TIDEWATER


More than 40 years ago, with one offshore supply vessel in the U.S. Gulf of Mexico and a handful of employees, Tidewater was established as a publicly traded, investor owned company. Today, Tidewater owns hundreds of service vessels, as well as thousands of natural gas compressors, all of which are operated and managed by thousands of employees worldwide. Tidewater is traded on both the New York and Pacific stock exchanges under the symbol "TDW."

With headquarters in New Orleans, Louisiana, Tidewater Inc. has two primary business segments: marine services and compression services.

Through Tidewater Marine operations, the company owns and operates the world’s largest fleet serving the international offshore energy industry. Tidewater vessels can be found in virtually every area of the world where there is significant oil and gas exploration, development or production. These provide a wide range of services including: transporting crews and supplies between the mainland and offshore locations; towing and positioning mobile drilling rigs; assisting in offshore construction projects; and a variety of specialized services including cable laying and 3-D seismic work.

Through Tidewater Compression operations, the company owns and operates one of the largest full-service rental fleets of natural gas compressors in the world. These compressors are a vital part of the transportation of natural gas along pipelines. Compression operations provide services for every stage of a compression project, from custom engineering to 24-hour a day operation and maintenance.

At Tidewater, quality is an ethic that commands not only what we do, but how we do it. Our philosophy is to invest the time and effort necessary to fulfill each customer's objectives, while maintaining high standards of safety and providing cost effective services to the energy industry.
