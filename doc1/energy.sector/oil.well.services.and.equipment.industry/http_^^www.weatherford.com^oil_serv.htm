Weatherford Enterra is the world's largest provider of specialized rental equipment to operators and drilling, workover and completion contractors. Weatherford offers a complete rental line of pressure control equipment, tubulars and tubular handling equipment.

Critical well control equipment is maintained separately for immediate shipment for high pressure well completion and blowout control.

Weatherford Enterra fishing technicians stand ready when unexpected problems develop downhole. They are prepared to free stuck strings of pipe and eliminate obstructions or perform downhole milling and cutting operations.

Weatherford Enterra has crews with specialized tools for setting the casing and tubing in exploration and production wells. These crews are trained to use computerized equipment to monitor the correct makeup torque. Weatherford Enterra also offers the broadest range of tubular running equipment and power tongs in the industry.
