Weatherford Enterra Compression Company

No One Is Better under Pressure®

Weatherford Enterra Compression Company is a leader in providing quality cost effective natural gas compression products to a wide variety of markets and customers throughout the world.

Our approach capitalizes on the reputations and expertise of five legendary companies, each with over 25 years in the business:
  • Energy Industries;
  • Cherco Compressors Incorporated;
  • Cherco Barber Ltd.;
  • Gas Compressors Incorporated;
  • and Gemini Engine Company.
The synergy created by combining the capabilities of these five companies offers you the largest selection of products and services available in the industry today.

  • Total Contract Compression Total design, service and operations of your compression requirement, including horsepower management, compressor package design, site development, complete service and all operational reporting.

  • Gas Compressor Manufacturing We manufacture the GEMINITM brand of reciprocating, balanced-opposed natural gas compressor frames and cylinder combinations to meet any need up to 7,200 horsepower (5,400 kW) and 110,000 lbs. (50,000 kg) combined rod load.

  • Compressor Packaging We have a wide selection of compressor frame and cylinder models, drivers and package configurations, custom engineered to meet your requirements.

  • Sales and Lease/Purchase We sell and lease with lease/purchase options complete compressor packages. In addition, we sell original equipment compressor frames and cylinders to other packagers.

  • Rental We have available for you one of the largest, most comprehensive rental fleets in the industry.

  • Compressor Package Service and Support We provide a variety of maintenance and repair services for the entire package, regardless of the make or model.

  • Original Equipment Parts We offer a complete line of factory authorized replacement compressor parts for Energy Industries, Chicago Pneumatic, Cooper Superior and Gemini units, as well as OEM parts for most other major manufacturers.

  • Used Compression Equipment We buy and sell used compression packages and components and provide from a large selection of "as is, where is" units or reconditionable units.

  • Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) Vehicle Refueling Systems We offer a cost effective line of high pressure gas compression systems for natural gas vehicle refueling applications.

  • Business Alliances We offer our gas compression products and services as a single source, turnkey supplier.

  • Financing Options You have the widest selection of financing options in the business from short to long term choices with flexible interest rates and payments to fit your needs.

  • Offshore Capabilities We provide turnkey level support that includes package fabrication, start-up, and total package service to the offshore industry.

  • International Capabilities We deliver and support our equipment worldwide.

  • Customer Training Our training includes maintenance, safety, operations and environmental compliance.
