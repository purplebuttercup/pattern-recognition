Petroleum Helicopters Inc.

Technical Services Division

Established in 1949, PHI leads the helicopter industry as an operator of helicopters and as a maintenance provider. PHI sets the standards for helicopter maintenance procedures and is respected around the world as being the most experienced company in rotary wing maintenance. Aircraft manufacturers rely on PHI to develop design improvements and have utilized PHI as a product testing and proving ground for many years.

Our experience operating and maintaining a large and varied fleet of helicopters led to the creation of an independent business unit that is dedicated to providing operators with the highest level of helicopter maintenance expertise available.

Our wide range of capabilities enable us to offer helicopter operators with comprehensive maintenance support programs. Services include airframe inspection, repair, and refurbishment, repair and overhaul of turbine engines, gearboxes and drive system components, fuel systems and accessories. These services are available for a variety of Bell, Eurocopter, and Sikorsky aircraft.

The Technical Services unit is becoming an increasingly important part of PHI's revenue base - handling everything from minor repairs to the complete rebuilding of aircraft.



PHI has a full time Engineering Department. This department assures that aircraft modifications have proper documentation, approval, and safety built in right from the start. Our mechanics and technicians work hand in hand with Engineering to turn good ideas into FAA approved data. Whether it's a full EMS interior or a customer requested modification, PHI does it and does it right.
