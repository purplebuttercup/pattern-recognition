Petroleum Helicopters Inc.

International Operations

The International Operations Department has extensive experience in operating aircraft outside the U.S. having worked in over 39 different countries around the world. PHI’s crews and helicopters have flown every conceivable type of mission in some of the most isolated locations of the globe doing everything from slinging bananas in Honduras to making a movie in Israel.


We currently have helicopters and/or personnel in Angola, Zaire, Peru, Colombia, Venezuela, Ecuador, Philippines, Vietnam and Kazakstan mostly in support of offshore oil exploration and production as well as jungle exploration. We also have a Hawker HS-125 currently based in Tashkent, Uzbekistan offering executive jet transport in that area of the world.

Each international contract is tailored to suit the customer’s particular needs and requirements.

For more information on helicopter and/or fixed wing transport availability on a worldwide basis, please contact the International Operations Department at any of the numbers listed below.
