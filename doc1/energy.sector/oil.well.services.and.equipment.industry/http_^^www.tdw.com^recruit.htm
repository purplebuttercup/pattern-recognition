Turn The Tide On Your Future



The future is full of possibilities. And Tidewater Inc. is on course to achieve unprecedented success. We are the world leader for support vessels for the offshore oil and gas industry. Our corporation consists of three strong and growing operating segments: Tidewater Marine, Tidewater Compression and Quality Shipyards, Inc. Explore the following job opportunities and comprehensive benefits package and come aboard to "turn the tide on your future".

Tidewater Marine owns and operates approximately 750 vessels, the worlds largest fleet dedicated to serving the offshore energy industry. Tidewater vessels can be found in virtually every area of the world where there is significant oil and gas exploration, production or development.

Tidewater Compression owns and operates one of the largest full-service gas compressor rental fleets in the world - about 2,800 units totaling more than 470,000 horsepower. The compressors are a vital part of the transportation of natural gas along pipelines.

Quality Shipyard, Inc. uses state-of-the-art CAD equipment to design, build and repair vessels up to 15,000 horsepower. The marine and oil industries depend on the resources of Tidewater's full-service machine shop to meet their special needs and requirements.

With our focus turned toward added expansion and continued domination in the markets we serve, Tidewater Inc. is riding an incredible wave of momentum. Forty (40) years in the business and we are still on the rise....bringing our corporation to new levels of accomplishment.
