ARCO... A Global Energy Company.

ARCO (Atlantic Richfield Company) and its affiliates operate globally in all aspects of the energy business: the exploration, production and marketing of crude oil, natural gas and natural gas liquids, and the refining, marketing and transportation of petroleum products.

ARCO has interests in chemicals and coal and has created a variety of businesses ancillary to its operations, including retail convenience stores, cogeneration of steam and electricity, and electronic payment systems. ARCO's earliest predecessor, The Atlantic Petroleum Storage Company, was founded on July 1, 1866, in Pennsylvania.

ARCO has maintained a tradition of excellence and has established itself as a leader in the industry. ARCO makes environmental sensitivity and community concern an integral part of its business everywhere it operates.
