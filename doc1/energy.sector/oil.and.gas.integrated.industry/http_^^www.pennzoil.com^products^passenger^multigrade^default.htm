Pennzoil Multigrade Motor Oil is available in four viscosity grades: SAE 5W-30, SAE 10W-30, SAE 10W-40 and SAE 15W-40. Every grade of Pennzoil Multigrade Motor Oil offers outstanding performance for gasoline and turbocharged engines. Each exceeds warranty requirements of every car maker in the world requiring API's highest standard for engine protection, “SJ.” SAE 5W-30 and 10W-30 meet ILSAC GF-2 requirements and display the starburst certification mark. All multigrades are formulated with Pennzoil's revolutionary Pennzstar™ molecule that provides outstanding engine protection. (Always check the owner's manual for recommended viscosity)

Uses: Pennzoil Multigrade Motor Oil offers high-temperature protection and fuel economy for today's high-revving 4-, 6-, and 8-cylinder engines (even turbocharged), as well as older, high-horsepower engines. It is specially formulated to protect engines during the stress of cold starts and short-trip driving.
