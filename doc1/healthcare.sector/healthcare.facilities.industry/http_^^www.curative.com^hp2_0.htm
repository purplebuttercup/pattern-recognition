ABOUT THE COMPANY
Curative Health Services, formerly Curative Technologies, Inc., is the leading disease management company in the chronic wound market today. Curative's nationwide network of over 100 wound care programs provides a comprehensive continuum of care through multi-disciplinary treatment programs. Wound Care Centers® are managed by Curative Health Services on behalf of acute care hospitals. These innovative "Centers of Excellence" incorporate a range of advanced technologies into their treatment programs including outpatient and inpatient services, such as vascular surgery, laboratory, radiology, growth factor therapy, skin grafting, home health care, pharmacy, nutrition, infection control, protection devices, rehabilitation, hyperbaric oxygen, and patient education.

CURATIVE: THE LEADER IN WOUND CARE MANAGEMENT
Historically, traditional therapeutic approaches - cleansing and protecting wounds, controlling infection with antibiotics - have met with limited success. As a result, it has been difficult for patients to receive comprehensive treatment for wounds unless separate medical specialists are consulted. Using a case management approach, Curative provides each patient with the appropriate level of care to minimize waste, save money and assure each patient does not receive unnecessary or duplicate treatment.

Curative's Wound Care Center® programs have been successful in promoting the healing of wounds that previously resisted healing and saving many limbs from amputation. Curative's Wound Care Centers® develop comprehensive therapeutic protocols tailored to the individualized needs of each patient and utilize the most advanced and cost-effective wound care technologies. This may include the use of Procuren®, a novel, patient-specific therapy developed by Curative. Procuren® is made from naturally occurring growth factors derived from the patients own blood. Growth factors have been shown to promote the growth of skin, soft tissue and blood vessels.
