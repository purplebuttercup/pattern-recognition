The Curative Courseware is a multiple modules training program designed for health care professionals who work in Curative Wound Management Programs. To enter the Courseware, click on the Login Button below. A window will appear that asks for your Username and Password. Please enter your Courseware User ID and Courseware Password where indicated.



If you have not registered to utilize this system please contact the program director for your Wound Management Program to receive your User ID and Password.

If you are a registered user, and are having difficulty accessing the Courseware, contact Christy Pines cpines@psinet.com. If you are a Curative Health Professional and you have forgotten or misplaced your user information contact, your program director or Christy Pines via email at cpines@psinet.com or call her at (972) 680-1122.
