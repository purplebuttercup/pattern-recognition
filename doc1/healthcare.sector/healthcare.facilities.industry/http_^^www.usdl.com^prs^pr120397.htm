US DIAGNOSTIC INC. NAMES L.E. RICHEY M.D. AND C. KEITH HARTLEY
CO-CHAIRMEN OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS

West Palm Beach, FL, December 3, 1997 – US Diagnostic Inc. (NASDAQ:NMS: USDL) today announced that the Company’s Board of Directors has elected L.E. Richey M.D. and C. Keith Hartley as Co-Chairmen of the Board. Dr. Richey and Mr. Hartley are both currently Directors of US Diagnostic Inc.

Dr. Richey and Mr. Hartley succeed Laurans A. Mendelson who announced his resignation as Chairman and a Director of US Diagnostic Inc. due to extensive time constraints required in connection with his responsibilities as Chairman, President, and Chief Executive Officer at HEICO Corporation (AMEX: HEI). Mr. Mendelson stated, "When I initially accepted US Diagnostic’s Board’s request to become Chairman, it was with the understanding that this would be on an interim and temporary basis. I now feel that the necessary changes and remedial actions have been accomplished, including the settlement of the shareholder lawsuits, settlement of the Nasdaq listing review, the establishment of a strong corporate governance, as well as the addition of senior management that will enable the Company to begin its rebuilding process and move forward on a successful path. I am very confident of both Dr. Richey’s and Mr. Hartley’s abilities to lead and guide US Diagnostic into a new year."

According to Dr. Richey, "I am honored to be named as a new Co-Chairman of the Board of US Diagnostic Inc. We have gone through a very difficult and trying period this year and are now well on the road to recovery with outstanding personnel and infrastructure necessary for us to regain our momentum. I am very excited at the prospect of working with our senior management team and we will endeavor to return to an acquisition program and strong growth pattern. I look forward to working closely with Keith Hartley, as Co-Chairman, and the other Board members to bring US Diagnostic back to profitability."

Mr. Hartley added, "As a Director of US Diagnostic Inc. for over a year, I have been committed to working closely with the Board and senior management to help the Company through the difficult issues we have faced. As we approach 1998, I am excited by the prospects facing us and will continue to work even harder as Co-Chairman to bring about renewed strength and growth at US Diagnostic."
