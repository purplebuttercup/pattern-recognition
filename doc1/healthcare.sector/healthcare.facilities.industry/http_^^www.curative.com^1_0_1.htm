WOUND CARE CENTER® and WOUND MANAGEMENT PROGRAMS


Wound Care Centers® and Wound Management Programs are unique treatment programs managed by Curative Health Services. The centers and programs apply a comprehensive approach to the diagnosis and treatment of chronic non-healing wounds. Therapeutic treatment plans are tailored to the individual needs of each patient.

Wound Care Centers® and Wound Management Programs have been successful in promoting the healing of wounds that previously resisted healing and in reducing amputations.

If you have more questions, talk to your doctor or call the Wound Care Center® closest to you.
