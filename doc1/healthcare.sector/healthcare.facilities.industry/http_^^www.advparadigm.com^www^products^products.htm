Health Benefit Management Advance Paradigm believes that successfully managing healthcare means focusing on the entire continuum of care through a total quality management process designed to consistently improve medical outcomes. We refer to this concept as Health Benefit Management.

Pharmacy Management Advance Paradigm offers fully integrated, wholly owned services, including clinical services, mail services, sophisticated claims adjudication with excellent network access, and a extensive nationwide retail pharmacy network. This allows us to deliver integrated solutions to your problems without bias or having to rely on any outside contractors.

Medical Management Advance Paradigm understands the significant pressure our clients experience in trying to control rising healthcare costs. We believe the answer lies in implementing focused disease management programs, which can deliver significant overall bottom-line savings. Advance Paradigm's care management component strategically integrates multiple health services into a focused delivery system of beneficial and cost-effective patient interventions. Information management is the key ingredient in performing effective outcomes management.

Support Systems Advance Paradigm offers innovative clinical programs to maximize your total healthcare dollar and to improve patient care and quality of life.  We recognize that your organization is unique, so only after thorough analysis will we offer customized solutions to address your individual needs. Advance Paradigm understands the value of converting data into meaningful information and therefore introduced our proprietary decisions support system ApotheQuery. Our extensive database repository is fully integrated and patient identification is held in the strictest of confidence.

We listen to your concerns and then implement timely solutions to your problems.
