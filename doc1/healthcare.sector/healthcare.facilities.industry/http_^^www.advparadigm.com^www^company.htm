Advance Paradigm, Inc. Announces Pharmacy Benefit Management Services Agreement with Costco
Announcement Calls For Integrated Clinical Strategies to Reduce Drug Costs and Improve Outcomes

IRVING, Texas, Nov. 3 -- Advance Paradigm, Inc., (Nasdaq:ADVP), today announced that it has been selected to provide pharmacy benefit management services for Costco Companies, Inc. based in Issaquah, Washington. Under the agreement, Advance Paradigm will provide Costco with fully integrated pharmacy services including: claims adjudication, formulary management, clinical services and network management. In addition, Costco will have access to Advance Paradigm's health benefit management products including disease management and personal care management. This agreement became effective November 1, 1997, and will cover approximately 100,000 members based in 23 states.

``Our integrated clinical strategies will provide Costco with the ability to control drug cost, the flexibility to offer innovative new products and the opportunity to deliver the highest levels of service and care to their members,'' said David D. Halbert, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Advance Paradigm. ``Our clinical leadership, innovative information management capabilities and aggressive commitment to customer service, positions us well to meet Costco's need to provide coordinated medical care management. A good example is Advance Paradigm's decision support system, ApotheQuery(R), which is used to accurately profile physician prescribing patterns, improve compliance, and thereby positively impact the overall quality of care.''

In addition to physician and member profiling, Advance Paradigm will provide Costco Companies, Inc. with access to other AdvanceSolutions (SM) clinical intervention strategies including: AdvanceChoice (SM), an innovative Formulary POS (point of service) MAC program and AdvanceSecure (SM), a clinically focused prior authorization program.

Advance Paradigm, Inc., is a leading independent provider of health benefit management (HBM) services. The Company provides cost-effective, high quality, integrated PBM services including innovative clinical programs, mail service and sophisticated claims adjudication with PPO and EPO network management. The Company's expanding HBM programs include customized disease management programs targeted at specific disease states and personal care management (PCM) capabilities. Advance Paradigm's client base of more than 12 million lives includes HMOs, PPOs, BlueCross & BlueShield organizations, Fortune 500 employers, insurance carriers and third party administrators.

Advance Paradigm, Inc. Announces Record Revenues and Earnings For Fiscal 2nd Quarter 1998
Net Income Increases 245 % As Revenues Top $100 Million For The First Time
IRVING, Texas, Nov. 3 -- Advance Paradigm, Inc., (Nasdaq:ADVP), a leading independent provider of health benefit management (HBM) services today reported operating results for its fiscal 1998 second quarter and six months ended September 30, 1997. For the second quarter, the Company generated revenues of $114.4 million and net income of $1.7 million versus last year's $58.0 million and net income of $507,000 for increases of 97 percent and 245 percent respectively. Earnings per share were $0.17 on a 43 percent increase in shares outstanding compared to $0.07 per share for the second quarter of 1997. For the six months ended September 30, 1997, the Company reported revenues of $192.1 million an increase of 78 percent over the same period last year and net income of $3.1 million versus $1.2 million, an increase of 158 percent over the same period a year ago.

David D. Halbert, Chairman and CEO, stated, ``We are proud to report another record breaking quarter for revenues, net income and claims processed. For the quarter ended September 30, 1997, the Company processed over 9.1 million pharmacy claims, an increase of 63 percent over the prior year's quarter. Beginning July 1, 1997 the Company began providing services for the United Mine Workers of America Health and Retirement Funds. The addition of this important relationship was a key contributor to the growth we experienced this quarter.''

The Company earlier today announced they have been selected to provide health benefit management (HBM) services for Nabisco and Costco Companies, Inc., including pharmacy benefit and disease management programs. Other recent new client announcements include Pharmaceutical Care Network and Wellmark, Inc. These clients represent over 2 million new lives, and bring the Company's total managed care lives to over 12 million.

``Nabisco and Costco Companies, Inc. are a direct result of the employer group initiative we implemented earlier this year,'' Halbert continued. ``Our strategy to provide services which go far beyond traditional pharmacy benefit management services has contributed to the Company's success in this and other market segments. This strategy provides integrated disease, information, personal care, and pharmacy benefit management services which focus on delivering cost effective programs which optimize outcomes and service for our clients' members.''

Advance Paradigm, Inc., is a leading independent provider of health benefit management (HBM) services. The Company provides cost-effective, high quality integrated pharmacy benefit management(PBM) services including innovative clinical programs, mail service and sophisticated claims adjudication with PPO and EPO network management. The Company continues to expand its HBM capabilities including customized disease management programs targeted at specific disease states and personal care management (PCM) capabilities. Advance Paradigm, Inc.'s client base of more than 12 million lives includes HMOs, PPOs, BlueCross and BlueShield organizations, Fortune 500 employers, insurance carriers and third party administrators.

Advance Paradigm, Inc. and the UMWA Health and Retirement
Funds Announce Pharmacy Services Agreement
Irving, Texas, May 21 -- Advance Paradigm, Inc., (Nasdaq:ADVP), today announced a letter of intent providing for a multi-year agreement to provide pharmacy benefit management services for the United Mine Workers of America Health and Retirement Funds ("Funds"). Under the proposed agreement, Advance Paradigm will provide pharmacy benefit management services, several disease management programs and assistance with the Funds' medical management initiatives.

"The opportunity to work with the Funds is very exciting for us," said David D. Halbert, Chief Executive Officer of Advance Paradigm. "The Funds present a unique set of health care management challenges which our health benefit management initiatives have been designed to address. Through a collaborative effort with the Funds' staff, we will work to positively impact not only the Funds' beneficiaries' quality of care, but also help control the Funds' overall health care costs."

"The demographics for the Funds' beneficiaries are quite different from what we typically see," Halbert continued. "With an average age of nearly 78, this population faces the many challenges of advancing age and its associated illnesses. Advance Paradigm's initial disease management focus will be on Diabetes, Hypertension and Congestive Heart Failure which all have significant impact on this geriatric population."

The UMWA Health and Retirement Funds is a group of six multi-employer benefit plans that provide benefits to eligible coal industry retirees and their families. The three health benefit plans consist of the Combined Benefit Fund, the 1992 Benefit Plan and the 1993 Benefit Plan and covers approximately 90,000 beneficiaries located primarily in rural areas. These plans provide over $100 million per year in pharmacy benefits.
