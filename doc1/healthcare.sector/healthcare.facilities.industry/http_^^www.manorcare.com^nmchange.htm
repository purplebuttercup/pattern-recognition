Assisted Living
-------------That's What's New
ManorCare Health Services has rapidly grown into one of the country's most dynamic, broadbased health care providers offering a wide range of health and personal care services. In recent years these services have expanded to include two exciting assisted living products -- Arden Courts Alzheimer's Assisted Living and Springhouse. Assisted living is a relatively new approach to residential care for older people that enhances life through wellness programs, activities, socialization and personal care services. In many instances, it is the ideal setting for those who can no longer remain at home, but do not require skilled nursing care.

New Assisted Living Facilities

The real new is, we're still growing. We plan to open over 100 facilities throughout the country during the next five years.

Arden Courts, which has been available in the Washington, DC, Philadelphia, West Palm Beach, Chicago, and Detroit metropolitan areas, has recently become available in New Jersey, Ohio, and Farmington Connecticut (our first center ever in New England). Springhouse, already available in Florida, Ohio, Illinois, Michigan and Los Angeles, is now available in Indiana and Tucson, Arizona.
