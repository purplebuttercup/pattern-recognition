Paving Your Career Path

Sun Healthcare Group, Inc. offers rewarding and challenging career opportunities, whether your expertise is in administration or in hands-on healthcare delivery. Accountants, financial managers and marketing and sales experts work in a fast-paced environment at our company headquarters in Albuquerque, N.M. In our nursing home and rehabilitation facilities located throughout the United States, Canada and the United Kingdom, therapists, nursing home administrators and nurses are part of a team of professionals dedicated to providing quality healthcare and superior service to our customers.

At Sun Healthcare Group and its subsidiaries, we maintain a work atmosphere that fosters professional growth and stability. Our employee benefits program is one of the best in the industry, and we offer a stock purchase plan as well as a 401(k) program.

Sun Healthcare Group, Inc., is a diversified long-term care provider. Sun companies operate long-term care facilities across the United States and Canada, and manage regional pharmacies that serve the nursing home industry. Our network of rehabilitation facilities offer physical, occupational, speech and respiratory therapy, both on an inpatient and outpatient basis.

Sun Healthcare Group, Inc. is an Equal Opportunity Employer.
