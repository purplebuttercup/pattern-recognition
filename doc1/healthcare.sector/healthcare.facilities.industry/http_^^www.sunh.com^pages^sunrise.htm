  Dedicated to Service

Choosing a nursing facility for a family member is an important decision. Sunrise Healthcare Corporation has earned a reputation for providing the highest quality care in the industry, serving residents in more than 180 facilities across the country. We believe that our residents' happiness is just as important as their healthcare needs. Visit the Sunrise directory for a list of services and a brief description of each facility. You can search by state or zip code, or by the special services your family member may need.

Many Sunrise facilities have specialized units for people with Alzheimer's disease and other types of dementia. These programs meet the psychosocial needs of Alzheimer's patients, as well as their physical needs. The environment is designed to reduce confusion and foster safety and security.

For older adults who do not require 24-hour nursing care, Sun also provides assisted living residences in the United States. Visit our SunBridge Assisted Living Residences site if you would like more information about these residences. SunBridge residences combine hospitality and healthcare with private luxury apartment living. These are designed for older people who have healthcare concerns and require assistance with some daily tasks, but do not need full-time nursing care.

You will find the services you need at any of our skilled nursing facilities. If you have any questions about how to use this directory, please feel free to call the corporate communications department at (505) 858-4572, or E-mail us at sun@sunh.com.

Sunrise is a subsidiary of Sun Healthcare Group, Inc., a diversified healthcare company that offers rehabilitation therapy, respiratory care services and other healthcare services important to long- term care patients. Sun companies also provide pharmaceutical services and medical supplies to long-term care patients in the United States and the United Kingdom.
