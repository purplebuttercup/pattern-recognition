Sun Healthcare Group, Inc.
Corporate Headquarters
101 Sun Lane NE
Albuquerque, New Mexico 87109

Sun Healthcare Group, Inc. is a provider of long-term and subacute care in the United States and the United Kingdom. The company was founded in 1989 and began trading on the New York Stock Exchange (trading symbol: SHG) in 1993.

In the United States, Sun has a presence in 48 states through its long-term care and its contracted rehabilitation therapy and institutional pharmacy services divisions. The long-term care facilities are the core of the company's operations. Sun is customer-service oriented and has a strong understanding of the particular needs and concerns of our long-term residents.

Sun Healthcare has enjoyed rapid growth by adding ancillary services that enable the company to meet residents' and patients' needs and compete successfully in the changing healthcare market. Headquartered in Albuquerque, New Mexico, the company employs more than 48,000 people. In 1996, it generated gross revenues of approximately $1.3 billion.

Through its subsidiaries, Sun offers a well-balanced menu of services. The Sun companies share a commitment to high-quality patient care and smart, efficient operations.
