Busisness Development

Patent and Technology Specialist

Currently, we have a great opportunity in our Alameda office for a qualified Patent & Technology Specialist. In this key role, you will work closely with internal staff and outside counsel to maintain our current patent portfolio; draft new patent applications & responses; and organize data from internal scientific work. You will also support our business development efforts by investigating patents & scientific data to assess the value of various technologies. The position requires a Ph.D. in the biological sciences along with prior experience in the gene therapy field. Candidates must possess superior organizational, scientific/technical writing, and communication skills. Prior experience or familiarity with patents is highly desirable. It is expected that the chosen candidate either has or will acquire the qualifications needed to become a patent agent. (Job# BIO0922-PTS).

Production and Development

Development Scientist

Process Development Scientist to join our expanding R&D team. Your principal duties and responsibilities will include developing new methods for the purification of recombinant AAV. This will require experience with standard purification tools available to protein chemists and virologists, such as column chromatography, density gradient centrifugation, ultrafiltration/diafiltration, etc. Further requirements are experience with equipment such as HPLC, FPLC and BioCad/AKTA. Experience developing and performing assays such as protein measurement, SDS-PAGE, Westerns, HPLC, Southern blotting, dot blot assays, virus titration assays, virus transduction assays, flow cytometry etc. 

You must possess a Ph.D. in Biology/Chemistry with 3-5 years of relevant experience. You must be able to think on your feet and recommend actions based on your observations. Furthermore, you must be capable of making decisions based on knowledge of GMP to develope a scaleable and manufacturable process. (Job# SF0608-RS)

Research Associate

In this position, you will work with our Development Group. You will be responsible for large scale purification of plasmids and their subsequent characterization. The position requires a B.S. and 2-3 years experience with molecular biology techniques. Relevant skills include cloning, aseptic technique, banking cell stocks, cell culture growth, DNA sequencing, DNA transfection, viral DNA extraction, PCR, Southern & Dot Blot assays & restriction mapping . Computer applications are also desirable. Candidates must be able to work independently and possess superior organizational skills.  (Job# SF0608-RA)
