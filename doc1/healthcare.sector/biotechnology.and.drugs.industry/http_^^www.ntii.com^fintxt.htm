As of September 30, 1997 Neurobiological Technologies, Inc. (Nasdaq: NTII) had cash and cash equivalents of $2.3 million and no long-term debt. The company's cash and investments are expected to fund operations through the quarter ending December 31, 1997. The company will need to raise substantial capital to fund subsequent operations.

The company reported a net loss of $1,276,000, or $0.20 per share, for its quarter ended September 30, 1997. This compares to a net loss of $1,734,000, or $0.27 per share, for the same quarter a year ago. For the fiscal year ended June 30, 1997, the net loss was $7,369,000, or $1.13 per share, compared to a net loss of $5,211,000, or $1.05 per share, for the year ended June 30, 1996.
