16 October 97 - USANA, Inc. Reports Record Results in Third-quarter Sales and Earnings



USANA is a health company founded by Dr. Myron Wentz in 1990 and has become one of the fastest growing network marketing programs in the world. Strong management and scientific operations teams have positioned USANA for strong growth featuring excellent product lines. The company moved into a 100,000-square-foot facility in the fall of 1996.
