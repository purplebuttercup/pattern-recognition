Employment Opportunities

CEL-SCI Corporation has employment opportunities to work with cutting edge technology in a modern facility. CEL-SCI offers a competitive salary and benefits package with includes:

  • Medical Insurance
  • Dental/Vision Insurance
  • Long-term Disability Insurance
  • Flexible Spending Account
  • 401(k) Plan Education Assistance Program
  • Employee Referral Program

CEL-SCI is an equal opportunity employer.

Positions Available

Laboratory Manager (Immunobiology)-Baltimore, Maryland

CEL-SCI’s R&D and Quality Control laboratory has an opening for a hands on laboratory manager. The candidate must have a Ph. D. in immunobiology or immunochemistry and at least 4-5 years of postdoctoral research experience.

Knowledge of and experience in immunoassay and bioassay development and validation is required. Experience in biologics R&D and Quality Control in the pharmaceutical/biotechnology industry necessary. The candidate must also have experience in a GMP and GLP work environment and have experience in management of R&D and technical teams.

Assistant/Associate Immunologist-Baltimore, Maryland

CEL-SCI’s R&D and Quality Control laboratory has an opening for an Assistant/Associate Immunologist (level commensurate with experience). The candidate must have a relevant degree (BS or MS) and experience in immunology, immunochemistry, cell culture, or molecular biology. Knowledge and experience with cGMP, aseptic technique and the manufacturing or Quality control of a biologic is desired.
