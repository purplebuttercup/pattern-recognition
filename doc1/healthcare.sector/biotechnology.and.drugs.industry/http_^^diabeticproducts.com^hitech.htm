Hi- Tech Pharmacal Co., Inc., based in Ammityville, Long Island, a publicly traded company (NASDAQ: HITK), is a health care company with state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities. Products include oral liquids, creams, ointments, suppositories, sterile ophthalmics and inhalation products.

The Health Care Products Division of Hi-Tech Pharmacal markets branded products for diabetic patients. These products include Diabetic Tussin®, DiabetiDerm Deep Moisturizing Cream and Lotion® and DiabetiSweet Sugar Substitute®.
