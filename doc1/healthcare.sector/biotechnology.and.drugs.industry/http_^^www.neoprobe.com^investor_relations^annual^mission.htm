mission


Neoprobe Corporation is dedicated to improving the standard of care, enhancing quality of life, and providing tomorrows for cancer patients worldwide. The company's unique, proprietary RIGS® system products are designed to provide cancer surgeons with immediate, indispensable information during surgery to help them make better treatment decisions for their patients. Neoprobe is also committed to expanding the capabilities of our technology to other medical applications beyond surgical detection and treatment ofcancer.
