IPO Summary Information:

EPIX completed its initial public offering of 2.3 million shares of common stock at a price of $7.00 per share, which included the exercise of the underwriters over-allotment option of 300,00 shares, in February 1997. Hambrecht & Quist, LLC and Wessels, Arnold & Henderson, LLC co-managed the offering. Following the offering 8,614,740 common shares were issued and outstanding.
