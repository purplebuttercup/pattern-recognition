 ATRION DECLARES ROUTINE QUARTERLY DIVIDEND

ARAB, Alabama (November 12, 1997)-ATRION Corporation (Nasdaq/NM - ATRI) today announced that its Board of Directors has declared a quarterly cash dividend of $.10 per share. The dividend is payable on December 1, 1997, to stockholders of record as of November 24, 1997.

 

ATRION Corporation designs, develops, manufactures, sells and distributes medical products and components to markets worldwide. significantly higher income from continuing operations for the third quarter and first nine months of 1997.
