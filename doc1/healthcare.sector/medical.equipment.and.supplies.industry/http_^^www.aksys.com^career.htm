Developing innovative products for renal replacement therapy that cost effectively improve patient outcomes and quality of life. We are actively seeking to fill the following positions to help us bring our lead product, the Aksys PHDTM Personal Hemodialysis System to market.

 

Process Engineer - Plastics
Responsible for product/process development of disposable plastic medical devices and molded components. Work with suppliers to optimize design and manufacturing specifications. Conduct reliability tests. Develop high volume blood tubing assembly and test techniques. Knowledgeable of injection and blow molding processes. BS Mechanical or Plastics Eng. with 1-3 years of manufacturing experience.

Quality Engineers
QE Hardware and QE Disposables responsible for product validation management and compliance including validation test plan development, execution, and documentation, FMEA and product hazard analysis. Ideal candidates will have CQE and statistics background, with working knowledge of device GMPs, ISO, AAMI standards. BS Eng./Science 3 years experience with medical device product development.

Principal Engineers - Software
Embedded Software - Responsible for development of data acquisition and control systems utilizing real time multitasking operating systems.
GUI Development - Responsible for implementing user interfaces on Aksys medical devices utilizing object oriented techniques.
SW Test - Work with development engineers in developing, performing, and reporting unit test also performing code reviews.
These positions require candidates to be highly skilled in OOAD, and C++. Medical device experience is desirable.BS Eng. / Science with greater than 4 yrs. Experience in SW development.

Principal Engineer - Systems
Responsible for the development and integration of sensor subsystems into the design of Aksys medical devices. Skilled in the design and calibration of temperature, pressure, conductivity , optical and ultrasonic sensors. BS Electrical Eng. with greater than 4 yrs. Experience.

Electrical Engineer
Responsible for the design, from concept through production, of medical instrumentation electronics. Responsibilities include analog and digital circuit design and analysis, circuit simulation and test, sensor system analysis, and creation of production documentation, while working with state-of-the-art tools. B.S.E.E. required, M.S.E.E. a plus, 5+ years relevant experience.
