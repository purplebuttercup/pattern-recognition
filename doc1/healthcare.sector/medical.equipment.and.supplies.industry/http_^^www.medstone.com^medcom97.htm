Company Overview

Medstone International, Inc. was founded in 1984 as a shockwave therapy company. Medstone's equipment employs high pressure, precisely focused shockwaves to treat kidney stones without invasive surgery. Medstone is an international company with over 90 systems in operation around the globe. Medstone has over 12 years of experience in shockwave research, manufacturing and field service. Medstone is investigating the expansion of shockwave therapy to other diseases such as gallstones, non-healing bones and pancreatic duct stones.

Medstone's business strategy is to be the world's only vertically integrated shockwave technology company. Utilizing our company-owned fleet of mobile lithotripsy service direct to hospitals, clinics, physician groups and HMO's throughout the U.S. Over 25,000 patients were successfully treated on Medstone equipment in 1996, the tenth successive record year for our company. Our plans to continue this record growth include the development of gallstone and orthopedic applications for our shockwave therapy equipment, and the expansion of our company-owned fixed/land mobile fleet.

Our patients enjoy the highest rate of effectiveness, safety delivered. Our physician-users enjoy the only completely software-driven system available, with an ease of use unmatched by our competition. The owners of Medstone equipment enjoy our commitment to keep our 12-year installed base up-to-date with modular upgrades, eliminating obsolescence concerns. Our system owners also enjoy the excellent financial returns that Medstone delivers.
