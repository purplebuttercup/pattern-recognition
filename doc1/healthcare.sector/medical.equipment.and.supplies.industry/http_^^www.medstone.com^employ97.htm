Employment Opportunities

Medstone is a leader in manufacturing and support of shockwave therapy equipment. We manufacture equipment that employs high pressure, precisely focused shockwaves to treat kidney stones without invasive surgery. We are an international company with over 90 systems in operation around the globe. Our business strategy is to be the world's only vertically integrated shockwave technology company.

Medstone has immediate openings in our Administrative and Engineering Departments.

Medstone provides a competitive salary and generous benefits

Our Full-time Employee Benefits include:
  • A Professional Management Staff and excellent teamwork atmosphere.
  • Professional Office environment with state-of-the-art equipment
  • Work with and implement Leading Edge Equipment and Software
  • Medstone is a Non-Smoking office
  • 401k with matching, and a Company Bonus plan
  • Insurance : Medical, Dental and life
  • Reimbursed Travel & Mileage expenses
  • Continuing Education Reimbursement
  • Paid Holidays, Christmas shut-down
Part-Time Receptionist Friendly, outgoing team-player to operate the switchboard, signing in visitors, and other miscellaneous office duties, including filing, copying, typing labels, and faxing.
Engineering Assistant PC literate assistant needed to support the Engineering Department by creating source control drawings, bills of material, and other documentation required for completion of product development cycle.
