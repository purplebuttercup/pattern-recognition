Diabetes Links On The Internet
If you have a greater appetite for diabetes on the internet, here's a listing of some of the more helpful sites. These links will take you into the diabetes mainstream, and many have extensive, often updated link pages, so go check 'em out!

Associations:

    American Association of Clinical Endocrinologists (AACE) - Mostly info for its professional membership, with much on diabetes.

    American Diabetes Association (ADA) - This is ADA central, a must visit and explore.

    Juvenial Diabetes Foundation (JDF) - Good info on the disease and funding activity for a cure.

Governmental:

    CDC Diabetes Home Page - From a division of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention; translates scientific research into health promotion, disease prevention and treatment strategies.

    National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Disease (NIDDK) - Department of the National Institutes of Health (NIH). Good info on diabetes. Go here if you're just learning about the disease.

Others:

    Diabetes Monitor - A site set up by the Midwest Diabetes Care Center, rich in diabetes info. Especially good links to more diabetes resources, direct link: Diabetes on the Web

    children with DIABETES - A well done site, with lots of diabetes info pertaining to children. Contains a very good listing of Web resources.

    On-Line Diabetes Resources - Rick Mendoza's excellent compilation of diabetes resources. Go here if you're especially looking for news groups and chat groups.

    Health:Diseases and Conditions:Diabetes - Yahoo's diabetes listing, well organized.

News and Chat Groups:

    misc.health.diabetes - This is the place to find people to talk with about diabetes. (misc.health.diabetes)

    alt.support.diabetes.kids - A support group for parents with children with diabetes.

    Diabetic Mailing List - A mailing list coming from LeHigh University.

MiniMed Strategic Partners:

    Boehringer-Mannheim - A leader in glucose meters, link goes to Boehringer's diabetes page.

    Eli Lilly and Company - A leader in diabetes care, that should need no introduction. This link will take you directly to Lilly's diabetes page.
