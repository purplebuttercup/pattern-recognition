Community involvement is a way of life for Abbott and Abbott people, whether it's at our Lake County, Illinois, headquarters or at one of the other manufacturing or research facilities around the world.   Learn about Abbott's HIV/AIDS science education programs.

With more than 54,000 employees and more than 50 manufacturing facilities, Abbott is a major presence in many communities around the world. We play an active role in the areas in which our employees live and work by directing our resources to a wide variety of community programs, initiatives and institutions. Supporting United Way, Junior Achievement, minority employment and scholarship programs are just some of the ways we contribute to these communities. Read on for information about some of our community programs.
