Abbott's mission is to improve lives. We do this by developing technologies that build, protect and improve people's health. With one of the most diverse product lines in the industry and unique expertise in many of the most common and important medical conditions, we touch the lives of millions of people around the world every year. Here's a closer look at the health areas we specialize in. 
Here you'll find up-to-date information about HIV and AIDS, as well as the integrated family of products that help detect and treat this disease.
Proper nutrition is essential to building and maintaining the good health of infants and toddlers. Here you'll find information on how to build your child's health through nutrition, and you'll learn about the world's broadest line of scientifically formulated pediatric nutritionals.
We pioneered the idea that nutritional intervention can improve and even save lives while helping to significantly reduce overall medical costs. Take a look at how Abbott nutritional products can help build, maintain and improve adult health and speed recovery time for hospital patients.
Abbott has been a leader in providing treatments for patients with renal (kidney) disease for 25 years. Here we give you facts on the causes and symptoms of kidney disease and other helpful information.
