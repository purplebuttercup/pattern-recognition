Breakthrough AIDS tests and treatments, the world's best-known nutritional products, powerful antibiotics, innovative anesthetics and environmentally friendly pesticides are just a few of Abbott's thousands of products. You can learn more about them below.
You can find Abbott products in more than 130 countries. Here's a map of locations that tells you where we are around the world.
Abbott's nutritional products can improve the health and well-being of people of all ages worldwide. Here you can find specific nutrition, health and lifestyle information from Abbott's Ross Products Division, including infant formulas, adult nutritionals, medical nutritionals for patients with special dietary needs, and enteral feeding devices.
Abbott's pharmaceutical product line-up includes one of the world's best-selling antibiotics, a breakthrough treatment for AIDS patients, and a neuroscience compound to treat migraine, epilepsy and manic depression, among many others.
Anesthesia for surgery patients, diagnostic imaging agents and drug-delivery systems are just a sampling of the products we provide to help hospitals deliver innovative medical solutions while lowering the overall cost of health care.
Abbott developed the world's first AIDS test in 1985. Today, Abbott diagnostic products help protect the world's blood supply from AIDS and hepatitis, screen blood samples for workplace testing, measure disease progress and help millions of people with diabetes around the world lead normal — and safe — lives.
For years Abbott has specialized in developing manufacturing processes producing sophisticated drugs. Building on this expertise, we pioneered the development of environmentally friendly pesticides and plant growth regulators used by farmers around the world. Today we're also developing innovative products that improve animal health.
