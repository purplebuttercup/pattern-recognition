Common Stock

EnergyNorth, Inc.'s common stock is traded on the New York Stock Exchange. The symbol is EI.

Dividend

EnergyNorth currently pays an annual indicated cash dividend rate of $1.28 per share ($.32 per share per quarter). Historically, the Company has paid a cash dividend in March, June, September, and December. EnergyNorth offers shareholders the opportunity to have their dividends electronically deposited in a savings or checking account.

Dividend Reinvestment and Stock Purchase Plan

EnergyNorth offers all registered shareholders a convenient method to purchase additional shares through dividend reinvestment and with voluntary cash payments of not less than $50 up to a maximum of $2500 per calendar quarter. Other features of the plan include:

  • Plan participants do not pay brokerage commissions, service charges or fees with respect to the reinvestment of dividends or the purchase of shares with optional payments.
  • Plan participants may sell their unissued shares through the Stock Transfer Agent.
  • A safekeeping service is available at no cost to the shareholder to hold stock certificates and thereby protect against lost of theft.

 A Plan prospectus will be mailed by the Company as requested.
