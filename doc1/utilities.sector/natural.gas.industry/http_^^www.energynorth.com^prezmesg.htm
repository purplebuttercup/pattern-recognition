Welcome to EnergyNorth, Inc., a major supplier of natural gas and propane to New Hampshire consumers. I'm Bob Giordano, president and chief executive officer of EnergyNorth. Through its two operating subsidiaries, EnergyNorth serves more than 82,000 natural gas and propane customers in southern and central New Hampshire. EnergyNorth distributes natural gas to 80 percent of all natural gas customers in New Hampshire.

The ability to use the World Wide Web on the Internet to communicate the clean fuel advantages of natural gas and propane is exciting. So whether you are an investor, a customer of EnergyNorth's, or an interested consumer, click on one of the icons below for more information on EnergyNorth and learn more about our business, our people, and the services we provide to our customers. We hope you find our site useful and informative.

If you have any questions about either the content or the format of our website, please send them via e-mail to Mike Netkovick at netkovick@energynorth.com

For more information on natural gas and other New England gas companies, contact The Clean Fuel Network. This is a useful site for students who are researching the history and the uses of natural gas.
