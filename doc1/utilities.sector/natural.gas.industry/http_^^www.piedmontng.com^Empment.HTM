Career Opportunities

Piedmont employees approximately 2,000 people in North Carolina, South Carolina, and Tennessee. The Company offers career opportunities in Gas Supply, Information Systems, Engineering, Marketing, Accounting/Finance and various other related business areas. Every effort is made to place new employees in positions which best utilize their abilities and in which they will be able to achieve both personal satisfaction and opportunity for growth.

Piedmont offers excellent salary and benefits packages that include retirement, 401(k), stock purchase and tuition refund plans.

If you are interested in career opportunities with Piedmont, please send us a letter of interest, specifying the type of position that you are interested in, and resume, including salary history and requirements, to:
