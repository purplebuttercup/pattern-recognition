AGL Resources Stock

Q. Can I buy or sell stock directly through AGL Resources?

A. Yes. First, you must enroll in ResourcesDirect, the company's direct stock purchase and dividend reinvestment plan - which you can access through this web site by clicking on ResourcesDirect.

Q. How can I enroll in ResourcesDirect?

A. You must obtain a prospectus and complete an enrollment form. Those materials are available - free - by clicking on ResourcesDirect. Or you may call 1-800-866-1543, and the materials will be mailed to you at no charge.

Q. Will the company charge me for buying shares through ResourcesDirect?

A. No fees or commissions are charged for purchases of stock through ResourcesDirect. For more details, see ResourcesDirect.

Q. Where is the AGL Resources stock traded and what is its ticker symbol?

A. It's traded on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol ATG.

Q. How do I find the stock listed in the newspaper?

A. It's listed in newspapers as AGL Res.

Q. What is the cusip number?

A. 001204-10-6

Dividends

Q. What is AGL Resources' common stock dividend rate?

A. The quarterly cash dividend is 27 cents per share as of September 1, 1997.

Q. Does AGL Resources offer dividend reinvestment?

A. Yes, through ResourcesDirect. For additional details, see ResourcesDirect.

Q. When are dividends usually paid?

A. They traditionally are paid on the first of March, June, September, and December.
