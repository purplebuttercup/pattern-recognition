Piedmont Natural Gas Company, Inc., incorporated in 1950, is an energy and services company primarily engaged in the transportation and sale of natural gas and the sale of propane to 623,000 residential, commercial and industrial customers in North Carolina, South Carolina and Tennessee.

The Company's utility operation serves over 575,000 natural gas customers. The Company and its non-utility subsidiaries and divisions are also engaged in acquiring, marketing and arranging for the transportation and storage of natural gas for large-volume purchasers, in retailing residential and commercial gas appliances and in the sale of propane to over 48,500 customers in the Company's three-state service area.
