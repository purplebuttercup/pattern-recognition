Subsidiaries

IEI Investments was formed as a subsidiary holding company to separate the operations and financings of the non-regulated businesses from the regulated businesses. Its subsidiaries are: Energy Realty, which is a limited partner in three affordable housing complexes in the Indiana Gas service area and the owner of a warehouse that's leased to Indiana Gas; and IGC Energy, which is co-owner of ProLiance Energy, LLC. Proliance is a strategic alliance to provide natural gas and related administration services to utilities and customers in Indiana and surrounding states. IGC Energy's partner is an affiliate of Citizens Gas and Coke Utility of Indianapolis.
