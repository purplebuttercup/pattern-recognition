Alliances

IGC Energy is a joint owner of ProLiance Energy, LLC, a strategic alliance to provide natural gas supply and related administration services to utilities and customers in Indiana and surrounding states. The co-owner is an affiliate of Citizens Gas and Coke Utility, a local natural gas distribution company serving Indianapolis, Ind. ProLiance Energy began operation April 1, 1996.

For more information, visit the Proliance web site.
