The Williams Companies 1996 Annual Report

Welcome to The Williams Companies, where technology and talent create exceptional achievement. Williams is a high growth energy and telecommunications company with headquarters in Tulsa, Okla., and offices worldwide.

Williams consistently provides a high rate of return for our shareholders. Part of our ambitious financial goals include delivering superior, sustainable returns for our investors. Join Chairman Keith Bailey for a quick review of our goals and strategy.

We also have financial information available through our annual report and other SEC registered documents. The latest company information is available on the Williams News page.

Williams currently has assets in excess of $11.5 billion with 11 operating companies that range from the nation's largest system of interstate natural gas pipelines to an innovative telecommunications network.

How did we develop into the diverse, industry leading company that we are today. The Williams Companies' story began in 1908 when Miller and David Williams began a small construction company. Since then, Williams has maintained the highest standard of performance and ethics in business and the communities in which we live and work.
