Become an AGL Resources Shareholder Directly with ResourcesDirect.

AGL Resources recently added a direct stock purchase feature to its Dividend Reinvestment and Stock Purchase Plan, and renamed the plan ResourcesDirect. Now potential investors can become an AGL Resources shareholder by making a direct, first-time investment from $250 to $5,000.

ResourcesDirect also allows you to -

  • have your cash dividends automatically reinvested to purchase more shares;
  • make optional, cash payments twice each month - from $25 per investment to $5,000 per month - which are used to purchase additional shares of common stock for your account;
  • make purchases without fees or commissions.
