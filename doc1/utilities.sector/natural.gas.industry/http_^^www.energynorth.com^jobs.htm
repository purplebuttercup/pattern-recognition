Vice President, Chief Information Officer

EnergyNorth, Inc. is looking for an individual who will perform strategic technology planning, design and implementation. The successful candidate will coordinate applications development initiative and resources, review requests for new/improved information systems and applications, and make recommendations.

With responsibility for the ongoing enhancement of technology infrastructure and architecture, the successful candidate will identify, research and qualify desirable enabling technologies, and ensure efficient information systems to improve service to customers, suppliers and employers.

A bachelor's degree in computer science, business, engineering or equivalent is required. Experience as a senior-level manager, ideally in a small to medium-sized technical company, is desired. Experience with client/server systems, an understanding of mainframe and Internet technologies, and excellent communication, writing, analytical, presentation and budgeting skills are essential.

EnergyNorth offers an excellent salary and benefit package, including health, dental, 401K and retirement plans.

EnergyNorth, Inc. is looking for an internal application developer who is eager to take on new challenges and migrate with us to a client/server environment.

We are offering an exciting ground floor opportunity for the right candidate to grow with EnergyNorth as we migrate from our current IBM mainframe environment to the new world of Microsoft NT networks, Windows 95 desktops, Oracle RDBMS and PowerBuilder.
