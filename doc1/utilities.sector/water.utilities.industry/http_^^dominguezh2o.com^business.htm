Dominguez can offer significant value to cities, small water companies, and developers through strategic partnerships. The company has demonstrated expertise in the areas of groundwater production, water rights, and treatment. Whether you are a city official looking for ways to add to your water supply on a tight budget, a mutual water company operator facing increasingly strict water quality standards, or a developer seeking a knowledgeable company to install new water facilities at a low cost, Dominguez can help you.

Talent

Dominguez takes pride in its staff of highly-qualified professionals with experience in both public and private sectors. In addition to its state-certified water distribution and treatment operators, Dominguez employs a capable mix of registered professional engineers, certified public accountants, water chemists, and staff with other relevant academic and technical credentials. But no matter what their area of expertise, each Dominguez employee is committed to efficiency and service.

Technology

Dominguez has put state-of-the-art technologies to work to ensure success in the areas of groundwater development and water treatment.

In the South Bay area of Los Angeles County, Dominguez adds one million gallons of water per day to its supply through use of a reverse-osmosis desalination facility. In Kern County, Dominguez utilizes leading-edge technologies, including filtration and polymer-injection, to provide a high quality water supply. And, to treat supplies in all of its water systems, Dominguez uses on-site chlorine generators, a method which is safer and less expensive than traditional chlorination methods.

Other technologies increasing Dominguez' operational efficiency include:

  • Computer monitoring of tank levels, well production, and system controls (SCADA)
  • Linked data and network systems, including integrated computer-aided drafting and design (CADD)
  • Integrated in-house, certified water quality testing laboratory



Resources

Dominguez offers two more resources through partnership: extensive water rights holdings and investment capital. The company, which was formed in 1911, has extensive water rights in the South Bay area of Los Angeles County. This asset has given management valuable expertise in brokering water rights.

Dominguez also maintains a superior financial structure. Profitability and prudent fiscal management afford Dominguez the resources for strategic investment.
