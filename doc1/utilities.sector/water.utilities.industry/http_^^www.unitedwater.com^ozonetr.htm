United Water pioneers water and wastewater treatment through innovative technologies such as ozone treatment, an advanced drinking water purification process. United Water, which operates a state-of-the-art ozone plant in New Jersey, is evaluating the ozone process at other facilities.

United Water uses an innovative ultraviolet disinfection system at a wastewater treatment plant in Florida. This process uses ultraviolet light instead of chlorine to remove bacteria from the water.
