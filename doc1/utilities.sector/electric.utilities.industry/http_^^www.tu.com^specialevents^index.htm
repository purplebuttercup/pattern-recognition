June 14, 1997
KRLD's Al Carrell Fan Club in partnership with TU Electric promotion begins today. For those without air conditioning, summers in Texas can be life-threatening. KRLD listeners are being encouraged to help out by donating new electric fans to the needy. TU Electric's Energy Aid representatives will direct donors to participating social service agencies where they can drop off their gifts. Last year KRLD and TU Electric collected more than 2,000 electric fans for Metroplex residents. In the second year of the campaign, the promotion has been expanded to all KRLD listeners. To make your donation, call TU Electric Energy Aid, 1-800-550-9801, Monday through Friday, 7:30 a.m. to 6 p.m., this summer.
