Logon screen for licensed suppliers
PP&L has been a leader in the effort to give electricity customers a choice because we believe it's good for customers. Now we are offering a pilot program that gives our customers the chance to save money while seeing how customer choice works.
  • Five percent of our customers will get the opportunity to 'test the waters' of competition. They can select a new electricity supplier or choose to buy electricity from PP&L. Participants can drop out of the pilot at any time.
  • PP&L will continue to deliver high-quality electric service, maintain the transmission lines and answer questions.
  • Customers will get the chance to shop for the company that supplies their electricity -- and to look for savings. PP&L will give a credit of as much as 4 cents for each kilowatt-hour of electricity. (Credits will vary by rate schedule.)
  • The registration period ended Sept. 29. We will select customers at random from those who applied.
  • If you are not a PP&L customer, but you are interested in participating in Pennsylvania's Customer Choice Pilot Program you need to contact your local electric utility.
