MidAmerican Energy, Iowa's largest utility, provides electric and natural gas service in a 10,600 square mile area with a population of 1.7 million. The service area includes much of Iowa and portions of Illinois, Nebraska and South Dakota. The largest communities served by MidAmerican are Des Moines, Cedar Rapids, Sioux City, Waterloo, Iowa City and Council Bluffs, Iowa; the Quad Cities area of Iowa and Illinois; and Sioux Falls, South Dakota. MidAmerican provides service to 642,000 electric customers and 610,000 natural gas customers.
