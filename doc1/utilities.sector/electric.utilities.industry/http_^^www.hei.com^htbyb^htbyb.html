Hawaiian Tug & Barge Corp. and Young Brothers, Ltd. are the principal movers of maritime cargo within the state of Hawaii, operating a fleet of 11 tugs and 10 barges. Hawaiian Tug & Barge Corporation provides charter tug towing and harbor-assist services. Young Brothers, Limited  provides regulated, scheduled barge service to all of the state's major islands.
