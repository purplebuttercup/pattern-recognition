Texas Utilities announces it will acquire Lufkin-Conroe Communications Company, a privately-held local exchange telephone company serving Southeast Texas, for $328 million.

The Texas Utilities Company board of directors elects Jerry Farrington chairman emeritus. Succeeding Farrington as chairman is Erle Nye, who will serve as both chairman and chief executive of TU Company.

Texas Utilities reports first quarter earnings of 51 cents a share.

EarthFair 97 and the Solar Model Car Race Championship Series are on board! Regional races are being held every weekend until the finals. Bring the family to Energy Park on April 19th to see some amazing new technologies and watch the races.
