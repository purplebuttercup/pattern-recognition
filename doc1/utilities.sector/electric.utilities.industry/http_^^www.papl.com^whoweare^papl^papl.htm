PP&L, Inc. serves a region with a population of about 2.5 million people. Its principal cities include Allentown, Bethlehem, Harrisburg, Hazleton, Lancaster, Scranton, Wilkes-Barre and Williamsport.

PP&L operates the Susquehanna nuclear power plant near Berwick, Pennsylvania – consistently rated as one of the safest and best-run nuclear facilities in the country. PP&L also has five coal-fired power plants and two hydroelectric plants. The Martins Creek generating plant has the capability to burn either oil or natural gas. PP&L has about 6,500 employees.

But generating power is only part of the story. PP&L is deeply involved in its communities through programs to promote economic growth, help low-income customers, enhance education and make wise use of our natural resources.
