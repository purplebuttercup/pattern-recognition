The world is full of opportunities to make sound investments in electric power projects. PP&L Resources, Inc. is plugged in to this exciting new market through PP&L Global (formerly Power Markets Development Co.), a subsidiary formed in 1994.

In July 1996, PP&L Global purchased a 25 percent interest in South Western Electricity plc, a British regional electric utility company. This is PP&L Global’s largest overseas investment to date, and an important part of the company’s effort to grow from a U.S. regional supplier of electricity to a more broadly based energy company. PP&L Global also has investments in Argentina, Bolivia, Peru, Spain and Portugal.

Through PP&L Global, we’re capitalizing on opportunities for growth in the changing electric industry.
