Unicom Thermal Technologies Inc. is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Unicom Enterprises which provides district cooling service buildings in Chicago's Loop and near north areas in the central city. It was incorporated July 30, 1993 after Senate Bill 770, Energy Options for Illinois, cleared both chambers of the Illinois General Assembly and was signed into law by Governor Edgar. The company is also a corporate relative of ComEd.
