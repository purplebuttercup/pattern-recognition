PP&L has a reputation as a good place to work.

 We want to preserve and enhance that reputation. This is a time of exciting changes for the electric utility industry, and we seek to find individuals with the motivation and skills to help us meet those challenges. Having a knowledgeable, dedicated work force makes strong business sense and ensures us of delivering on our promises to those we serve.

      At PP&L, employees are our future. We want them to be satisfied, motivated and proud to work here. We'll continue our constant focus on the safety of our employees, and provide ample opportunities for personal and professional development. We're committed to giving all of our employees the tools and training they need, along with attractive salaries and an excellent benefits program.

PP&L practices equal employment opportunity for all persons without regard to race, religion, color, sex, national origin, age, veteran status or disability.

At present we are only interested in responses to PP&L posted career opportunities.

 To apply for our posted vacancies, you may e-mail us directly. Send your cover letter as the main document and provide your resume as an attachment in text (.TXT), Rich Text Format (.RTF) or MS Word for Windows (.DOC) format. You may also fax your resume to us at (610) 774-4995. Please indicate in your cover letter the six-digit code number of the position for which you are submitting information. (The code number can be found at the beginning of each online position description.)
