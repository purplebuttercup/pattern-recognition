HEI Power Corp. (HEIPC) is a wholly owned subsidiary of Hawaiian Electric Industries Inc. (HEI), one of the three largest corporations in Hawaii and parent to Hawaiian Electric Company (HECO), the electric utility which provides electric power to 95% of the people of Hawaii.

HEIPC was established in 1995 to develop, build, own and operate electric power generation facilities in the Asia Pacific Region, using the standards of excellence and technical expertise in isolated power systems that have served HECO's island utility customers well for over 100 years.
