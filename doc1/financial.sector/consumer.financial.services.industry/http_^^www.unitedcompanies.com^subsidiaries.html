UC Lending originates home equity loans on a retail basis through direct marketing and third-party referral sources.


UNICOR® purchases home equity loans through a network of mortgage brokers and correspondents.


GINGER MAE® offers home equity loans through a network of commercial banks, savings and loans, credit unions, and other traditional lending sources.


Southern Mortgage Acquisition purchases home equity loans in bulk from a variety of sources.


UC Funding acts as one of a variety of lending sources for dealers and purchasers of manufactured housing.
