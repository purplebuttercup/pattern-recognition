ACE has three franchise options:

1) Full-service store franchise - A traditional store capable of offering the complete variety of financial services - $30,000 initial franchise fee and a initial total investment of $150,000 to $200,000. Royalty is 5% of gross monthly receipts.

2) Kiosk franchise - A self contained center that can be located within a convenience store and offer check-cashing, money orders, wire transfers and others depending on the net size. - $15,000 initial franchise fee and a initial total investment of $75,000 to $120,000. Royalty is 5% of gross monthly receipts.

3) Small-market franchise - A kiosk that is located in towns with a population of 15,000 or less. - $7,500 initial franchise fee and a initial total investment of $75,000 to $120,000. Royalty is 5% of gross monthly receipts.

Act Now!
If you are serious at becoming a franchise owner you should act now. For a limited time, ACE will waive the first three months royalty payment in an effort to help a franchise owner build their business. After all, we want your system to grow, so we can grow!!
