Credit Depot Corporation, founded in Gainesville, Georgia in 1986, is a full-service non-conforming residential mortgage lender and servicer. We feature A, B, C and D grade mortgage loan products that are originated exclusively through approved lenders and brokers.

Credit Depot Corporation originates, underwrites and services its mortgage loans through locations in Florida, Georgia, Illinois, Kentucky, Michigan, Mississippi, North Carolina, Ohio, South Carolina, Tennessee, and Virginia. We are a publicly traded company (NASDAQ:LEND), an Equal Opportunity Employer and a member of numerous state and national lending and business associations.

After a careful evaluation of available mortgage programs, Credit Depot Corporation has concluded that the secondary market does not provide appropriate access to borrowers who do not fit the mold of more traditional residential mortgage programs. In response, we offer a full spectrum of mortgage products that allow the originator to serve a broader customer base than traditional conforming products will allow. Credit Depot Corporation has become a leader in the industry with our unique, non-conforming mortgage product lines.
