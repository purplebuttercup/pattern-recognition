More Than Just Financing

MLC is structured differently than traditional financial services companies. We work as partners with our clients to advise them on all aspects of managing fluctuating markets. With broad experience in up-and-down interest rate environments, MLC's investment professionals help you determine the best way to identify your objectives, minimize risk, and achieve your goals. We remain your partner throughout the process, ensuring prompt closure of the transaction: It's a partnership that optimizes opportunities.

With over two decades of direct experience in the financial services and leasing industry, MLC is uniquely positioned to offer individualized solutions to whatever diverse financing challenges you may have. This dynamic combination of financial, marketing, and equipment experience allows us to serve your financial needs with speed, efficiency, and discretion.

Financing Expertise

MLC provides its customers with leasing/financing of information technology assets as well as an analysis of financing provided by other companies. Programs include:

Operating leases (true FASB leases)
Capital leases
Lease purchase financings
Rentals
Secured loan and operating loan financings
Transaction-based financings
 
Our strength and focus in this area is an understanding of, and the ability to, finance non-traditional assets such as software, services, and maintenance/warranty agreements -- a vision we believe is the future of information technology financing. In addition to mainframes and peripherals, we finance used equipment, networks, and distributed systems. MLC also provides lease financing for the more traditional hard assets.

Legal/Contractual Expertise

Like any business transaction, the details of the lease or financing agreement are important and can have a profound effect on the lessee both in terms of cost and flexibility. Experience has placed MLC on both sides of contract negotiations and we've developed an impressive amount of expertise in the critical areas of documentation and protection of the interests of all parties. We thoroughly research the customers' present and future needs so that maximum flexibility and minimum costs are obtained. Leasing is a binding legal arrangement in which all issues must be thoroughly examined: MLC's precision is invaluable during this process.
