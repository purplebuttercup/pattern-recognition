Freddie Mac (NYSE- FRE), is a commercially traded Fortune 100 company. As a national leader in the secondary mortgage market, we are one of America's top 5 diversified financial companies. In January of 1997, we reported our 26th consecutive year of profit and are still going strong. For the third consecutive year, Freddie Mac has been named among the most successful, well managed companies on Fortune magazine's "Most Admired" list. We have an excellent record of hiring, diversity, company growth, benefits, salary increases, training, and promotions. Freddie Mac also helped finance the homes of nearly 20 million American families - that's one in six homes in America. All of this adds up to make Freddie Mac an excellent place to work!

Some of our locations even have recreation programs and a fitness center, full service cafeteria, ATM machine, and on-site dry cleaning. Freddie Mac is an Equal Opportunity Employer who is committed to diversity in the workplace.
