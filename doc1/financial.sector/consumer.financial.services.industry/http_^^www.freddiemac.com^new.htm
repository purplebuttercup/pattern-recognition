What's New At Freddie Mac This Month
Our site has grown! In our continuing effort to make this web site both useful and informative to our customers and friends, we have added a lot of new information:

For Lenders: We've expanded our Information for Single-Family Lenders . For the first time, lenders can use the Internet to read the latest Single-Family Seller/Servicer Guide Bulletins, or peruse descriptive factsheets about programs and services like Loan Prospector®, Expanding MarketsSM, Gold Cash Xtra®, Gold Connection® and EarlyIndicatorSM..
You'll find detailed information here about GoldWorks®, a secure network linking mortgage lenders to Freddie Mac and more than 1,000 leading players in the mortgage industry.


For Investors: Our Mortgage Securities site has been enhanced to give investors better access to more comprehensive information about their holdings. Investors can now obtain complete descriptions of Freddie Mac PCs, historical factor details and other security-specific information. Investors can also obtain electronic versions of base offering circulars for the range of Freddie Mac mortgage security products.

For Homebuyers: Our "Freddie Mac Homes" site has been redesigned and renamed. If you are a prospective homebuyer, be sure to visit the new HomeSteps site. HomeSteps lists thousands of available properties, from single-family homes to city high-rise and country properties. You'll find a link to HomeSteps on our Homebuyers page, along with a compilation of Frequently Asked Questions we've put together in response to questions from homebuyers. In addition, you'll find two useful Freddie Mac publications for homebuyers:
  • Credit Scores: A Win-Win Approach, offers answers to many of the typical questions homebuyers may have about the use of credit scoring in the mortgage loan assessment process, and
  • A Consumer Home Inspection Kit provides invaluable advice to help homebuyers make informed decisions about a prospective home.

For Everyone: Are you interested in Freddie Mac's current research and information on mortgage finance and housing issues? Take a look at our redesigned Secondary Mortgage Markets Online: the online version of Freddie Mac's Secondary Mortgage Markets magazine.

In the months ahead, we expect to continue to add information and new features. We should point out, however, that Freddie Mac provides this site as a means of helping our friends and customers keep up-to-date about on-going activities. At present, we are not able to conduct business through this Internet site, and ask that you not direct business questions here. Please feel free to direct any comments about the site itself to our Webmaster.
