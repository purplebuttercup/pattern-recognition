A principal business activity for Freddie Mac is the purchase of single-family and multifamily residential mortgages from mortgage lenders, which include both depository institutions and mortgage bankers.

Single-Family: This site offers a host of information for single-family lenders, from the latest Seller and Servicer Guide Bulletins to descriptive factsheets about programs and services like Loan Prospector®, Expanding MarketsSM, Gold Cash Xtra®, Gold Connection® and EarlyIndicatorSM.. Lenders can also find Freddie Mac publications here to help answer homebuyers' inquires about credit scoring and consumer home inspections, along with a few of the key forms necessary to do business with Freddie Mac.

Multifamily: Freddie Mac offers a full range of competitively priced, reliable multifamily mortgage purchase programs for the acquisition, refinance or moderate rehabilitation of apartment buildings. To help the nation's lenders take full advantage of these programs, our Multifamily Lenders site provides detailed descriptions of key multifamily products such as our Conventional Cash Mortgage Purchase, Rate Reset Mortgage, Structured Transactions, PC One, and Low-Income Targeted Initiatives. You can also find information on our standard and early-rate lock delivery options, uniform instruments, and a contact list of our Program Plus® lenders -- lenders who have years of lending expertise and proven track records of success in multifamily mortgage loans.
