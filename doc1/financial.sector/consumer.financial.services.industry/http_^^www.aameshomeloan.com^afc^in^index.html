PR Newswire
 



Aames Financial Corporation (NYSE:AAM), as a mortgage banker, funds most of its loans for securitization and sale in the secondary market. Aames-sponsored securitizations also include loans originated by other lenders and purchased through its correspondent program. Aames' singular focus on the subprime-credit lending market offers stockholders a "pure play" in this growing industry.
