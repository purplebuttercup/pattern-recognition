Roxy Roxborough, LVSC president and founder, has led the sports bookmaking industry into a new hi-tech era, including the dynamic new product "Instant Odds," a state-of-the-art computer software program which provides real-time sports odds from major Las Vegas sports books. Roxy was recently named to GQ Magazine's "Fifty Most Important People in Sports," and to the Daily Racing Form's "Ten Most Influential People in Gaming."
LVSC is the primary odds provider for 75 percent of Nevada casino sports books. International customers include bookmakers and lotteries in Canada, Mexico, England, Ireland and Australia.
The staff at LVSC is responsible for setting odds on all major North American sporting events, as well as providing customers with the latest information on weather, injuries, starting times and line movements.
LVSC, in business since 1982, combines the latest computer technology with personal service to provide odds and pointspreads to legal sports bookmaking operations worldwide.
