Ordering literature online is much like grocery shopping; you may place several different items in your "shopping cart" before checking out. Of course, unlike the grocery store, this shopping trip is free!

When your literature arrives, be sure to give your investment representative a call - he or she can help you decide how Franklin Templeton products may help you meet your financial objectives.

You may search for literature specific to any of our mutual funds or retirement plans by clicking on the appropriate link. You may order one of each piece that interests you.

For more information on any of our funds, contact your investment representative or download a free prospectus, which contains important information about a fund's investment objective, sales charges, expenses and risk considerations. Please read the prospectus carefully before investing or sending money.
