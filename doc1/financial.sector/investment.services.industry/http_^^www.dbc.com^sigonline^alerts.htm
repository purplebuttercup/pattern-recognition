New Alert System

Signal Online allows you to act quickly on market movement through a new alert system.

You can receive bid, ask, price, and volume alerts on any of the issues in your portfolio via pager, email, cellular phone or your desktop through audio or visual alerts -- so you can act immediately on profit-making opportunities.

Click here for other valuable Signal Online Features!

If you have an alpha numeric pager or a SmartPhone, you can enter its address in the "To:" field in the Preferences dialog box to receive alerts that you select to send to your pager or SmartPhone; however, your computer running the application must be connected to an ISP in order to send alerts to your pager or phone.

Once you have set up your email preferences, click the "e-mail alert" select box for each alert that you want to receive via e-mail.
