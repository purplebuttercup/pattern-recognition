B.C. Ziegler and Company Divisions



Ziegler Securities

Ziegler Securities is responsible for all healthcare and long-term care bond financings underwritten by B.C. Ziegler and Company. Other financial services offered through Ziegler Securities include asset and liability management, special products for capital restructurings and interest rate management strategies. Ziegler Securities is headquartered at One South Wacker Drive, Suite 3080, Chicago, Ill. 60606-4617. In addition, Ziegler Securities, has several office locations around the country including West Bend, Wis., Walnut Creek (San Francisco), Calif., St. Petersburg, Fla., Washington, D.C. and Indianapolis, Ind.

Principal Preservation Portfolios, Inc.

Principal Preservation Portfolios, Inc. is a family of mutual funds sponsored by B.C. Ziegler and Company. Investment objectives of Principal Preservation funds include aggressive growth, growth and income, income and capital appreciation, federally tax-exempt income, double tax-exempt income for Wisconsin residents and preservation of principal with high current income.

Preferred Stock

Located in Mequon, Wis., this division is staffed by five professionals who have developed a niche in the institutional and broker/dealer preferred stock marketplace.
