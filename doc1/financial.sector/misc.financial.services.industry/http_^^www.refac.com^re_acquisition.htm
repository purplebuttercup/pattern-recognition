Acquisition Interests

REFAC is actively seeking acquisitions in the following general areas:

  • Licensing companies that own or control valuable intellectual property rights - - - patents, know-how, trademarks or copyrights.
  • Professional service companies engaged in specialized fields of industry and/or high tech venture capital projects. 
  • Small companies with competent top management that have patented technologies and proprietary know-how with good growth potential and promising licensing possibilities.

The company we seek must be currently profitable and have capable management that will regard its REFAC relationship as an opportunity for growth.

Although we are interested in the types of businesses outlined above, we are willing to consider exceptional opportunities that go beyond those listed.
