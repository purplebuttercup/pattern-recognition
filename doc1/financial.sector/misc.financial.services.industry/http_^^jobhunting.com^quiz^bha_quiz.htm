Yes No Have you been thinking about making a change for more than six months?

Yes No Have responsibilities been taken away from you?

Yes No Do you start watching the clock at 3:45 in the afternoon?

Yes No Do you feel underpaid?

Yes No Is your job affecting your health?

Yes No Do you browse through the Sunday classifieds?

Yes No Do you feel your talents are underutilized?

Yes No Do you suspect imminent plans for a corporate takeover, merger or buyout?
