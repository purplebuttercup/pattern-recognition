Welcome to Allied Capital's Interactive Small Business Forum. Here's where you can meet people who have a similar interest in small business, share your ideas with fellow entrepreneurs, business brokers, or other intermediaries as well as discuss your issues related to running a small business. The Small Business Forum acts like a specialized newsgroup on the web ­­ you can access the Forum as a web page and jump back quickly at any time using a bookmark. When you enter a new message in the forum, you have the option of being notified immediately via e-mail when someone replies to your message.

We hope this forum will provide our site visitors with the opportunity to interact in an open exchange regarding what's happening in the world of small business today. You're encouraged to visit frequently and to participate in the dialog.

Join in and let your voice be heard!
