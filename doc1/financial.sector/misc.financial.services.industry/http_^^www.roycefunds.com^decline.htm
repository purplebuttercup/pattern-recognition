This type of down market performance differential is in keeping with our historical performance patterns, especially when you consider:

  • Core portfolios, Royce Premier and Micro-Cap Funds, along with Royce Total Return and Low-Priced Stock have all outperformed the Russell 2000 in all of the Index's down quarters since their respective inceptions.

  • Newer portfolios, PMF II, Royce GiftShares and Royce Global Services, outperformed the Russell 2000 in the Index's only down quarter since their respective inceptions.

  • Flagship fund Pennsylvania Mutual Fund has outperformed the Russell 2000 in all of the Index's down quarters in the last fifteen years.
