    		 INVESTMENT FLEXIBILITY

    No-Load Funds put all of your money to work on investments, not into sales commissions.

    Low Minimums give you the flexibility to diversify. You can start your investment with $2,000 ($5,000 for Royce GiftShares Fund) and subsequent investments of $50 or more. You can open an IRA or 403(b)(7) account for as low as $500, and accounts opened with an Automatic Investment Plan can start with $500.

    Automatic Reinvestment of dividends and capital gains distributions keeps your money working hard for you. Or, you may choose to receive your distributions in cash.

    INVESTMENT PLANNING

    Telephone Purchase allows you to lock in your purchase price as of that day's close of regular trading on the New York Stock Exchange.

    Express Service permits you to purchase or redeem shares periodically or at your discretion by telephone or computer online access and to have funds automatically transferred to or from your bank account.

    Direct Deposit lets you "pay yourself first" by having your investments made directly from your net payroll or government check into your Royce Fund account each pay period.

    COMMUNICATIONS

    Investor Services representatives are available from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. Eastern time, Monday through Friday to answer questions regarding The Royce Funds and to assist you in completing your applications.

    Investor Services representatives are available during regular business hours to assist you with transactions and questions about your account.

    Royce InfoLine is available around the clock, 7 days a week, for you to check a fund's closing price, your account balance, time and price of your last transaction or distribution and the total returns of your Fund, and to request statements and tax information.

    Online Access is available through the Internet for e-mailing, learning more about the Funds, viewing and downloading prospectuses and applications and soon you will be able to access your account information and transact purchases, exchanges and redemptions.

    Account Statements, prepared on a cumulative year-to-date basis, are mailed after every transaction and on a semi-annual basis.

    Fund Reports are mailed semi-annually. Each contains details about your Fund's investments and its most recent performance.

    These services are described in detail in the Funds' Prospectus.
