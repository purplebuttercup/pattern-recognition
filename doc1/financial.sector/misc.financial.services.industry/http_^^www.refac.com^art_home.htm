Welcome to our Home Page!

Advanced Resin offers the only line of thermoplastic polyurethane hot melt adhesives in the industry. After more than 10 years of research and a development investment of more than $7 million, Advanced Resin is now able to make the benefits of polyurethane chemistry available in thermoplastic hot melt adhesives. The result is a new family of high performance, low viscosity thermoplastic polyurethane hot melt adhesives that offer many characteristics that were not previously available in one adhesive. This unusual combination of high strength, heat resistance, cold resistance, solvent resistance and adhesion to various substrates, together with the absence of solvents or hazardous materials, make these products truly unique. From performance in extreme cold to under the hood, with resistance to water and petroleum based products, this technology significantly expands the use of hot melt adhesives for the manufacturer and converter.

To find out more about Advanced Resin's products, please click on one of the following links:

Bondstar TM Hot Melt Adhesives - Designed for bonding and laminating fabrics, films, foams, flexible PVC, polyester non-wovens and many other substrates.

SnapTak TM Hot Melt Contact Adhesives - Designed for the furniture, marine and automotive industries, as well as woodworking, cabinetry and carpet-backing applications.

Memoriflex TM Elastomers - Designed for the compounder blending polyurethane, PVC and many other thermoplastics.
