Bernard Haldane Associates was founded in 1947 by Dr. Haldane,
the father of modern career development. For more than 50 years, Bernard Haldane has explored the dynamics of human potential, making his name synonymous with job hunting. He is recognized as the preeminent authority on career planning and personal development, and has empowered thousands of men and women with his tools for successful career advancement. Dr. Haldane received a White House commendation in 1946 for developing a reliable career transition system for military officers and since then, has designed programs and performed services which have assured continuing employment for individuals of all skill levels. Programs built upon the systems and procedures he pioneered are used by major corporations, religious institutions and government agencies, including Polaroid, the Atomic Energy Commission, and the Peace Corps. He is also the author of 14 books on the subject of career planning, including Career Satisfaction and Job Success.
