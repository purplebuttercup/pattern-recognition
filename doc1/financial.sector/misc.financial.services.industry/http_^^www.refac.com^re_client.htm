Typical Clients and Projects

Over the years, REFAC has represented many distinguished clients covering a broad range of products and product technologies. Today, our primary business may be broadly described as international licensing and technology transfer and consists of the following general categories:

  • The licensing of the untilized and partially developed patents of individual inventors and small companies, including the responsibilities, when necessary, of patent enforcement.
  • With our own seed funding or in cooperation with other entrepreneurs, the initiation and management of programs of patent development with the creation of new enterprise as the objective.
  • Representing the interests of manufacturers seeking to establish their products in foreign markets and/or their technology in noncompetitive fields of use.
  • The licensing of spin-off technologies and unexploited patents of major corporations.
