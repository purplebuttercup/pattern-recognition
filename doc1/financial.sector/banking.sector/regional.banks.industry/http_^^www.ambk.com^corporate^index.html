AMBANC Corp. is a multi-bank holding company incorporated under the laws of Indiana. Its wholly owned subsidiaries are AmBank Indiana, N.A. the lead bank established in 1888. AmBank Illinois, N.A. with principal office in Robinson, Illinois, established in 1932. Through these principal offices and their branches, AMBANC Corp. engages in a wide range of commercial and personal banking activities, providing a full range of products and services with 26 branches, in seven counties and two states.
