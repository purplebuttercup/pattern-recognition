Magna Reports Record Third Quarter Earnings
Magna Group, Inc. today announced third quarter earnings of $21.2 million, or 64 cents per common share, an increase of 32.6 percent over the $16.0 million, or 57 cents per common share, earned in second quarter 1996. See earnings.

Magna Declares Quarterly Dividend
The board of directors of Magna Group, Inc. declared a quarterly cash dividend of 25 cents per share on its common stock. The dividend is payable on December 10, 1997, to stockholders of record on November 15, 1997. See dividend.
