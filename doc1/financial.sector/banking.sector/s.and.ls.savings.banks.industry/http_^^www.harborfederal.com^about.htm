Corporate Profile

Harbor Federal Savings Bank is headquartered in Fort Pierce, Florida and is a community-oriented savings institution offering traditional deposit and mortgage loan products. With approximately $1 billion in assets, Harbor Federal Savings Bank is one of the largest independent financial institutions in Florida.

At Present, Harbor Federal Savings Bank operates 23 full service banking offices throughout Brevard, Indian River, Martin, Okeechobee, St. Lucie and Volusia Counties which are located in East Central Florida.
