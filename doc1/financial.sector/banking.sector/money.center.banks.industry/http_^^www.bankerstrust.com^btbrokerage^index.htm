BT Brokerage is the wholly-owned broker/dealer subsidiary of Bankers Trust New York Corporation where we own two seats on the New York Stock Exchange, and are members of the NASD, MSRB and associate member at the American Stock Exchange.

Founded in 1985, BT Brokerage provides its institutional clients superior execution services at discount prices on all major exchanges and over-the-counter markets.

A key benefit is the direct access we provide to our trading desk, which facilitates order execution while providing timely and accurate market information.
