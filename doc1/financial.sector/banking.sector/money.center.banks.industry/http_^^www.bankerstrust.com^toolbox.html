The Bankers Trust site requires a browser capable of displaying HTML tables.

The site has been tested extensively and works well with the following browser:

  • Netscape 3.0x
    We have also found the site to work well with:
  • Microsoft Explorer 3

    Additional software used by the site includes:

  • Adobe Acrobat

    Note: For best results, your system should be set for 256 colors.

    Navigation
    There are several different methods available for navigating through the site. The bottom of every page contains a list of links that will bring you to the main sections of the site. Links can also be found in the lefthand column on some pages. In addition, all pages have a navigation icon similar to the one pictured at right. Simply click on a word to get to that section, or click on the "Bankers Trust" words on the bottom of the icon to return to home page.
    Viewing Graphics
    We've made many of the graphics and screens on the site smaller for optimal downloading. To view these screens full size:
    1. Click on the desired image. An option menu is displayed.
    2. Select "View this Image". The selected image is displayed at its full size.
    3. To return to the previous screen click "Back".
