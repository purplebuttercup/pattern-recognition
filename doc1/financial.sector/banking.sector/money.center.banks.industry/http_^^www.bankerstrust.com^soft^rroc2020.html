The service is designed to give investors information on the risks imbedded in their portfolios, including the types of risk and levels of exposure, and the location of the risks, by asset type, product, country, or by manager. RAROC 2020 shows the risks and potential risks and opportunities involved in existing or proposed positions under a variety of scenarios. It represents a probabilistic view.
