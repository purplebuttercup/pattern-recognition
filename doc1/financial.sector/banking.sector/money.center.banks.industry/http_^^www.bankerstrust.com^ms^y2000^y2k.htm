The senior management of Bankers Trust Company has accepted the challenge of ensuring corporate Year 2000 compliance to meet your business needs now and into the next century. Our goal and corporate policy is to deliver uninterrupted service across the full product and service offering that we provide to our clients. As one of your key financial service providers, we accept these responsibilities and anticipate working closely with you and others to achieve this goal.

Like all members of the global financial services community, Bankers Trust recognizes the risk caused by the Year 2000 problem. To address these risks, we have created the "Bankers Trust Year 2000 Program," a worldwide effort among our businesses, clients, external technology providers, participants and regulators. Risk assessments and mitigation activities are ongoing now, and it is our goal that they will be completed by December 1998 or earlier.

For additional information, please contact your Relationship Manager
