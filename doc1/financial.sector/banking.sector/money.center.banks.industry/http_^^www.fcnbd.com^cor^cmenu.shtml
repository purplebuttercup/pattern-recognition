Corporate Financial Services

With a reach that stretches around the globe and a powerful line-up of products and services, First Chicago NBD is well equipped to serve corporations and institutions at home and abroad.

Both merger partners bring expertise that enhances the total menu of products and services, and by joining forces, we have more resources to invest in technology to keep pace with our customers' changing needs.

Our new company operates a network of international offices that now includes locations in England, Germany, Canada, Japan, Hong Kong, Australia, South Korea, China, Taiwan, Argentina, Mexico and Singapore. With our growing presence in the international arena, First Chicago NBD can bring world markets closer to all customers, both the large corporations historically served by First Chicago and the mid-corporate companies that made up NBD's traditional customer base.

But whether or not our customers operate internationally, the complexities of today's world demand access to the kinds of global products and services available from First Chicago NBD. We provide financing solutions -- securitized vehicles, syndications, publicly issued debt, tax-exempt securities -- to meet our customers' needs, anywhere they do business, any place on the globe.

In global risk management, for example, our company is a leader in providing interest rate, currency, equity and commodity derivatives, as well as in asset/liability modeling of market risk. In derivatives and foreign exchange, we are a major market maker in key financial centers in the U.S., Europe and Asia.

First Chicago NBD is also a dominant player in global cash management, offering corporate treasurers a full range of collection, payment, investment and information reporting services. Our lockbox network, which now includes a Toronto location, is one of the most extensive in the country; and our leadership in electronic data interchange -- the exchange of data such as invoices and payments electronically -- ensures our customers the latest in the paperless movement of information and funds.

In fact, our company is an innovative leader in delivering sophisticated electronic information products to help customers manage their cash positions, access data, move funds and hedge risk.

In the trade arena, under the banner of FIRSTTRADE®2000, First Chicago NBD offers a family of electronic trade services geared specifically to the needs of importers and exporters. By combining expertise in international banking, cash management and electronic trade banking, First Chicago NBD can provide a comprehensive service that is customer-focused, customer-driven and leading-edge.

With our subsidiary First Chicago Trust Company of New York, our company ranks as the U.S. market leader in providing stock transfer and shareholder services. First Chicago NBD is also active in master trust and global custody services, offering sophisticated products and services to pension fund sponsors.
