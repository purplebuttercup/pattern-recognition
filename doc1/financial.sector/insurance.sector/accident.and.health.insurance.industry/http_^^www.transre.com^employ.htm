Accounting Positions

Transatlantic's Corporate Accounting Department is responsible for all Statutory and GAAP Financial Statements which are prepared on a quarterly and annual basis. The department also provides various schedules and reports for the SEC 10Q and 10K filings, and ensures the completion and accuracy of all filings, which also includes the Risk Based Capital (RBC).

The Corporate Accounting Department has an opening for a senior accountant to assist in achieving the objectives stated above whose responsibilities will include, among others, various investment reports.

Position: Senior Accountant

Requirements:

  1. Three or more years of statutory financial accounting experience.
  2. Investment accounting experience.
  3. Excellent communication skills.
  4. Experience in Microsoft Windows software.
  5. Experience in Freedom software desirable.

Actuarial Positions

Transatlantic has a full service Actuarial Department which is intimately involved with all facets of the company. The department establishes and monitors loss reserves, analyzes the profitability of the various profit centers for senior management, provides pricing support to the underwriting areas, and assists in the preparation of various financial statements and reports.

The Actuarial Department has openings for senior manager, mid-level manager, and entry level positions with the respective requirements below. In general, we are looking for bright, highly motivated people who are technically competent and who possess excellent analytical and communication skills.

Position #1: Senior Manager

Requirements:

  1. Six or more years of actuarial experience.
  2. Three or more years of reinsurance pricing and/or loss reserving experience.
  3. Staff management experience.
  4. Excellent communication skills.
  5. Associate or Fellow of the Casualty Actuarial Society.

Facultative Underwriting Positions

The Property Facultative Department within our Chicago branch is seeking a well presented and motivated individual.

Position: Underwriter

Ideally, this individual should be capable of managing day to day underwriting of his or her particular segment of the book of business. Reporting to the branch Property Manager, he or she will be responsible for developing and marketing both broker and direct sources within the Mid-West and West Coast regions. Strong marketing skills are essential, as well as the ability to consider both traditional and nontraditional product lines.

Requirements:

  1. Five (5) or more years experience within either Onshore Energy, Construction, Multi-National or Global markets.
  2. Progress toward CPCU or similar professional qualification.
  3. Good communications skills, both oral and written.
  4. Strong computer skills. Software skills should include Microsoft Windows NT, Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Lotus 1-2-3, etc.

Treaty/Facultative Property Department

Transatlantic's Treaty/Facultative Property Department offers a full range of coverages from standard to complex, including reinsurance of Personal, Commercial, Excess and Surplus, Forced Order, Flood, and other Specialty classes - such as Onshore Energy and Nuclear risks. As a prominent property writer with large risk capacity, we can also provide national and regional catastrophic coverage in conjunction with our clients' risk business.

The Treaty/Facultative Property Department currently has an opening for an Underwriting Assistant.

Position: Underwriting Assistant

Requirements:

  1. Excellent computer skills. Software experience should include
    Microsoft Windows NT, Excel, Word, Paradox, and Lotus 1-2-3.
  2. Cat modeling-IRAS/CATMAP (preferred).
  3. Strong math background.
  4. Good communication skills - oral and written.
  5. Self motivated and highly organized.
  6. Some insurance experience.
  7. Reinsurance experience (preferred).
  8. Associates or bachelors college degree or equivalent coursework.
