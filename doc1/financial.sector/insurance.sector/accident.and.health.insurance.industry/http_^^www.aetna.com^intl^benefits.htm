Aetna International Group Benefits
A Business of Aetna International, Inc.
Through its two primary business lines, Aetna Bermuda and the Aetna/Generali Network, Aetna International Group Benefits provides international benefits plans to employee groups across the globe.

Aetna Bermuda
A direct subsidiary of Aetna International Inc., Aetna Bermuda provides portable group benefits plans for International Assignees (Expatriates and Third Country Nationals) and Key Local Nationals in hundreds of countries around the world.

The Aetna/Generali International Benefits Network
Aetna International Inc. and Assicurazioni Generali, the leading European insurer, work in cooperation to provide insurance plans (multinational pooling arrangements) for large multinational groups through more than fifty individual insurers, affiliates and correspondent companies throughout the world.
