American Annuity Group
At A Glance

The AMERICAN ANNUITY GROUP markets individual and group annuities nationwide to the savings and retirement markets through wholly-owned subsidiaries, including

  • Great American Life Insurance Company
  • Annuity Investors Life Insurance Company
  • Loyal American Life Insurance Company
  • Lifestyle Investments, Inc.
  • Retirement Resources Group, Inc.

Through American Memorial Life Insurance Company, and Purple Cross, the American Annuity Group markets individual life and annuity policies with the sponsorship of state associations of funeral directors as well as individual funeral directors and independent agents across the country.

Every day, more Americans realize the importance and urgency of building their retirement savings so they can maintain the lifestyle desired during their retirement years. Currently, there are approximately 55 million Americans aged 55 years and over, that is expected to grow to over 59 million by the year 2000. Based on a recent study revealing that four out of five Americans believe that pre-arranging a funeral is a good idea, it is not surprising that the number of funerals being pre-arranged is expected to grow from the current rate of one million per year to an estimated four million per year by the end of the century.
