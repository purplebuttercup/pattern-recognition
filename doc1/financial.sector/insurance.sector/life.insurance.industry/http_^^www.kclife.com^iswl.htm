Interest Sensitive Whole Life – Guarantees and More

Enjoying tomorrow what you plan today

Kansas City Life's Interest Sensitive Whole Life policy starts with the same guarantees as our traditional policy and builds from there. With this product, you'll earn a current competitive interest rate on your cash values in addition to having the security of guaranteed premiums, cash values and death benefits.

Interest Sensitive Whole Life provides the security you want today and the growth potential you need for tomorrow. Other features include:

  • An option that lets you put extra money in the policy
  • A wide variety of riders that allow you to customize the policy to meet your specific needs
  • An option to purchase additional paid-up coverage
  • Attractive loan privileges
  • An option to suspend premium payments in the future
  • For policies of $100,000 or more, extra interest earnings for keeping your policy in force*

*Not guaranteed in the contract
