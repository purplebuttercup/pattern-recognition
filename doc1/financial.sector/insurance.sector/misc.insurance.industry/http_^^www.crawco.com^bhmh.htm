Crawford's mental health/substance abuse managed care reviewers are psychiatric nurses and board-certified psychiatrists who can coordinate their specialized services with existing Employee Assistance Programs. They review and manage the growing number of mental and substance abuse treatment cases that account for almost 20 percent of today's medical cost.
  • Pre-Admission Review: Our registered nurses use objective criteria to assess the medical necessity of treatment. We also can develop alternative treatment plans using available resources.
  • Preferred Providers: We identify and evaluate mental health and substance abuse treatment providers and negotiate case-specific discounts.
