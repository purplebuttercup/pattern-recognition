E. W. Blanch Co., Inc. (EWBCo.) is the largest and fastest growing independent U.S. reinsurance intermediary.

EWBCo. is our core business on which the majority of EWB resources and energies are focused.

EWBCo.’s client bases consists of a diverse group of insurance companies located throughout the world and insurance companies that specialize in particular lines of insurance.

It is our goal to forge strong partnerships with clients and markets through targeted marketing, an aggressive and experienced sales force, a service-oriented approach and a strengthened reinsurer relationship.



Pioneered efforts to create "Alternative Distribution" possibilities for our clients.

Chosen as Lead Intermediary for the California Earthquake Authority.

Coordinated unprecedented reinsurance commitment of $1.8 billion.

Increased new account production from quality and highly visible entities (approximately 50 new accounts added in 1995).

Expanded our position and revenues with approximately 40 existing clients.

Led the effort to restore capacity to the distressed Florida property market, including the completion of the first 2 JUA de-population transactions.

Developed cutting-edge property exposure management programs for several major clients.

Successfully converted Florida self-insured workers compensation funds to insurance companies.

Enhanced capabilities in certain specialty lines, particularly D&O, E&O and Employment Practices Liability.
