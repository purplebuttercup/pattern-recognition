Liberty Financial Companies

Liberty Financial Companies (NYSE: L) is an integrated asset accumulation and management organization. It manages over $51.9 billion of assets on behalf of more than 1.5 million investors worldwide through an array of fixed, indexed, and variable annuities, private and institutional accounts, and 74 mutual funds. Its operating companies include Stein Roe & Farnham Incorporated, Keyport Life Insurance Company, Newport Pacific Management, Inc., Colonial Mutual Funds, Independent Financial Marketing Group, Inc. and Liberty Asset Management Company.
