Recent client inquiries reveal that when a person is out of work for 7 days or more, there's a 90% likelihood that her or she will become a long-term disability claimant. Clearly, the sooner you intervene, the better your chances for preventing an LTD claim...and the high costs that come with it.

Crawford's disability management services give you the greatest opportunity to control medical and lost-time costs by safely returning employees to work. We offer a full slate of medical and vocational resources that can be tailored to achieve these results:

  • Resolve claims faster and facilitate an earlier return to work.
  • Identify medical factors that prolong the disability period.
  • Improve employee moral and restore productivity.
