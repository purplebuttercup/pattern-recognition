INTERNAL AUDITOR. Responsibilities include performing audits of operational, corporate, and technical service areas, with the underlying purpose of enhancing the internal control environment, operating efficiency of the processing systems and overall enterprise (domestic/international) operating environment. Requirements include 4 year college degree and 2-4 years of audit experience, and/or experience with insurance/reinsurance broking operations. In addition, strong abilities in the analysis and evaluation of processing systems (financial, etc.) are a plus. Candidates should have the ability to travel, with the possibility of overseas travel.



REINSURANCE ANALYST TRAINEE. Trainees benefit from a formalized in-house training program learning the reinsurance profession from “the ground up”. After successful completion of the training program, trainees may be assigned to sales related positions in EWB’s MN office or in one of its regional offices (Chicago, New York, Boston, Philadelphia, Atlanta, Dallas, San Antonio or San Francisco).

Requirements include a 4-year college degree, excellent oral and written communication skills, strong PC skills, strong interpersonal skills, and proven leadership qualities. Must be very comfortable understanding/working with numbers and quantitative/financial concepts plus have strong problem solving skills. Excellent self-management skills -- must be able to successfully juggle several priorities and meet deadlines.


TECHNICAL ACCOUNT REPRESENTATIVE Responsibilities include handling communications between client companies and reinsurers, analyzing and verifying data received and collection and payment of accounts between parties. Requirements include a 4-year college degree, excellent communication skills, knowledge of PC’s, strong analytical/problem solving ability and detailed oriented.


CONTRACT WRITING ANALYST Responsibilities include translating raw material of a reinsurance transaction into a contract document. Requirements include a 4-year college degree, excellent analytical and computer skills and a high degree of competence in English language. Competence in Microsoft Access, Excel and Word required.


CATASTROPHE RISK ANALYST Responsibilities include intensive data analysis and modeling; preparing reports that effectively present analysis findings; and providing assistance to sales personnel, clients, and markets. Requirements include a 4-year college degree in Accounting, Finance, Math, Computer Science, Statistics or other similar analytically oriented major. Applicants must have PC experience using relational database (Access, dbase) and spreadsheet applications and possess strong oral and written communication skills. Familiarity with statistical programming languages would be a plus.


JUNIOR APPLICATIONS PROGRAMMER Responsibilities include developing program logic and system flow, coding programs primarily in Microsoft Visual Basic, writing SQL commands to access database, maintenance programming of existing systems, testing and debugging program, documenting programs and files to IS standards and working with users to determine needs at the program level. Requirements include a 4-year college degree (Computer Science emphasis preferred), good oral and written communication skills, knowledge of Microsoft Visual Basic language, MS SQL Server Database, Excel, Word and Access. PC-based programming experience preferred.
