Crawford's Beginnings provides screening, education and counseling to reduce health benefit expenses associated with pre-term births and Cesarean deliveries. Registered nurses with experience in maternity and home care provide the following services:
  • Patient Evaluation: In the first trimester, we will determine the existence of any risk factors.
  • Education/Counseling: We give mothers the latest information on risk factors and maternal health.
  • Care Coordination: Working with the patient and her physician, we coordinate service options to help ensure full-term births.
  • Toll-Free Phone Line: Our medical team is always available to address patient concerns.
