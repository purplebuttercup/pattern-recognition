Approximately 20% of medical cases account for 80% of medical expenditures. Crawford's Medical Case Management is designed to address these large claims by ensuring that appropriate, cost-effective care is provided – resulting in optimal outcomes for the payer, patient and family. Our case managers identify unnecessary treatments and recommend equally effective but less costly alternatives. They achieve measurable cost savings; in 1996, medical case management produced an average ROI of $15.30 to every $1 spent by our group health clients.
