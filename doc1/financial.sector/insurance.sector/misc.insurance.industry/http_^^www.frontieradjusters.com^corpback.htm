Frontier Adjusters of America, Inc. (American Stock Exchange: "FAJ") is a leading provider of claims adjusting services to insurance companies, self-insureds, municipalities, general agents, brokers, captive insurers, and others. Based in Phoenix, Arizona, Frontier’s operating company, Frontier Adjusters, Inc., has over 650 advertised locations in 50 states and Canada. Frontier offices are owned and operated by independent owner-operator franchisees or licensees, except that the Company owns and operates claims adjusting offices in Phoenix and Tucson, Arizona. Frontier’s owner-operators have an average of 21 years of multi-line claims experience and are affiliated with Frontier on an exclusive basis. Frontier supports its licensees and franchisees with customer referrals, advertising (including the Frontier Directory), marketing, promotion and technical support. Founded in 1957, Frontier has grown through its commitment to the principle that experienced professionals, who own and operate their own businesses, will provide superior quality service to clients requiring claims adjusting services.

Mission Statement

Frontier's mission is to provide high quality professional claims services to our clients at fair prices that will earn our owner-operators and our stockholders a fair profit.
