Liberty Financial Companies is an integrated, diversified asset management organization that provides a broad range of brand name financial products to help investors achieve their financial goals. Liberty Financial manages $48 billion in assets for over 1.5 million investors and policyholders worldwide across two major business lines: annuities - fixed, indexed and variable - and traditional asset management, including mutual funds, wealth management and institutional money management.
