OUR STRENGTHS ARE YOURS

Hilb, Rogal and Hamilton of the District of Columbia is part of a growing network of professional insurance companies across the United States. While each office is managed and staffed by local professionals, our clients benefit from the resources of a national brokerage firm. These services include loss control analysis, self-insured programs, risk management, as well as specialty agency programs and direct access to international markets. Since all of our agencies have various areas of specialty and expertise, the local offices share this knowledge and assist one another when necessary. These efforts are coordinated through our home office in Richmond, VA.

We represent over 25 companies, and we are aggressive in pursuing the best coverage and price from our markets on behalf of our customers. We have a staff of professionals who provide the day-to-day servicing of accounts such as processing changes in coverage and giving advice on various insurance issues. We also have a claims department to process and follow up on any losses that may occur.

For more than 75 years, we have grown because of our commitment to superior service. Our employees take pride in our company's achievements and share a dedication to continued standards of excellence. The ultimate beneficiary of this commitment is our client.
