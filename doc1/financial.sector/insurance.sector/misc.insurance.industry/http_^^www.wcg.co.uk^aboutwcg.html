Willis Corroon Group

Willis Corroon Group is one of the world's largest insurance and reinsurance brokers. It can trace its origins in the United Kingdom to the 1820s, and in the United States to 1905. Ever since those early years its goal has been the same--to meet clients' needs in the management of risk to the highest attainable standard.

The Group's head office is in London at Ten Trinity Square and, in the USA, the principal office is located in Nashville, Tennessee. There are Group offices in all the major continental European capitals. The Group is represented in 74 countries and some 260 offices by more than 11,000 staff.

In 1996 the Group handled gross premiums of some £7 billion, nearly $11 billion at the 1996 year-end rate of exchange. Operating revenue of £725 million ($1.24 billion) produced profits before tax of £91.6 million ($156.6 million).

The shares of Willis Corroon Group are listed on the London, New York and Pacific Stock Exchanges.
