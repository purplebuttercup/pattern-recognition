Allmerica Financial’s Manager of Managers program adds value to your benefit program by providing an additional layer of quality assurance and expertise at no extra cost to you.

Manager Evaluation Committee. The committee, comprised of Allmerica Financial senior officers and external investment professionals, combines extensive pension investment management expertise with impartial, third-party judgment for the review process.

Are employees too conservative with their 401(k)s?

Independent Pension Investment Consultant. RogersCasey Associates, Inc., a leading national independent consultant, conducts research on over 1,000 investment firms, analyzes performance and offers recommendations to assist the Manager Evaluation Committee in selecting quality money managers.

Plan Sponsor. Of course, you and your advisors help decide just which funds make it into your plan. As a result, you’re able to offer your employees an integrated investment program that helps achieve your specific plan objectives.

It saves you money:
  • Eliminates costs of internal investment staff or outside investment consultants.

It helps you meet your fiduciary responsibilities:
  • Functions as an impartial, third-party investment consultant.
  • Manages a diverse selection of investment funds.
  • Provides access to a diverse set of money managers.
  • Rigorously monitors and evaluates the plan.
  • Actively manages money managers and fund options.

It facilitates, communicates and educates:
  • Reduces investment administration.
  • Minimal disruption to the fund or plan services upon substitution of money managers.
  • Fund performance reports and financial newsletters to both plan sponsors  and participants.
