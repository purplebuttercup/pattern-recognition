LaSalle Re Holdings Limited is a Bermuda-based reinsurer with a contact office in London, U.K. Through our operating company, LaSalle Re Limited, we write high-severity, low frequency reinsurance, primarily property catastrophe reinsurance on a worldwide basis. While the main focus is assisting clients in managing their exposures to natural events such as windstorms and earthquakes, our book of business also includes other risks.

With assets exceeding $700 million, LaSalle Re is rated "A" (Excellent) by A.M. Best and "A" (Good) by Standard & Poor's.

 

LaSalle's mission is:

  • to protect insurance companies from losses caused by natural events, or from the financial consequences of man-made disasters
  • by assuming such risks, to introduce an important element of stability into insurance company balance sheets
  • to provide this support on a financially secure basis, underpinned by a strong capital commitment

Our goal is to provide reinsurance solutions tailored to the needs of our clients.
