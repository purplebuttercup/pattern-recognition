Frequently Asked Questions

LaSalle Re Holdings Stock

  • Where are your shares traded?
    LaSalle Re Holdings Limited Common and Series A Preferred Shares are traded on the New York Stock Exchange.

  • What is your ticker symbol?
    LSH for the Common Shares. LSHpfdA for the Series A Preferred Shares.

  • Can I buy or sell stock directly through LaSalle Re Holdings?
    LaSalle does not offer direct stock purchases or sales. All transactions should be handled through a stock brokerage firm of your choice.

    •  
    Dividends

  • What is LaSalle Re Holdings' dividend rate?
    Our quarterly dividend is currently $0.71 per share for Common Shares, and $0.5469 per share for Preferred Shares.

  • In what months are your dividends usually paid?
    Historically, dividends on Common Shares have been paid in the months of January, April, July and October. Our most recent dividend was paid on October 17, 1997, to shareholders of record October 3.

    Our Preferred Share dividends are paid on the working days closest to the first day of March, June, September and December of each year. Our most recent dividend was paid August 29 to shareholders of record July 28.

  • Will taxes be withheld from my dividend payments?
    For foreign shareholders, taxes will be withheld according to the treaty reached between their country of domicile and the United States. For US shareholders, tax is withheld from dividend cheques by the Transfer Agent, unless the shareholder sends a letter or W9 certification form to the agent, listing his tax ID number.

  • Does LaSalle Re Holdings offer dividend reinvestment?
    LaSalle Re Holdings does not offer a dividend reinvestment program.
