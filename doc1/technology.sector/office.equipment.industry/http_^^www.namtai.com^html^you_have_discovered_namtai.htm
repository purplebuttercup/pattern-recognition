Nam Tai has been involved in the dynamic digital electronics industry from its beginnings in the early 1970s, and has grown to become a major supplier of the electronic items that make our lives easier in the office and at home.

Nam Tai manufactures a range of digital products for business and personal use, as well as key components and subassemblies for some of the world's largest manufacturers of advanced electronics products.

Nam Tai was established in 1975 and listed its shares for trading on the NASDAQ National Market System in March 1988. It trades under the symbol 'NTAIF'.

Nam Tai's common shares trade on the Toronto Stock Exchange under the symbol 'NMT'.
