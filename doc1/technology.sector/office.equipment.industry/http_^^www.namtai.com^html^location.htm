NAM TAI FACTORY MODEL

Nam Tai was founded in 1975 and was a major participant in the sale of consumer electronics products in China through the late 1970s and 1980s. The Company also established manufacturing facilities in Hong Kong in 1978, and subsequently moved to Shenzhen, China in 1987 to take advantage of lower overhead costs and competitive labour rates. Since that time it has concentrated on the manufacture of electronics products for its OEM customers.

Nam Tai moved to its present facility of approximately 150,000 square feet from a nearby location in August 1992. In 1995, the decision was confirmed to expand the facility and a new manufacturing complex was completed in May 1996. This new facility is comprised of approximately 160,000 square feet of manufacturing space, a 40,000 square foot office building, as well as new dormitories, recreation and dining facilities.

During 1996, production was transferred to the new manufacturing complex from the existing factory. This is being followed by renovation of the older facility. As additional capabilities are required to meet future orders, new equipment and personnel will be added. When the new expanded factory complex is fully utilized, the workforce will have grown from the current level of 2,250 to approximately 4,000 employees.

The new factory complex includes land and services for further expansion of an incremental 150,000 square feet of manufacturing space. This additional facility will be attached to the new factory and office building. It is anticipated that this further expansion will be completed in 1998, based on market demand during 1997.
