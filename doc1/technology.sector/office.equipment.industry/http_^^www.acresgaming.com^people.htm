The best and brightest engineers and technicians from all over the country and world make up the Acres Gaming team: software and hardware engineers, marketing professionals, mathematicians, computer scientists, technical writers, graphic designers, and even gaming psychologists. We also have an infrastructure of manufacturing, accounting, and human resources staff. And we have a top-flight sales force.

Acres Gaming employees are encouraged, and rewarded, for innovative ideas and work. We offer flexible hours, top pay and benefits, and generous bonuses.

A great place to live and work

Corvallis and the Willamette Valley (that's Will-am-it) is a wonderful place to live. With one of the mildest climates in the U.S., outdoor activities are plentiful. The local area boasts 50 parks, the Pacific Ocean an hour away, and the Cascade Mountains within two hours! There's fun for the whole family: hiking, fishing, camping, boating, biking, and skiing.

While the landscape offers a wonderful playground, school-age children get a superior education in a school district outperforming the state and the nation in SAT scores, student-to-teacher ratios, and continuance to higher education.

Corvallis is a university town (Oregon State University) with the trappings of college life, and a center for many of today's leaders in computer technologies - there are over 100 high tech companies in the area.

Many of our employees commute from outlying areas including Eugene, home of the University of Oregon.

Portland, the state's largest city, is only an hour and a half away.
