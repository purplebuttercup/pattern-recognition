Corporate Profile

Nashua Corporation is a leading manufacturer and provider of coated products, office supplies and photo products and services that is expanding its presence around the globe. The company offers a diverse mix of high-quality products and services including facsimile and thermal papers, pressure-sensitive labels, specialty papers, copier and laser printer supplies, and mail-order photofinishing services.

Principal Business Units

Nashua Corporation is composed of the Photo Group and the Commercial Products Group. Nashua's Photo Group is the world's largest mail-order photofinisher, providing film processing services under the York, Scot, Belmont and Truprint brand names in the United States, Canada, England, Northern Ireland and the Republic of Ireland. The Commercial Products Group is composed of three divisions: Imaging Supplies, Specialty Coated Products and Label Products. The Imaging Supplies Division provides toners for copiers and high-volume printers, as well as remanufactured laser printer cartridges. The Specialty Coated Products Division offers a broad range of thermal, carbonless and other specialty papers. The Label Products Division provides labels for data processing, supermarket and industrial applications.
