Welcome to PAR Vision Systems

PAR Vision Systems Corporation, a wholly owned subsidiary of PAR Technology Corporation, has taken years of U.S. Government sponsored research to develop two very unique products, offering solutions in both the ophthalmologic and food processing markets.

PAR's Corneal Topography System - CTS(tm) measures the true topography and elevation of the cornea. PAR's patented CTS technology is a critical tool in excimer laser surgery because only the CTS can directly measure corneal shape changes made by the laser.

PAR's QSCAN automatically inspects packaged foods to detect and reject products contaminated by shards of glass, metal fragments and stones.
