Adaptive Products

 AOA / Xerox Low Vision Awareness Program: Learn about the low vision awareness program, an initiative between the American Optometric Association Low Vision Section and Xerox Adaptive Products

Book Wise: integrates sight, sound and speech to provide a creative and fun learning environment. BookWise is software that can be used with any reading material including textbooks, workbooks, magazines, and more.

Outlook: Outlook is a closed circuit television (CCTV) that magnifies documents or other items placed on its viewing table up to 25 times their original size. Outlook enables visually impaired people to read anything from canned food labels to maps. Using Outlook, even newspaper characters can be enlarged to appear up to 3-inches tall as they are displayed on a video monitor.

Reading AdvantEdge : Software that extends the power of the pc by turning it into a "reading machine". The Reading AdvantEdge OCR software recognizes a printed or typed character and can read aloud virtually any typeface or size, italics, bold, underscore, documents with multiple columns, draft dot matrix print, even faxes. The software can recognize and read six international languages in addition to English.

The Reading Edge : A stand alone, transportable, completely integrated reading machine that is ergonomically designed and competitively priced. The Reading Edge can read a wide variety of printed materials including books, magazines, photocopies and documents with multiple columns. The patented BookEdge scanning mechanism is optimally designed to scan and read bound books with speed and accuracy not achievable with flat bed scanning technologies.

Connectivity Enablers

The Xerox Connection (TXC): supplies and supports connectivity enablers for Xerox printers. TXC incorporates proven technology from outside vendors to allow customers to implement their Xerox printing solutions in the widest range of systems environments. Xerox validates and stands behind these enablers, even to the extent of offering the Total Satisfaction Guarantee.

Networks

8000 network system: Allows assembly of an integrated office network in which users can electronically create, process, file, print and distribute information.

Encryption Unit: Electronic encryption device that mathematically encodes computer signals so they may travel in top security on ordinary local-area networks. Endorsed by National Security Agency in 1990.

Ethernet local-area network: For connecting workstations, printers and other office equipment.

XNS PC Plus: Low-cost local area network provides basic printing, filing and message services among computer workstations.

Services/System Solutions

5100 Paginator: Automatically encodes copies made on the Xerox 5100 with identifying numbers and characters to allow easy reference to a particular page, crucial for many legal documents.

Document Access Remote Transfer: Hardware/software system allows corporate in-house printing departments, commercial printers and quick-print/copy shops to receive and produce digital file submissions of sophisticated documents from customers over telephone lines.

Documents Direct: High-speed, high-capacity computer and telecommunications service from Xerox Business Services provides the benefits of a dedicated global network without the massive investment of capital and resources to maintain such a network.

Documents on Demand: Production-oriented document management software simplifies the input, storage, customization and printing of such materials as college course packs, books and journals; transforms paper masters into digital documents that can be edited, indexed and stored for printing when needed on one of the Xerox Production Printers, including DocuTech and DocuPrint.

Document Services Platform (DocuSP): A software program for integrating printers, desktops, scanners and storage devices.

Remote Interactive Communications: Diagnostic system that transmits copier-performance data via telephone lines.

Sixth Sense Technology: Sixth Sense Technology: Free on-line service allows Xerox service engineers to monitor operating performance of 5090 and 5390 duplicators at customer sites. System also provides automatic billing, eliminating need for manual counter readings. For a Sixth Sense Video, call the Product Quality Hotline 1-800-642-4911.

Xerox Document and Data Services: Provides production services for the engineering industry and other customers with large, technical documents; XDDS specializes in transferring documents from one form to another, typically from paper and film to digital; XDDS technical staff can also edit and reformat documents.
