DocuShare: A community-maintained tool for posting and managing collections of information via corporate intranets. DocuShare places control over shared documents back into the users' hands, thereby eliminating the bottleneck and overhead of centralized system administration. DocuShare allows users to store, retrieve, and update documents in many file formats and enables cross-platform access to shared files from any PC, Mac, Unix or SGI computer with a web browser and intranet access. Users can create accounts, distribution lists, storage folders and control access rights. Best of all, these functions are available without the aide of a systems administrator or knowledge of HTML!
