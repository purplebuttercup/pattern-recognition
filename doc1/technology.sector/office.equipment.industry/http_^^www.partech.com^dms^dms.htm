Welcome to Document Management Systems


With the incredible proliferation of data in today's computer environment, the ability of consumers to manage information is questionable. PAR Document Management Systems (DMS), the newest division of PAR Technology Corporation, has developed software products from technology based on PAR's groundbreaking work for various government agencies, including the Central Intelligence Agency (CIA), National Security Agency (NSA) and the Defense Intelligence Agency (DIA), to aid in the analysis of vast and complex documents.

PAR's Document Management Systems is offering two innovative products. INSIGHT is a desktop text data manager, which allows an individual user to organize, categorize, search and review large quantities of textual information. HAWKEYE is a real-time text data classifier which evaluates incoming documents and categorizes them according to user-specified or automatically generated criteria.
