Welcome to PAR Government Systems Corporation

PAR Government Systems Corporation (PGSC) provides computer-based system design and development services to the U.S. government and other non-government customers. A leader in its field for more than two decades, PGSC has earned a reputation for producing high-quality results ranging from theoretical studies to proof-of-concept hardware and software systems for very diverse applications in many areas, including signal and image processing, environemental and GIS applications, C3I, and logistic management systems.

Welcome to PGSC's web page. Please browse our site and learn more about PGSC's technical capabilities and accomplishments, and about the company's organization and offices.
