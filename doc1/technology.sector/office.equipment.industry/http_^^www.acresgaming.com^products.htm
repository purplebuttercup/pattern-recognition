Utilizing the industry's most popular slot machines, Acres Bonusing Technology™ enables casino operators to customize and create unique, multi-sensory slot experiences that not only reward players with bonus payouts, but create dynamic entertainment spectacles on the floor.

Acres Bonusing Technology is designed, engineered, and manufactured to support any theme. From hurricanes, stampedes, and alien invasions to jungle, carnival, and futuristic environments...the only limitation is the imagination. In addition, Acres Bonusing Technology can be customized to promote all architectural themes and other special characteristics unique to any casino. What's more, the casino is in complete control of the bonus criteria, the duration of each event, the amount of the bonus, as well as when the event and associated bonusing will occur. The entire system ties in with the signage, adding yet a further dimension to the overall experience.

Incorporating Acres patent-pending Multiple Jackpot Time™ technology, The Hurricane Zone™ and Rodeo Grande™ are unparalleled slot environments where players periodically experience a thrilling hurricane or rodeo simulation, and enjoy a chance to win two-to-nine-times the bonus jackpot. Other promotions featuring Acres Gaming Bonusing Technology exist "off-the-shelf," and promotions can be customized to support any casino's unique architecture and design theme.

 

Blackout Poker™
A new bonusing video poker game
from Acres Gaming

Blackout Poker combines proprietary Acres Gaming hardware and Concept III™ software with a standard video poker game in a competitive environment to offer players more excitement and the opportunity to win up to three additional bonus jackpots.

Developed in cooperation with International Game Technology (IGT), Blackout Poker consists of five independent video poker machines linked to a common "blackout" jackpot display. Players compete to "black-out" applicable cards on a 52-card display common to all machines.

Three bonus jackpots can be won in addition to whatever is earned from the poker hand itself. The common display also appears on a large, overhead sign.

Game play information from each Blackout Poker machine is passed to a computer across Acres' proprietary Concept III communications protocol. In turn, the computer processes and displays progressive amounts and creates the "blackouts" of winning-hand cards on the two (face card and number card) displays. The game is accompanied by innovative signs, CD-quality sound effects, and lighting to create an exciting playing environment.
