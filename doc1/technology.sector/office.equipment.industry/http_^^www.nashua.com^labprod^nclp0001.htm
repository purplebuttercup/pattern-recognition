The Label Products Division produces and sells a range of labels used in data processing, information transfer and product identification applications. Nashua has long been recognized as a leader in the label business.

Our labels are sold primarily in retail and distribution markets. More than just a standard pricing sticker, many of today's labels, and the computer-generated data they carry (often in the form of bar codes), are used increasingly in complex, integrated information systems for routing, inventory control/replenishment, cross-docking distribution and product identification.
