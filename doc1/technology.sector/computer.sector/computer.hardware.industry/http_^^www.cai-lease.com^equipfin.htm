CAI offers unique and creative products designed to meet its customers' equipment acquisition needs. Some examples of the products we offer are:

Off-Balance Sheet Loan

  • Effective even when the equipment retains no end-of-term residual value.
  • Beneficial for sales tax purposes for the sale/ leaseback of used or depreciated equipment.
  • Allows for longer lease terms on assets expected to experience greater technological obsolescence.

The Early Termination Lease

  • For a lessee who is not certain of the desired length-of-term or equipment.
  • For a lessee who wants to fix early termination obligations, up-front.
  • A superior way to hedge technological obsolescence.

The Exchange Lease

  • For users of equipment that is subject to rapid technological change.
  • Works well for any asset or situation where equipment needs may change.
  • Provides certainty - with options - for users to better manage their leased equipment portfolio.

The Cancelable Lease

  • For equipment used under short-term cancelable contracts.
  • Where lessee is uncertain of lease term or equipment desired.
  • Can be used to create off-balance sheet strategies.
  • Can be used to lessen budgetary constraints.

As your financial adviser, we can find the product to fit any situation. We see your equipment acquisition requirement as an opportunity for an innovative solution.
