Hiking for
Boomer's Heroes
 

On March 22 1997, Joe Lustik begin an adventure of great magnitude. Joe, a marketing representative out of Pomeroy Computer Resources' Greater Cincinnati Branch, begin hiking the National Appalachian Scenic Trail. The trek covers 14 states and 2,155 miles of the eastern section of the United States. Starting in Georgia and ending in Maine, the journey will take about 6 months to complete!

Joe is dedicating his walk to fighting Cystic Fibrosis. As he completes his hike, Joe will correspond using his Compaq PC companion computer and share his journey via e-mail and US mail. You can share in his adventure too! Please join us in our commitment to help find a cure for Cystic Fibrosis by supporting Joe in his journey along the trail and making a pledge to help find a cure.
