Our CommTec division represents more than 230 years of experience in telecommunication network cabling design and installation; facilities maintenance and industrial engineering management; construction management and process and project management.

At Pomeroy Computer Resources, we help you achieve optimum system performance by providing you with the cable best suited to your specific application. As a leading technological innovator, we have a division of people ready to prepare you for a seamless entry onto the Information Super Highway. Our CommTec team collectively holds licenses, certifications, affiliations and degrees such as: Certified Netware Engineer (CNE), Registered Communications Distribution Designer (RCDD), Licensed Electrician, AT&T Systimax, Systems Analysis, Fred Prior Project Management, Electrical Engineering Technology, BICSI member. CommTec has the experience and a long list of satisfied customers to verify our excellence in providing cabling solutions from small projects to large site projects to national roll-outs. Whether your needs call for fiber optics or copper cabling, FDDI, Ethernet, or token ring, CommTec will provide turn-key design, project management, installation, testing, certification, support services, and outsourcing.
