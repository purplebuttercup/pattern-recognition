With today's rapid technology turnover, information systems professionals must determine how they will advance to the next platform every few years. With that in mind, buying and owning the technology has major drawbacks, including: software needing more speed, more memory, more disk space, higher resolution video, faster communications. Along with these technology drawbacks there are depreciation and tax issues which make leasing the financing option of choice in the micro-computer industry.

Since we are one of the largest computer integration companies in the country, we understand the challenges you face in balancing user needs and corporate budgets. Our leasing solutions were designed with those challenges in mind. We provide you with realistic options for terms, early termination clauses and upgrades so that as your needs evolve, so can yourr technology assets.

Our newest program was developed to provide you with even more flexibility regarding IT assets. The program enables you to "refresh" your technology assets in a timely and efficient manner by allowing you to designate three critical dimensions:

  • Lease Term (one year, two years or three years)

  • Upgrade Starting Point (anywhere inside the term)

  • Upgrade Percentage (1% to 5%) depending on your needs. Lets look at an example:

  • Lease Term: 3 Years

  • Upgrade Starting Point: 18 months

  • Upgrade Percentage: 3%

In this example, after 18 months you can begin to return 3% of your technology assets each month with no penalty. This means that you could "turnover" more than half of these assets before the lease term has expired! An earlier starting point would enable you to refresh an even higher percentage. You decide! This program is simply an example of the flexibility that lease financing with Pomeroy Computer Resources can help stretch your IT budget while providing you with maximum user benefit.
