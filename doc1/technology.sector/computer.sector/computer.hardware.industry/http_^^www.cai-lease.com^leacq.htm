Capital Associates is an active buyer of quality equipment financing transactions. With a focus on growth, we have a consistent appetite for lease financing transactions. Call us for your equipment financing needs.

BUYING PARAMETERS

Credits:
Middle market and above. A cash flow and collateral approach is used to analyze credits. Lessee net worth $10 million.

Equipment:
Hard assets are preferred, but all assets types will be considered. Machine tools, production equipment, manufacturing, materials handling and FF&E are emphasized. Will not acquire aircraft or mainframes.

Equipment Location:
United States, Canada and Mexico

Transaction Size:
Individual transactions $250,000 to $10,000,000. Portfolios of any size will be considered up to $50 million.

Lease Rates:
Commensurate with risk

Lease Terms:
Minimum two years. Maximum seven years. Will accept new or aged portfolios.

Structures:
Individual transactions or portfolio of leases. FMV preferred but not required. Leveraged or single investor transactions are acceptable. Seller documentation, upon review, can be accepted.
