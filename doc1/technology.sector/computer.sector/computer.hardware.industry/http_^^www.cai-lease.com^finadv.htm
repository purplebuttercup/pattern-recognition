The CAI Financial Advisory Services Group provides value-added income tax, legal and accounting advisory services to clients involved in equipment financing activities.

Service Objectives

Most equipment leasing and finance companies' services are limited to providing equipment financing quotes. We provide you with a comprehensive equipment financing package - competitive, low-cost financing and assistance in evaluating your specific financial and strategic objectives.

Value-Added Benefits for Our Clients

We will assist you in structuring your financing transactions in order to obtain the lowest income tax cost and to obtain your desired financial statement presentation.

Financial Advisory Services Group

The Financial Advisory Services Group consists of dedicated professionals with combined experience exceeding forty years in the equipment leasing and consulting industries. The Group is backed by the resources of a full-service equipment leasing company.

INCOME TAX AND ACCOUNTING STRUCTURING SERVICES

The income tax, legal and accounting issues relating to equipment financing transactions are complex and subject to frequent change. Your needs and objectives require that you have the assistance of experts well-versed in such issues. Your general business advisers do not have the capabilities to provide the innovative solutions and value-added services that you require.

Income Tax Structuring: We work with your legal and tax advisers to structure financing options suitable for your income tax situation. Our goal is to provide practical solutions with tax efficient strategies that respond to your business environment. Equipment financing involves tax provisions that may cause very different results depending upon the transaction's structure. Our mission is to develop the highest benefit/least cost structuring options.

Accounting Structuring: The proper accounting structure will assist you in meeting many corporate goals including compliance with your debt covenants. The accounting rules relating to structured financial transactions change frequently. We have the expertise to assist you in keeping abreast of the latest developments.
