DIGITAL at a Glance
 
Digital Equipment Corporation is the leading worldwide supplier of networked computer systems, software, and services. DIGITAL pioneered and leads the industry in interactive, distributed and multivendor computing. An international company, DIGITAL does more than half its business outside the United States, developing and manufacturing products and providing customer services in the Americas, Europe, Asia, and the Pacific Rim.

DIGITAL partners with world-class researchers and strategic customers around the globe on carefully selected research projects and new technologies. DIGITAL 40th Anniversary
 
August '97 marks 40 years of innovation and customer success at DIGITAL. We are proud of our long history of technology leadership and our contributions to the growth of the computer industry. From pioneering the PDP-1 in 1960 to the latest Alpha systems, DIGITAL has played a significant role in defining interactive computing.
