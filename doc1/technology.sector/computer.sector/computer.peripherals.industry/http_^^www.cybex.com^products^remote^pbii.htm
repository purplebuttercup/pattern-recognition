Power up your computer remotely from your touch-tone telephone or Hayes-compatible modem!

Leaving your computer on every night exposes your system to the hazards of lightning, power surges and hackers. With PhoneBoot II you can turn on your computer from home when you're ready to work. Once you hang up the line, your computer can issue or receive calls or perform shut-down routines before PhoneBoot II automatically turns the system off.

Password Security

PhoneBoot II features an enhanced four-digit access code to provide better protection from hackers and accidental booting.

 

 

When you call PhoneBoot II the system will power-up only after you enter the correct code. If you don't need security, just flip the switch on the front panel to put PhoneBoot II into ring mode. Your computer powers up automatically after the number of rings you select.

User Configurable

You set the pace! Along with the ring count, PhoneBoot II lets you decide how long your computer stays on after you hang up. As shipped the system allows for an additional 18 minutes before automatically shutting down the computer again.

Local Control

A front panel switch lets you control how you operate your system. In the LOCAL ON position, Phone Boot II performs as a power switch for daytime use. The REMOTE ENABLE position lets you power your computer remotely through the telephone line.

Two versions of the PhoneBoot II are available: domestic and international. The domestic version includes a 6-foot power cord for use with grounded outlets.
