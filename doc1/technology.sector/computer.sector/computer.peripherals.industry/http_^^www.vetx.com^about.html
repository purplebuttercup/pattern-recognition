About
Vertex Industries, Inc.

Vertex Industries, Inc. is based in Clifton, New Jersey. Vertex sells its products directly, as well as via a network of value added resellers and strategic business partners.

A publicly held company, Vertex common shares are traded on NASDAQ Small CAP Securities under the symbol VETX and the Boston Stock Exchange under the symbol VER.
