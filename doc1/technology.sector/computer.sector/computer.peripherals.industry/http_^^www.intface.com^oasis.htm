Oasis Family Products

Oasis brings together our extensive mainframe connectivity skills with our host-to-desktop printing knowledge.

Oasis is a suite of document delivery solutions that allows host-based documents -- both AFP/IPDS and SCS -- to be printed, viewed, archived or faxed from virtually any desktop in your company. Oasis is the most convenient and affordable way to distribute host documents throughout your enterprise.

Printing/Viewing

Oasis SERVer lets you print any mainframe or AS/400 host document -- AFP/IPDS or SCS (including transparent mode data) -- to any LAN-attached PCL or IPDS printer. It even supports printing to ASCII dot matrix printers such as the Proprinter. If you wish to view documents before printing them, SERVer lets you output high resolution images that are faithful reproductions of the original host documents.

Archiving

Oasis ARCHIVer provides the means to archive host documents to a laser disk server or to CD-ROM for easy distribution. Since many legacy applications can only print in large batch runs, getting one document is often difficult. With the high-speed search capabilities of Oasis ARCHIVer, you can typically find the records you need within 1-2 seconds.

Faxing

Oasis FAXer is an automated FAX delivery system that allows you to send FAXes from your mainframe or AS/400® without intermediate steps or labor. FAXer can be scaled to support from a few faxes to over 2,000 per hour.

Turnkey Solution

Interface Systems now offers you a total turnkey solution to virtually eliminate the hassle of installing a distributed printing network. Our Microsoft Certified Engineers will custom build a system to your specifications including hardware, software, installation, training and support. All of our work and products are guaranteed and you only have one point of contact to get any problems taken care of.
