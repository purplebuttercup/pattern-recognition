Sales Offices

Interface Systems' products are the preferred choice of hundreds of thousands of satisfied customers across six continents. This includes many of the leading retail, financial services, manufacturing, communications, healthcare, and government.

Interface has sales offices, or representatives, on six continents. Corporate headquarters and the North American sales operation are located in Ann Arbor, Michigan with direct sales offices across the United States. International sales are headquartered in Slough, England, just outside London. In addition there are direct sales offices in Paris (France) and in Johnnesburg (S.A.).
