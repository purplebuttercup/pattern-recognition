Adjustable Wrist Rest

With the increased use of computers and word processors, repetitive motion injuries have become common in the office. Carpal Tunnel Syndrome (CTS) is a repetitive motion injury frequently associated with typing or data input. CTS can cause pain and numbness in the hands and, in advanced cases, can require surgery in order to be corrected.

The adjustable Wrist Rest by PerfectData helps the user avoid CTS by properly supporting the hands and wrists while typing.

Features:

  • Adjustable height - Can be set to a height which is right for your specific needs
  • Adjustable width - Fits keyboards up to 23" wide
  • Attaches to keyboard - Includes brackets with Velcro fasteners to attach Wrist Rest to keyboard and rubberized feet to keep the Wrist Rest firmly in place
  • Cushioned fabric - Covered with a soft cushioned fabric that has no sharp edges to irritate hands or wrist during use
