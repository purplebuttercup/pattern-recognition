Cybex Computer Products Corporation manufactures and markets products for computers that boost, split or switch the signals between the central processing unit (CPU) and the keyboard, video monitor and mouse (KVM). The Company's KVM switch, extension and expansion products are designed to work on IBM and compatible computers, Apple Macintosh, Sun, and certain Hewlett-Packard, IBM and Silicon Graphics workstations.

The company's KVM switch products are used on network computers and allow the user to control multiple computers with a single keyboard, video monitor and mouse. Cybex's proprietary AutoBoot Commander™ 4xP line of switch products enable up to four users to control as many as 2,160 computers. The switch products increase user productivity while providing substantial savings in reduced computer components and energy costs.

Cybex's KVM extension products allow the computer's CPU to be separated from the keyboard, video monitor and mouse by up to 600 feet. The company's KVM extension products are used to increase space for the user by moving the CPU to a remote location, provide increased security by moving the CPU off the user's desk to a secure location, and are used to remove the CPU from hazardous locations, such as plant floors, to a more protected environment.

The company also produces KVM expansion products that allow multiple keyboards, monitors and mice to be connected to a single computer. Application for KVM expansion products include classrooms where the teacher and/or students can view the other's work, and in applications where the user needs access to the computer from multiple locations. Cybex's KVM expansion products do not require special network software or multiple software licenses since the system is designed to work off of a single CPU.
