CopyHolders Systems

PerfectData CopyHolder Systems relieve eye, back and neck strain while increasing your productivity by putting your copy at the most comfortable height and angle for you.

The product line includes six different copyholder models and four unique accessories. Each model is made up of a ocpyboard and a supporting base. All bases and accessories attach to the copyboard by means of a "snap-on/off" system. No tools are required for assembly. Allows you to create the system you need!
