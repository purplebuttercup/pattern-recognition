Computer Research, Inc. (CRI) has been an active and innovative part of the financial services industry since 1963, serving hundreds of clients with our various accounting systems. We have developed long term client relationships based on a combination of technical know-how, quality service and competitive pricing. Since 1982, we have annually serviced over 100 financial service clients nationwide. CRI understands that today's financial industry is constantly reshaping through mergers, acquisitions and constant regulatory changes. These changes require bank investment division management to re-evaluate their current security processing systems in an effort to maintain continuity in operations, support a complex and diverse investment product line and to reduce overall operating costs. CRI has kept pace with this rapidly changing environment with its family of investment software applications. These applications are specifically designed for the banking environment of the 1990's to allow the user to enter the twenty-first century as a leader in banking security services. CRI's BANK-BOA Systems are unique in the data processing industry and are readily adaptable to any banking environment. They can be utilized independently of each other or linked together for a fully integrated application. Sensitive to customer needs and extremely user friendly, they are quickly and smoothly installed with on-going assistance from the CRI support group.
