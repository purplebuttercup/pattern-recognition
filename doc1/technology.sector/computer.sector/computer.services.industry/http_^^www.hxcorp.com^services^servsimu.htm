Simulation Systems Services

Halifax provides total system life cycle support for high-performance simulation hardware, software, networks and related systems. Our integrated systems approach means a single point of accountability and helps ensure the successful implementation and long-term support of your simulation project.
We bring decades of engineering, integration and simulation systems expertise to every project we undertake. Our experience includes:
  • Integrating hardware and software manufactured by various OEMs into a seamless integrated environment
  • Operation, maintenance, and training support for a wide variety of simulators including flight, combat, and instructional systems
  • Design, modification, and upgrade services for a wide variety of simulators or components of the simulation systems
  • Operation and maintenance for all computers and networks utilized in the simulation environment
  • Development, operation, integration, and maintenance services for simulators used in a wide range of applications including the automotive and transportation, electrical and nuclear, and chemical processing industries
Experienced and Innovative Simulation Personnel
Halifax personnel have been providing simulation solutions since the industry was in its infancy. Our involvement spans the evolution of simulation systems from large computer mainframes to today's high-speed workstations and Pentium-based PCs. This wealth of first-hand experience, taken together with our impressive record of innovation, makes Halifax the ideal partner for simulation projects both large and small.

High-Speed Digital Network Expertise
In today's distributed simulation environment, network expertise is a must. More and more simulation applications require connections with distant geographic locations so that remote users can participate in the simulation.

Halifax has a wealth of experience in this area, most notably with Distributed Interactive Simulation (DIS) networks. Our DIS network services include:

  • Network Consulting
  • DIS Setup and Configuration
  • Operation and Maintenance
  • Complete Network Administration
Full Spectrum of Simulation Services
At Halifax, we have the right blend of personnel and services to meet all your simulation solution requirements. Our comprehensive single-source services include:
  • Layout of the physical facility
  • Selection of hardware and visual systems software
  • Benchmark and analysis of hardware and software to determine best fit
  • Integration of all components into a complete working system
  • Maintenance and operation support
With personnel ranging from Ph.D.'s with specialized simulation expertise to hardware and software engineers we can manage your next project from initial planning through complete operations and management services.
