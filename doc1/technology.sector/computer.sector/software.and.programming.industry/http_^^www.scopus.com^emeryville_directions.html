How To Get To Scopus

Scopus World Headquarters

Highway map, courtesy Haas Business School, UC- Berkeley

Customized driving directions within Bay Area

From San Francisco International Airport
  • Take Highway 101 North
  • Watch for Bay Bridge signs (these appear about 10 miles north of airport)
  • Stay in right lanes
  • Take 80 East over Bay Bridge
  • After bridge stay in left two lanes
  • Follow left branch of highway. This remains 80 East. (Signs indicate Sacramento/Berkeley as destination.)
  • After the curve, move toward rightmost lane for next exit.
  • Exit at Powell Street.
  • Take left at light onto Powell Street (westbound).
  • Pass Shell service station and Holiday Inn.
  • Make right into Watergate Towers campus.
  • Take first left into multi-level covered parking garage.
From Oakland Airport
  • Exit from airport onto Hegenberger Road.
  • Watch for signs to 880 North to San Francisco/Downtown Oakland.
  • Take 880 North.
  • 880 will merge into 980 (right lanes).
  • Once on 980, move to the right lanes.
  • Watch for San Francisco-bound (580 West) exit ramp.
  • On ramp, remain in either of 2 left lanes.
  • You will be merging onto highway 580 West. Be prepared to move to the right lane. (Observe traffic merging leftward across your lane.)
  • Remain in rightmost lane.
  • You will merge onto 80 East.
  • Move into right lane. Prepare to exit.
  • Exit at Powell Street.
  • Take left at light onto Powell Street (westbound).
  • Pass Shell service station and Holiday Inn.
  • Make right into Watergate Towers campus.
  • Take first left into multi-level covered parking garage.
From San Francisco
  • Take 80 East over Bay Bridge
  • After bridge stay in left two lanes
  • Follow left branch of highway. This remains 80 East. (Signs indicate Sacramento/Berkeley as destination.)
  • After the curve, move toward rightmost lane for next exit.
  • Exit at Powell Street.
  • Take left at light onto Powell Street (westbound).
  • Pass Shell service station and Holiday Inn.
  • Make right into Watergate Towers campus.
  • Take first left into multi-level covered parking garage.
From Sacramento, Fairfield, Vallejo
  • Take highway 80 West toward Berkeley.
  • Exit at Ashby Avenue.
  • Take left onto Frontage Road southbound.
  • At Shell Service station, take right turn onto Powell Street.
  • Pass Shell service station and Holiday Inn.
  • Make right into Watergate Towers campus.
  • Take first left into multi-level covered parking garage.
From Contra Costa County (Walnut Creek, Concord)
  • Take 24 West (toward San Francisco)
  • Exit onto 580 West (San Francisco bound) by staying in the right lane of exit ramp .
  • Once on highway 580 West, remain in rightmost lane.
  • You will merge onto 80 East.
  • Once you're on 80, remain in either of the two rightmost lanes for the next exit.
  • Exit at Powell Street.
  • Take left at light onto Powell Street (westbound).
  • Pass Shell service station and Holiday Inn.
  • Make right into Watergate Towers campus.
  • Take first left into multi-level covered parking garage.
From San Jose, Silicon Valley, Fremont, Hayward
  • Take 880 North. (In some parts of San Jose and beyond, this same road is designated as highway 17).
  • 880 will merge into 980 (right lanes).
  • Once on 980, move to the right lanes.
  • Watch for San Francisco-bound (580 West) exit ramp.
  • On ramp, remain in either of 2 left lanes.
  • You will be merging onto highway 580 West. Be prepared to move to the right lane. (Observe traffic merging leftward across your lane.)
  • Remain in rightmost lane.
  • You will merge onto 80 East.
  • Move into right lane. Prepare to exit.
  • Exit at Powell Street.
  • Take left at light onto Powell Street (westbound).
  • Pass Shell service station and Holiday Inn.
  • Make right into Watergate Towers campus.
  • Take first left into multi-level covered parking garage.
From Marin County (San Rafael, Sausalito)
  • Take Highway 101 (north or south toward Richmond-San Rafael Bridge)
  • Take Richmond-San Rafael Bridge (highway 580 East)
  • Highway 580 merges with Highway 80 West in Albany, CA.
  • By the time you pass University Avenue, get into right lane.
  • Exit at Ashby Avenue.
  • Take left onto Frontage Road southbound.
  • At Shell Service station, take right turn onto Powell Street.
  • Pass Shell service station and Holiday Inn.
  • Make right into Watergate Towers campus.
  • Take first left into multi-level covered parking garage.
