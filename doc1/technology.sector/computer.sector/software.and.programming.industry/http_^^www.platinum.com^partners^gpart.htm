Our Growth is Your Opportunity
PLATINUM technology, inc., has become the premier vendor of distributed computing solutions for every major IT discipline. PLATINUM offers over 100 software solutions in the areas of Systems Management, Application Lifecycle, Data Warehousing, Database Management, and Year 2000. Annual revenues have skyrocketed to nearly $500 million in 1997.
 
PLATINUM provides customers with comprehensive solutions that integrate their diverse computing platforms, client/server networks, and databases. By joining forces with select third parties, we plan to capitalize on combined competencies in development, sales, marketing, and support and to realize our vision of a complete open enterprise solution.

Select the Individual Program that Matches Your Business
To maximize the benefits for everyone concerned, PLATINUM tailors its partnerships to match the unique characteristics, talents, and requirements of each partner. Here is a brief summary of the primary arrangements we offer. If the relationship you would like to pursue is not described here, we would be happy to discuss alternatives with you.
 
Consulting Partners Program is intended for organizations that focus on consulting and education services related to PLATINUM products and associated technology.
 
Integration Partners Program promotes the use of PLATINUM products for database design and tuning, application development and testing , systems project management, and Internet development. Targets global systems integrators, large consulting firms, and regional and application integrators who use PLATINUM products in conjunction with their value-added integration services.
 
Technology Partners Program enables software development firms to use PLATINUM products to design, build, or enhance their own products in specific vertical markets.
 
Year 2000 Partners Initiative enables global systems integrators, regional consulting firms, and off shore development firms to work jointly with PLATINUM in providing Year 2000 impact assessment, project management, and application conversion services to PLATINUM clients.
 
Business Partners Program is for national corporate resellers and distributors who provide ongoing fulfillment sales and support for certain PLATINUM products.
 
Strategic Partners Program is for information technology leaders. The resources to be committed by each party, including marketing efforts, distribution elements, PLATINUM development lab relationships, and others, are negotiated on an individual basis. Some examples of Strategic Partnerships include: preinstalling PLATINUM software on various hardware platforms; co-selling and co-marketing; joint development initiatives, and incorporating PLATINUM products into Strategic Partner's products.
