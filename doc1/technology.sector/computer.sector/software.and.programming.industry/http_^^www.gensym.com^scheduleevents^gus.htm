The Gensym Users Society (GUS)

GUS was formed in 1988 to provide opportunites for technical communication between Gensym customers and Gensym Corporation, and to promote the exchange of ideas among Gensym customers worldwide. A major meeting is held each year and smaller regional meetings are arranged periodically. The next worldwide Gensym Users Society meeting will be held in Paris.
