Tripos Software Consulting Services 

Partnering with you and others to provide the services you require 

We hear often from our clients that they do not wish to be dependent upon a single vendor to provide critical applications and services.  We understand that -- it's not a position that Tripos wants to be in, either.  At the same time, both vendors and clients seek to enter into close, but non-exclusive partnerships. This is why Tripos has demonstrated our willingness and success in working with other vendors, in order to provide our clients with superior service.  

ORACLE:  An Alliance partner of ORACLE, Tripos develops applications to work with ORACLE RDB systems. We are working jointly with ORACLE to design and build Scientific Information systems for multiple pharmaceutical clients. 

SGI: A strategic partner of Silicon Graphics for several years, we have considerable experience working with SGI hardware and staff, worldwide. We utilize SGI Challenge and Origin servers for Collaborative Network Computing Solutions in many client sites, as well as our own corporate network. We are an active value-added reseller of SGI systems. 

MDL: We've been working with MDL, the industry leader in Chemical Information Systems, in order to provide better integration of MDL products with our own Discovery Systems.  Last year we shipped the MTS (MDL translation system) to our many Unity customers, linking their corporate chemical databases with the advanced Unity 3D searching capabilities. We've also designed several of our Web-based clients so that they can access ISIS directly, for those users with ISIS licenses. We are also working jointly with MDL to provide ChemInformatics systems to some major Pharmaceutical companies. 

Netscape: An AffiliatePlus partner of Netscape, we develop solutions using Netscape servers and clients, and resell Netscape software. The Tripos corporate intranet is built on the Netscape Enterprise Server and Communicator clients.
