Medic Computer Systems, Inc. develops, markets and supports integrated practice management systems, clinical information systems, and related services to Managed Care Organizations, (MCOs), Management Services Organizations(MSOs), Integrated Delivery Networks (IDNs) and physician practices.

Medic’s products are designed to manage financial, administrative, clinical and managed care information. Currently, Medic supports more than 10,000 medical practices, representing over 50,000 physicians in 49 states.
