The Cerner Advantage

Cerner is a world-class organization recognized as one of the best small companies in America. Cerner doesn't have employees --we have associates. We don't work for each other--we join together to accomplish common goals. Cerner associates are empowered to make decisions crucial to the company, rather than waste time and resources wading through a hierarchical "chain of command." The company continues to experience growth, which keeps the environment dynamic and provides ample opportunities for advancement. And, you will never find a time clock at any Cerner location--counting the hours doesn't help us stay on top of the healthcare information industry. Cerner stays on the leading-edge of technology, serving as alpha and beta test site for many new industry-wide tech tools.

At Cerner, associates are our #1 resource. We believe in a total investment in each associate's life, offering benefits such as an on-site athletic club and "parent-friendly" day care center that you won't find at other companies. Add in the educational opportunities from Cerner Virtual University, some of the best investment and healthcare benefits available, and you begin to understand the Cerner Advantage.

Check us out and see why Cerner is the place for your future!
