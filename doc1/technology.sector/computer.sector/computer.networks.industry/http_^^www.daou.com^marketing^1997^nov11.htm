DAOU Names Noreen Regional VP for Southeast
    SAN DIEGO, Nov. 11,1997 – DAOU Systems, Inc. (Nasdaq:DAOU) has appointed Ronald Noreen as Regional Vice President for the Southeast in a move to strengthen the company’s presence in that area of the United States as well as to develop additional business opportunities.

     In his new capacity, Noreen will be responsible for building DAOU’s marketshare in the Southeast, expanding the company’s relationships with healthcare organizations, and delivering a suite of networking services to customers. He will be based out of DAOU’s Atlanta office.

    Noreen comes to DAOU with 18 years experience in technology sales and management. His most recent positions included Vice President of Operations for HBOC’s Connect Technology Group, and Healthcare Consulting Manager for Sun Data, Inc.

    “Ron has been an outstanding performer for some major healthcare information technology firms,” says Robert McNeill, DAOU Executive Vice President and Chief Operating Officer. “We believe that Ron’s leadership and customer service expertise will help us realize the full potential of the Southeast market.”

    Noreen holds a bachelor’s degree in business administration from Birmingham Southern College.

    Founded in 1987, DAOU Systems, Inc., designs, implements, and manages computer networks for the healthcare industry. The company offers solutions for voice, video and data networking, combining its technological expertise with its knowledge of the healthcare industry’s specialized needs. In addition, DAOU offers an array of operational and Internet solutions. DAOU’s clients comprise more than half of the nation’s top 50 integrated healthcare delivery networks and include such organizations as Centura Health, North Shore Health System, Mercy Health Services, Harris Methodist Health System, and Catholic Medical Center of Brooklyn and Queens.

    DAOU operates five regional offices and five operations centers across the country. The firm employs a staff of over 350 and has provided services to more than 600 organizations throughout the U.S.
