DAOU Appoints Gary Anthony Vice President of Southwest Region
    SAN DIEGO, July 17,1997 – As part of a strategy to strengthen its presence in key markets throughout the United States, DAOU Systems has appointed Gary Anthony Regional Vice President of the Southwest. In his new capacity, Anthony will establish a new regional office for DAOU in Dallas, and will oversee a staff of sales executives and network engineers. His team will be responsible for obtaining new clients, providing customer service to current clients, and managing the delivery of DAOU’s network design, implementation and management services to healthcare organizations throughout the Southwest.

    "We’re pleased to have Gary as a member of the DAOU team,” says Robert McNeill, Executive Vice President and Chief Operating Officer for DAOU Systems. “We view the Southwest region as a robust market with some truly world-class healthcare organizations. Gary’s expertise in both healthcare and information technology will help our firm establish a successful presence in this important region.”

    Anthony comes to DAOU with more than 17 years experience in healthcare information technology, previously serving as Western Regional Vice President for Oacis Healthcare Systems, as well as a sales executive for WSI, Inc., and Unisys Corp.

    He holds a bachelor’s degree in business administration from Missouri Southern University.

    Founded in 1987, DAOU Systems, Inc. designs, implements, and manages computer networks and desktop operations for the healthcare industry. The company also offers outsourcing, Internet, and network monitoring solutions. DAOU has provided network services to more than 450 hospitals and healthcare facilities nationwide. Headquartered in San Diego, the company has 6 regional offices and 230 employees. DAOU’s clients include such organizations as Centura Health, Lutheran Health Systems, Mercy Health Services, Harris Methodist Health System, and Catholic Medical Center of Brooklyn and Queens.
