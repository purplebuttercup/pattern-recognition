Cerner Knowledge Network
Available now for all Cerner clients. Contact your Cerner representative for details.
The Cerner Knowledge Network (CKN) is a web-based knowledge repository providing Cerner clients with the latest information available concerning Cerner products. CKN is a value added information source to assist Cerner clients is gaining maxium benefit from Cerner's software products.

CKN includes:

  • Cerner User Group -- Join the community of Cerner users in our Regional and Special Interest Groups.
  • Product Information -- Documentation, FAQs, PIMs, Rev-level updates and other detailed information about Cerner products.
  • The Cerner Knowledge Exchange (CKE) -- a web-based, threaded discussion area where clients can share files and best practices easily.
  • The Cerner Storefront -- Purchase equipment and supplies from Cerner Direct or visit our own wing of Amazon.com.
  • More services added weekly, and feedback is encouraged--tell us what you would like to see!
