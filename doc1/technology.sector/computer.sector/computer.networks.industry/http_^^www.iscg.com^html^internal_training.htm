      Every ISCG consultant develops an annual training plan with their manager that includes day and evening classes.  Consultants are encouraged to take several classes throughout the year to stay current with the rapidly changing market.  These classes support the application development and network management services groups of ISCG and are taught by external vendors and ISCG consultants.
      ISCG also offers several intensive extended training programs for new and current consultants.  In addition, consultants can attend daytime training, attend ISCG Evening School classes, participate in Special Interest Groups, and perform self-directed training.  As an ISCG consultant you have unbounded training opportunity starting from your first day of employment.  The only limit is in the amount of time and the dedication you have for learning.
