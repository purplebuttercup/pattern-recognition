      ISCG recognizes the dynamic nature of the computer technology industry and as a result has a strong commitment to training.  ISCG invests significantly in the education of its consulting staff through an extensive internal training program.  Our focus on training programs for consultants has generated opportunities for us to offer training to our clients through public training in several specialized technical areas.
      To support our training programs, we have three classrooms in our Wayne, Pennsylvania office, each with one PC per student, connectivity to UNIX, Windows NT, and OpenVMS servers, and full audiovisual capabilities.  Our Somerset, New Jersey office is similarly equipped with a state-of-the-art, hands-on training facility.

      All training at ISCG is provided or coordinated by the Education Services department.  Education Services strives to provide a rich learning environment for our clients.  The Education Services staff develop course materials, teach classes, and work with third-party vendors to provide the training necessary for ISCG consultants as well as our clients.
