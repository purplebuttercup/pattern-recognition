Inside the Read-Rite Head: Picture of Read-Rite's assembling processes



Wafer fabrication processes within Read-Rite's Fremont facility create thin film head sliders are transformed by subsequent testing, machine and assembly steps into recording head gimbal and head stack assemblies used to transfer data in hard disk drives.
