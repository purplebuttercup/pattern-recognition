nStor STOCK TRADING ON AMEX 

Effective April 18, 1997 nStor Technologies stock began trading on the American Stock Exchange. We ceased trading on the OTC exchange around 10:30 am in the morning and began trading on AMEX under the stock symbol of "NSO". 

This event marked a momentous occasion for nStor. The move from the OTC exchange to AMEX greatly benefits all of our shareholders, aid in the development of new markets and add to our list of significant accomplishments. Growth in the RAID and storage subsystem markets is imperative to survival, this move benefits our objectives.

Dan Smith
President, nStor Corporation
