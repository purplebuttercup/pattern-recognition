Summer College Programs

Join us in developing your career through a hands-on paid industry experience. Get to know us, attend training sessions, and learn more about our technology. Take advantage of our openings which are typically for engineers in thin film technology involving, masking, photolithography, sputtering, plating & etch, an related manufacturing areas. If your one year away from graduating, and are seeking an engineering opportunity in thin film technology area, send us your resume. Resumes are accumulated prior to March-April, and reviewed against summer openings for the following summer. Students are then contacted where a meaningful match exist, and invited to consider summer internship. Interested students are invited to send us their resume, or fax to 408-956-3212;
