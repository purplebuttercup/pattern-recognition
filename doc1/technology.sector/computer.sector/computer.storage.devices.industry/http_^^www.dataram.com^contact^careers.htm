Advance Your Career
Join Dataram’s Team Today!

If you are looking for a great career opportunity, the name you need to know is Dataram! We are a worldwide leader in providing memory solutions to improve computer performance. With over 30 years of computer industry experience, we offer a broad range of memory products for UNIX/Windows NT workstations, and high-performance servers. We serve customers of Compaq, DEC, HP, IBM, SGI and Sun computers. Expansion has created excellent career opportunities with growth potential.

Dataram offers excellent salary, choice of medical/dental plans such as Guardian, US Healthcare Patriot X/QPOS Flex Plan, and US Healthcare NJ/PA Premier Plan. Coverage for medical/dental plans begins on the 1st of the month following your hire date. In addition to the medical plans are: Life Insurance, Accidental Death & Dismemberment, Common Carrier Travel Accident, Short Term Disability, and Long Term Disability. Additional benefits offered through payroll deductions are: Section 125 Pre-Tax Accounts (pretax health & child care accounts), 401(k) Savings and Investment Plan; Dataram will match the employee’s contribution, up to a maximum of 6% of employee’s annual gross income, and the match percentage is dependent upon the employee’s length of service (enrollment for 401(k) is on the first day of every calendar quarter & Dataram accepts rollover contributions from other qualified 401(k) plans), and Employee Stock Purchase Plan. Special added benefits from Dataram: Tuition Reimbursement, Seminars & Certificate Programs paid in full, Eight Company Paid Holidays and Four company paid floating holidays per year, and Vacation Days; Pro-rated number of vacation days in the Fiscal Year of employment (May 1-April 30) based upon the month in which you are hired. Two weeks per year in the second Fiscal Year of employment and thereafter. Three weeks per year from five to ten years of consecutive employment. One additional day for each year in excess of ten years, maximum vacation not to exceed (20) current days at 15 years of service. You may carry forward the maximum of 20 vacation days into a new fiscal year. Dataram also has a corporate membership with the Princeton YMCA, once you are hired you automatically are enrolled under the corporate membership.

Make a move that will have a major impact on your career! Dataram’s work environment is casual and team oriented. The employees at Dataram are friendly, supportive, and positive team players.
