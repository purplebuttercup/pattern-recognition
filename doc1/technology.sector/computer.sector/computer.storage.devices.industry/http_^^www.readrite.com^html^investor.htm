Stock Exchange Listing

Read-Rite Corporation's common stock is traded on the Nasdaq National Market System under the symbol RDRT. The daily high, low and closing sales prices of the company's common stock are published in most major newspapers under the heading 'ReadRite' or 'Read Rt'.

10-K Report Available

A copy of Read-Rite's 1996 Annual Report on Form 10-K to the Securities Exchange Commission accompanies this Summary Annual Report. It is also available on request to Investor Relations, Read-Rite Corporation, 345 Los Coches Street, Milpitas, CA 95035, or by calling 408-956-3596.
