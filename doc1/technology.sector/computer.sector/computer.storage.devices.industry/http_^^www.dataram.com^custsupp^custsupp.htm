Technical Hotline

Our Customer Support Representatives can answer your questions and give you information on system/memory configurations, upgrade options, product specifications, compatibility and other technical topics or issues. These are highly trained support engineers who are involved in all aspects of memory design, layout, manufacturing and quality control.

If you have a question related to memory or need personal assistance, just call 1-800-599-0071 or 609-799-0071 between 8 a.m. and 8 p.m. Eastern Time, Monday--Friday. A Customer Support Representative will be on duty to respond to your call and help you. Information can also be faxed directly to Dataram Customer Support at 609-936-1369 or sent via Internet E-mail support@dataram.com

Installation and Technical Support

Dataram’s Customer Service Team is also here to provide help when you need it—everything from installation assistance to memory trouble-shooting, repair or replacement.

If you have a question call 1-800-599-0071 or 609-799-0071 between 8 a.m. and 8 p.m. Eastern Time, Monday--Friday.

If you encounter a problem with your Dataram memory, please help us assist you by providing the following information:

  • Part number, serial number and revision code (shown on board)
  • Other memory installed on computer
  • Computer make and model number, operating system, version
  • Symptoms of the problem

    Return Policy

    All product returns must be approved in advance by Dataram. If a product return is approved, a Return Material Authorization number (RMA#) will be issued. Once an RMA number is issued, it is the purchaser's responsibility to have the product returned within five (5) working days of the Return Material Authorization date.

    All products returned must be undamaged, unused, and in resellable condition. The customer shall pay all costs and bear all risks when returning products to Dataram. Dataram reserves the right to refuse delivery of any products returned without an RMA number.
