Corporate Profile

Dataram is a worldwide leader in providing memory products which improve the performance of computer systems, thereby enhancing the productivity of their users. With 30 years of experience in the computer industry, we offer a broad range of gigabyte memory upgrades for UNIX and Windows/NT workstations and servers. Customers receive the highest quality products with the highest level of responsiveness because of the way we develop, manufacture, market, and support our products. Dataram primarily serves Compaq, DEC, HP, IBM, SGI, and Sun users in diverse industries such as manufacturing, finance, government, telecommunications, utilities, research, and education. Our ability to respond quickly to change—in business conditions, technology, and the needs of memory users—uniquely positions Dataram for future growth in markets throughout the world.
