MTI Technology TOTAL SUPPORT

MTI Technology Corporation is a leader in the design, manufacture, marketing and servicing of high-performance, cross-platform storage solutions for the DEC® VMS®, UNIX® open systems (including DEC, HP®, IBM®, Silicon Graphics® and Sun®) and Windows NT® computing environments. MTI integrates its patented applications and embedded software with its advanced servers and industry-standard storage peripherals to meet customer's information management needs.

In addition, MTI provides the highest level of customer satisfaction in the industry. MTI's worldwide service combines open-system expertise with fast response time, highly qualified system specialists and customized support options to meet every customer need. MTI offers hardware and software support on MTI-manufactured products as well as other open system platforms, including Sun Microsystems and Silicon Graphics hardware.

MTI's Service Advantage

  • Local spare parts availability
  • MTI factory-trained Field Engineers
  • Guaranteed response time
  • Guaranteed updates for compatibility and performance enhancements
  • 24 x 7 customer support hotline
  • Automatic software updates and bug fixes
  • Remote dial-in support
  • Management escalation
  • "Phone Home" support for selected MTI RAID products
