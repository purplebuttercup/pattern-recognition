ECCS Announces Nine Month Results,
Revenue Up 46%, Net Income Up 270%


ECCS, Inc., Tinton Falls, New Jersey, October 15, 1997 -- ECCS Inc. (NASDAQ SmallCap: ECCS) today reported positive results for the nine months and the third quarter ended September 30, 1997. Net sales for the nine months ended September 30, 1997 were $26.5 million, an increase of $8.4 million or 46% as compared to $18.1 million in the nine months ended September 30, 1996.

Net income for the nine months ended September 30, 1997 was $900 thousand or $.12 and $.10 per share on a primary and fully diluted basis respectively. This represents an improvement of $1.4 million, when compared to the Company's net loss of $530 thousand or $.16 per share for the nine months ended September 30, 1996.

Net sales for the third quarter ended September 30, 1997 were $8.7 million, an increase of $3.5 million or 67% as compared to $5.2 million in the third quarter of 1996. For the third quarter of 1997, ECCS reported a net income of $202 thousand, or $.02 per share on both a primary and fully diluted basis, an improvement of $357 thousand as compared to the third quarter of 1996 net loss of $155 thousand or $.05 per share.

This quarter's results reflect an extraordinary charge of $120 thousand which resulted from our transition of accounts receivable
financing to NationsBank. Net income per share before this extraordinary item for the third quarter of 1997 represented $.04 and $.03 on a primary and fully diluted basis respectively. Net income per share before this extraordinary item for the nine months of 1997 represented $.14 and $.11 on a primary and fully diluted basis respectively.

G.M. Azcuy, President and Chief Executive Officer said, "The Company is continuing to make good and solid progress this year. This is the result of the success of our innovative products, our new and existing customers, and the efforts of our dedicated employees. We are continuing to invest in our R&D and Engineering organizations as reflected by our more than 60% spending increase in this quarter, as compared to the same quarter last year. We have several new products that are in the final stages of completion, and I am confident that we have a solid vision for the future of mission critical and clustered storage in the Windows NT, Unix and Open Systems marketplace. We have much work ahead of us and we will continue to remain true to our mission of seeking innovation, growth and profitability in concert."

ECCS, Inc. designs, manufactures, markets and maintains high availability RAID storage devices for both direct bus attach and network attach environments. ECCS products are surrounded by an intuitive Graphical User Interface to tune, monitor, and manage ECCS products. ECCS has been incorporated since 1980, providing high availability solutions for UNIX and NT based computer systems. ECCS is headquartered in Tinton Falls, New Jersey.
