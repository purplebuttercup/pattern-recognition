NMS’ NaturalMedia products extend Telephony Services Architecture (TSA) beyond voice and call processing to include support for mixed-media, such as fax, speech recognition, and text-to-speech as well as vocoding for wireless and IP telephony solutions.

Within the TSA architecture the Alliance Generation supports fax processing via the addition of NaturalFax 2.0 software-only product . NaturalRecognition supports speech recognition with software and the addition of a daughter card for the the Alliance Generation platform.

For In-building Wireless, wireless PBX, Wireless Local Loop, and PCS/cellular applications, the IS-641 (ACELP) vocoder and Transcoder and Rate Adapter Unit (TRAU) software executes as a downloadable module on the Alliance Generation® Realtime/2 (AG-RT/2) hardware platform.

For building IP Telephony Gateway solutions, the AG Realtime is part of the FusionTM development platform along with the Alliance Generation T1 and the TX2000 IP Router. Fusion is a scalable, programmable platform which supports PSTN network interfaces, data network integration, voice encoding and decoding, fax modems, and data modulation/demodulation. Fusion supports TCI/IP, PPP, UDP and evolving H.323 protocols.

Designed to execute on the Alliance Generation® (AG) Realtime family of digital signal processing engines, the Natural MicroSystems G.723.1 TRAU (Transcoder and Rate Adapter Unit), is a full-duplex implementation of the ITU G.723.1 dual-rate vocoder with integrated echo cancellation and the Natural MicroSystems MS GSM Transcoder and Rate Adapter Unit (TRAU) is a full-duplex implementation of Microsoft’s GSM 6.10 vocoder with integrated echo cancellation. Voxware's MetaVoice RT 24 algorithm is a 2.4 kbps, realtime voice encoding algorithm designed specifically for applications requiring minimal processing power and high-quality speech.
