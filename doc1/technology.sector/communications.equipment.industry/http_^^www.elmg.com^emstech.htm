EMS Technologies, Inc. (EMS), based in Norcross, designs and manufactures custom solutions for microwave systems, antennas, subsystems and components for communications, surveillance, radar and countermeasure applications in satellite, airborne and surface environments. Most recently, EMS has formed an additional Business Group, EMS Wireless, with emphasis on cellular base station antennas as well as custom solutions for wireless applications in the cellular and PCS frequency ranges.
