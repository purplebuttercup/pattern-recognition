An Overview of . . .

Electromagnetic Sciences, Inc. provides wireless communications systems to a "world on the move." The Company seeks to meet the needs of mobile information users with a unique range of technologies developed from years of experience in advanced wireless communications, including extensive cutting-edge satellite and defense wireless systems. In particular, the Company serves the markets for wireless logistics, infrastructure for public and private wireless networks, satellite communications, and space and other specialized technology applications.

Electromagnetic Sciences serves its markets and customers through three wholly owned subsidiaries, EMS Technologies, Inc. and LXE Inc., each based in Norcross, Georgia, and CAL Corporation, based in Ottawa, Ontario. Each subsidiary contributes its own unique experience and wireless technology. The combined products and technologies enable the Company to address wireless data communication needs ranging from high-speed local area computer networks in warehouses and hospitals to satellite-based communications with aircraft and ground vehicles roaming the world.

Electromagnetic Sciences is publicly held, with its common stock traded on the NASDAQ National Market System (symbol ELMG) and held by approximately 4,000 shareholders.
