ENGINEERING

Applications Engineer
You will be interfacing technical application requirements for RF power amplifier based systems and subsystems with our leading-edge in-house engineering team and customer field supply.

Design Engineer (RF)
You will be part of a state-of-the-art team designing and developing RF power amplifier based systems and subsystems for high volume commercial manufacturing.

Digital Engineer
You will be working with a leading-edge team designing and developing digital circuits for RF power amplifier based systems and subsystems for high volume commercial manufacturing.

Project/Program Engineer
You will manage major RF amplifier projects in which products and processes developed in R&D are transferred to manufacturing.

Mechanical Engineer
You will be working with a fast paced team designing and developing packaging of RF electronics and rack and panel type assemblies for high volume commercial manufacturing.
