.Natural MicroSystems products are based on the Telephony Services Architecture (TSA), a hardware and software framework to support the broadest range of open, high-capacity, switching intensive, mixed-media applications for worldwide telephone network integration. TSA meets developers requirements’ for adherence to industry standards, easy integration of mixed-media with applications, built-in scalability, and a comprehensive set of development tools to deliver solutions on-demand into the next decade.

Natural Platforms Hardware and resident software products, including the award-winning Alliance Generation family of digital signal processor (DSP) engines, the AG Connect family of telephone network interfaces, the VBX first-generation telephony family of hardware and software, and the VOX family of DSP and interface platforms from VOX S.A., a European subsidiary of Natural MicroSystems.

Natural Media Software and hardware extensions to the Natural Platforms products for integrating facsimile, speech recognition and text-to-speech into a single application.

Natural Access Software for the Natural Platforms and Natura Media products including software development toolkits, application programming interfaces (APIs), libraries, switching software, and application modules.
