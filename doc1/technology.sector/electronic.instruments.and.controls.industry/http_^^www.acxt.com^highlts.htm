Highlights

Go to for Stock Data, - NYSE Symbol: ACX

  • ACX Technologies manufactures value-added industrial products. Its businesses produce high performance consumer and industrial packaging, and advanced technical ceramics.
  • Through innovation these businesses have a technological advantage, resulting in product uniqueness or lower costs.
  • ACX Technologies believes that the diversity of its businesses makes it less susceptible to changes in economic cycles.
  • ACX Technologies is committed to achieving double-digit growth earnings per share and a 20% operating income return on assets employed at its two core businesses, while maintaining an investment-grade debt rating.
  • The management of the company is focused on shareholder value.
