The HTMS·2510 Stereo Surround Sound System
True 3-Dimensional Sound From Any Two Speakers

HTMS·2510: The Affordable Surround Sound System
Spatializer Audio Laboratories announces the simple and affordable alternative to today's expensive sound enhancement and multi-speaker surround sound systems -- the HTMS·2510.
The Spatializer HTMS·2510 connects to any stereo TV, VCR or stereo system to produce audio with unparalleled depth, dimension, and realism through only two speakers.
Now consumers everywhere can breathe new life into their favorite music, movies, and television programs.
Easy Installation
The HTMS·2510 is a self-contained surround sound system in one sleek, modern unit. It doesn't require additional speakers, amplifiers, or decoders to provide a thoroughly satisfying surround sound experience. Just plug it in and turn it on.
Simple Operation
With the HTMS·2510, there's no need to balance the volume of multiple audio channels and no time delays to set or adjust. Just a few simple controls that anyone can set in moments! It even includes a remote control for added convenience.
Universal Compatibility
Most multi-speaker systems provide surround sound effects only when the audio has been specially pre-encoded. With these systems, if your favorite movie or program isn't encoded, all you get is plain old stereo! Not so with the HTMS·2510.
With the Spatializer HTMS·2510, you can enjoy enhanced 3-D stereo with all your movies, TV shows, audio tapes and CDs. Spatializer's exclusive and patented Double Detect and Protect (DDP) circuitry allows the enhancement of stereo source material without over-processing -- no matter what you're listening to.
Improves Existing Surround Sound Systems
The HTMS·2510 actually enhances the surround sound effects of most existing multi-speaker surround sound systems. Consumers can now experience an incredible new level of three-dimensional sound with all their movies, programs and recordings -- encoded or not.
From the World Leader In Audio Enhancement Technologies
The HTMS·2510 and its patented 3-D stereo technology are products of Spatializer Audio Laboratories, Inc., a world leader in the develoment of advanced audio enhancement. Spatializer's HTMS·2510. It's audio with a passion for life.
