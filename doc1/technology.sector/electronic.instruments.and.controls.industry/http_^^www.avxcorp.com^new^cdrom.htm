AVX's GREATEST HITS ON CD!


Electronic Components * Software * Product Catalogs * Technical Papers



AVX announces a new CD-Rom containing the latest in software, product and technical information and its broad selection of electronic components. This Electronic Catalog System contains the latest catalog information on MLC and tantalum capacitors, power supply capacitors, resistor chips, arrays and networks, integrated passive components, thin-film inductors, SMT fuses, transient suppressors, filters, piezos, and timing devices. AVX technical papers are included along with information from AVX's recent technical seminars.

AVX's new Electronic Catalog System offers component and design engineers a ready resource for specifications, product information and technical data. AVX's SpiCap & SpiTan software available on the CD help access circuit modeling information for MLC and tantalum surface mount chip capacitors such as ESR, ESL and impedence changes with frequency. Also included is AVX's exclusive CALCI program that aids customers in choosing appropriate ceramic capacitors for power supply applications.
