 The Radiation Measurements Division, located in Cleveland, Ohio, serves the diagnostic x-ray and radiation therapy markets with precision instruments and associated accessories. These instruments accurately measure radiation emission levels of x-ray machines and nuclear radiation sources and are used to calibrate radiation therapy and x-ray equipment in hospitals and manufacturing processes. The Radiation Measurements Division has built a reputation of meeting the customer's needs for efficiency and accuracy with precise products that have long-term stability and reliable factory service and recalibration.
