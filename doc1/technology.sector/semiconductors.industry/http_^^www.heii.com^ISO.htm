Corporate Page

On June 8th, 1995 HEI was awarded a certificate of registration to the ISO 9001 Quality Systems Standard for it's Victoria, Minnesota based design, development, production, installation and servicing operations. The company was audited to the newly revised 1994 version of this standard for Quality Assurance by representitives from the National Standards Authority of Ireland, an International Standards Registrar.
