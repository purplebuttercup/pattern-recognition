MEDICAL PRODUCTS

For over 25 years HEI has supplied high reliability microelectronic assemblies to medical companies worldwide. Innovative and responsive, we initiated the first electronic hybrid packaging for the health care industry.

 Today patients and physicians demand smaller, lighter, and more cost-efficient equipment. HEI works with the medical device companies to design and fabricate ultraminiature packaging for medical microelectronic applications where both small size and high reliability are critical factors.

 Driven by both customers and technology, HEI designs and manufactures thick film hybrids-Ultra Fine Line Packaging with less than .005" lines and spaces to make smaller medical products possible.

 Ultraminiature electronics can reduce current densities for lower power requirements, eliminate unnecessary interconnects, increase reliability and allow circuits to operate at higher clock speeds.

 HEI offers proven microelectronic solutions for medical applications:

 

  • Blood Analyzers
  • CAT and MRI Equipment
  •  Hearing Aids
  •  TENS Devices (Trancutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulus)
  •  Drug Delivery Systems
  • Electro-Physiology Products
  •  Smart Sensors

    • CIC(Completely in Canal) Hearing Aid
