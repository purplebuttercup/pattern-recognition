A/D Converter Brochure

Check Out Our Wide Variety of CMOS Sampling A/D Converters



Low Power, Serial 12- and 16-Bit Sampling A/Ds
ADS7812 and ADS7813 are low power, single +5V supply, 12- and 16-bit sampling analog-to-digital converters with sample/hold, clock, reference, and serial data interface. Key specs: 40kHz sampling rate, multiple input ranges, 25µs conversion time, 35µW power dissipation, and 50µW power down mode. ADS7812 features 72dB min SINAD and ±0.5LSB max INL and DNL; ADS7813 features 87dB min SINAD, ±2.0LSB max INL, and 16 bits no missing codes. Both are available in 16-pin 0.3" plastic DIP and 16-lead SOIC packages.




16-Bit CMOS A/D Samples at 250kHz
ADS7815 is a monolithic 16-bit capacitor-based SAR A/D converter complete with S/H, precision reference, clock, µprocessor interface, and three-state output drivers. With typical specifications of 98dB THD and 100dB SFDR, ADS7815 offers outstanding AC performance—ideal for multi-channel data acquisition systems. ADS7815 is your solution of choice for ATE, wireless base stations, and CCD applications. Key specs: full parallel data output with latches, 250mW max power dissipation, 84dB min SINAD, 96dB min SFDR with 100kHz input, and internal or external reference. ADS7815 is available in a 28-lead SOIC package.




22-Bit ResolutionPLUS Delta-Sigma A/Ds Feature "Turbo Mode" Operation
ADS1212 and ADS1213 are are precision, wide dynamic range, delta-sigma A/D converters with 24-bit resolution operating from a single +5V supply. Effective resolutions of 16 bits can be maintained up to a sample rate of 1kHz through use of the unique "Turbo" modulator mode of operation. Key specs: 22 bits no missing codes, 20 bits effective resolution at 10Hz, 1.4mW low power, differential inputs, and programmable gain amplifier. ADS1212 is a single channel converter and is offered in both 18-pin DIP and 18-lead SOIC packages. ADS1213 is a four channel input multiplexer and is available in 24-pin DIP, 24-lead SOIC, and 28-lead SSOP packages.




1mW Low Power, 12-Bit A/D in Compact 8-lead SOIC
ADS1286 is a 12-bit, 20kHz, 1mW microPOWER™ sampling A/D converter with a fully differential input and sample/hold amplifier. This low power device—consuming only 250µA of supply current—is packaged in a compact 8-lead SOIC. ADS1286 offers an SPI and SSI compatible serial interface for communications over a two or three wire interface. The combination of a serial two wire interface, micro power consumption, and low cost make the ADS1286 ideal for remote and isolated data acquisition, transducer interface, and battery operated systems. ADS1286 is available in an 8-lead SOIC and an 8-pin plastic mini DIP.




World's Smallest 12-Bit A/Ds
ADS7816, ADS7817, and ADS7822 are 12-bit, low power A/Ds available in an 8-lead Mini Small-Outline Package (MSOP)—the smallest 12-bit A/Ds available. ADS7816 and ADS7817 feature 12-bit resolution, 200kHz sampling rate, 1.9mW power dissipation, synchronous serial interface, differential input, and automatic power down. ADS7822 features operation from 2.0V to 5V, 12-bit resolution, 75kHz sampling rate, and micro power (0.54mW at 75kHz, 0.06 at 7.5kHz). They are ideal for battery operated systems including watches, smoke detectors, and home medical appliances. The converters are also packaged in an 8-pin plastic mini-DIP and an 8-lead SOIC.
