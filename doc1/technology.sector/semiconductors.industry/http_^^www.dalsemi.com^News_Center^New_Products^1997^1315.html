DS1315 Phantom Time Chip

Product Features

The DS1315 Phantom Time Chip is a combination RTC and nonvolatile memory controller. The on-chip Real Time Clock provides hundredths of seconds, seconds, minutes, hours, day, date, month, and year information with leap year compensation valid up to 2100. The nonvolatile controller incorporates all of the support circuitry to convert a CMOS RAM to a nonvolatile memory. The DS1315 (5-volt version) is a drop-in replacement for the DS1215 and is pin- and function-compatible with the DS1215.

Key Features

  • Real time clock
  • NV RAM control for external SRAM
  • Available in either 3.3- or 5.0-volt operating range
  • Phantom interface, exclusive to Dallas' DS1315, is transparent to the system

Packages/Voltages

The DS1315 is available in either 3.3- or 5.0-volt operating ranges. It is also available in three packages, 16-pin DIP and SOIC, and 20-pin TSSOP.

Applications

Replacement for existing DS1215 designs which require a high-resolution RTC (hundredths of a second) along with nonvolatile control for external memory.
