Employment Opportunities

Current Opportunities:

Software Engineers
The qualified candidate(s) will have a minimum of 6 years experience in product engineering software development and a BSEE (MSEE preferred) or Computer Science degree. Must have significant experience in microcomputer programming using assembler and C languages. Hands-on hardware capability a plus.

Senior Engineer
Candidates must have BSEE (MSEE preferred) and 6-10 years experience in electronics design engineering work with RF/analog design experience. Product development experience a must, preferably with volume, commercial products. Data communications experience required.

Also searching for applicants with digital design specialty in addition to the above qualifications.

Product Engineer (LF)
Candidate must have BSEE (MSEE preferred) with 6-10 years experience in electronics design. Analog and digital circuit board design and debug, circuit failure analysis, and RF practical application or equivalent experience preferred. Product development experience required, preferably with volume, commercial products.

Product Marketing Manager(s) (LF and HF)
Responsible for product marketing including market assessment and product definition, applications engineering, lead generation and major account engagements. The successful candidate with have 7+ years experience marketing high technology products and a BS degree, preferably in a technical discipline (MS or MBA preferred). Experience with product planning, business planning, lead generation and technical sales required.

Director, Tech Support
Responsible for pre- and post-sales prospect/customer development. Will provide technical support to the Transportation Solutions Group's sales, and especially its implementation efforts. Requires hands-on knowledge of hard ware and software, presentation skills, travel to prospect/customer and industry meeting venues. Successful candidate will have minimum of Bachelor's level degree in an electro-mechanical engineering field, plus computer hardware and software training.

Software Applications Engineer
This person will develop Windows-based smart card applications. The candidate should be familiar with C++ and Visual Basic. A background in security and encryption is preferred. The job will require working with a variety of third-party software packages and developing data sharing procedures for them. A BSCS or BSEE is required and 1-3 years industrial experience is preferred. A self-starting attitude and strong motivation to make a contribution to a new and fast-growing "high-tech" market are required.
