Etec supplies advanced pattern generation equipment for the fabrication of masks used in the production of integrated circuits. Etec's Electron Beam systems and Laser Beam systems combine advanced patterning technologies with production-worthy equipment design to set the industry standard in maskmaking capability. As the only manufacturer of pattern generation equipment to offer both electron and and laser beam technology, Etec can provide a total patterning solution for the full range of production strategies of its customers worldwide.

As the leading supplier of pattern generation equipment, Etec has established a global presence and supports the largest installed base of such equipment in the world. Etec maintains customer support and sales support offices in all major centers of integrated circuit production.
