CyberOptics designs and manufactures non-contact process control sensors and measurement systems that utilize laser and vision (video) technologies as well as advanced software for collecting, analyzing and displaying data.

Our sensors and systems are marketed worldwide to the rapidly-growing electronics industry for SMT (surface mount technology) applications, and to industrial users with applications requiring high-resolution measurement of micro-precision components.
