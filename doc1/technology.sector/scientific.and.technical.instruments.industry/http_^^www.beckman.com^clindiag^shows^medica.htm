Welcome to Beckman Coulter
at MEDICA '97

With the close of the Coulter acquisition 31 October, Beckman Coulter will introduce a unified, powerful presence to the worldwide medical community at MEDICA '97. While each company will maintain its own booth, both will carry the banner of Beckman Coulter. Visit Hall 3, space A64 to view Beckman products; Coulter products are displayed in Hall 2, space B28/C37.

MEDICA marks the official European introduction of the new SYNCHRONÆ system for the high-volume laboratory – the LX4201. This much-anticipated new product provides throughput advances and exciting new features for high-volume laboratories.

Visit the Beckman Coulter booths to learn more about our complete line of innovative products and the revolutionary process improvement concept – The Power of ProcessTM. Supported by major new product additions to the industry's leading line of instrumentation and reagents, Beckman Coulter's Power of Process can help your laboratory simplify and automate its operations.

Experience the Power of Progressive Automation, Immunodiagnostics, Rapid Testing Systems and Disease Management. And don't forget the Power of Menu, now even stronger with the addition of Coulter's testing capabilities. We've got it all. Talk to a member of our team and find out how the combined strength of Beckman Coulter can Bring the Power of Process to your lab.
