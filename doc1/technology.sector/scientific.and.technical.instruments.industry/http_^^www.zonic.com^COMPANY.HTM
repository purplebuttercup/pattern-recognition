Welcome to Zonic

Zonic was founded 30 years ago as the result of pioneering research in structural analysis at the University of Cincinnati. Extensive research had shown that the "tuning" of the dynamics of structures could give great improvements in product performance and quality.

Over the years, Zonic has continued to introduce innovative tools to help people in a variety of industries to design and manufacture products with enhanced safety, quality, and marketability. We put this commitment into practice in the design of all our systems.

Today, Zonic designs and manufactures noise and vibration measurement systems and software that are used in the aerospace, automotive, chemical, paper, steel, and utility industries. In virtually every major world market Zonic's tools are effectively used to evaluate the performance of our customers' products and processes. The difference that Zonic makes is reflected in our commitment to offering you the best vibration analysis tools available.

Zonic believes that your data acquisition shouldn't be limited by your systems capabilities. We also believe that you should not have to custom configure a system for data acquisition. That's why Zonic systems are designed with many of your requirements built in. Our systems provide broader dynamic ranges, more input channels, greater frequency ranges, portability, and open software architectures for ease in customization. And since every customers' application requires something a little different, we can consult with you on how you might integrate third party software. This allows you to focus on your application and process, thus saving you time and money.
