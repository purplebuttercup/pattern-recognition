Solder Paste Inspection Systems

CyberOptics is the industry’s leading supplier of solder paste inspection equipment used during SMT circuit board assembly. LSM and Sentry systems are used to measure and evaluate wet solder paste which has been screened onto printed circuit boards.

By inspecting solder paste immediately after screen printing, defects can be detected before components are placed. Correcting printing problems during production results in increased yields, reduced scrap and less rework.
