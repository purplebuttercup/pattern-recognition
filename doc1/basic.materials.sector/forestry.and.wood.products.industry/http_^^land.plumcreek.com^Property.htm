One visit to Northwest Montana, and you will know why John Steinbeck fell in love. The signature of Northwest Montana is the Flathead Valley. The Flathead Valley is one of the most popular destinations for recreation in the Rocky Mountains due to its diversity and beauty. There are outstanding opportunities to fish, hike, view wildlife, golf, ride horses, picnic, camp, swim, raft/boat, hunt, ski (cross country in many areas and downhill in Whitefish at Big Mountain Resort), and snowmobile. The Plum Creek properties are ideally located to take advantage of all of these recreational opportunities.

Flathead Lake, in the center of the area, is one of the cleanest, most spectacular mountain lakes in the world. The lake is the largest freshwater lake in the western United States. and is well known for its majestic setting beneath the Rockies, as well as the boating and fishing it offers. To the north of Flathead Lake is the "Crown Jewel of The Rockies," Glacier National Park. Glacier Park is internationally renowned and one of the most visited national parks in the country due to its extraordinary beauty, wildlife, and recreational opportunities. In addition, the Flathead Valley has ten golf courses within a 50 mile radius with unrivaled settings, including Eagle Bend Resort and Meadowlake Resort.

Properties are currently available which range in size from 20 to 900 acres, and vary in price from $240,000 to $6.5 million. In addition, Plum Creek has identified other lake, river, and mountain properties throughout its ownership which have extraordinary recreation, conservation, and development values that will become available in the future. Ashley Lake. The Ashley Lake properties range in size from 100 to 225 acres, with 2600 to 3700 feet of frontage. Ashley Lake is located west of Kalispell and is a popular second home area. The lake is very private, particularly in the area close to some of the prime Plum Creek parcels on the north and west sides of the lake. Ashley Lake is one of the most beautiful lakes in Northwest Montana and the Plum Creek properties around the lake afford the buyer a unique opportunity to own a large parcel in this area. The prices for the Ashley lake properties are listed between $885,000 and $1,200,000.

Island Lake. The Island Lake property is located in Lincoln County approximately 60 miles west of Kalispell. The property is approximately 300 acres with approximately 7,700 linear feet of frontage on Island Lake. Island Lake is a small, pristine and private lake located just north of Pleasant Valley and is known for its bass fishing. The listing price for the Island Lake property is currently being evaluated.

Lake Mary Ronan. The Lake Mary Ronan properties are comprised of approximately 3,000 acres of land on and around Lake Mary Ronan. The properties make up approximately two-thirds of the lake shore. Lake Mary Ronan is one of the most popular fishing lakes in Montana and has very little available property because most of the shore is owned by Plum Creek. These properties offer both recreational development and private estate potential. The size of the parcels range from 60 to over 920 acres, with 2,950 feet to four miles of lake shore, and the listing prices range between $1,250,000 and $6,500,000. There is also a non-lakefront parcel (with lake views) which is approximately 120 acres, listed at $240,000.

Little Bitterroot Lake. The Little Bitterroot Lake properties are comprised of approximately 2,000 acres of land with approximately two-thirds of the lake shore along Little Bitterroot Lake. The lake is approximately 30 miles west of Kalispell, near the community of Marion. The size of the parcels range from 20 to over 400 acres, with 1,200 to 7,500 feet of lake shore, and the listing prices of the parcels range between $550,000 and $2,950,000.

Salmon Lake/Clearwater River. The Salmon Lake /Clearwater River properties are located in the southern portion of the beautiful Swan Valley. The parcels include a private and isolated 40 acre parcel on the west side of the lake, and two parcels along the Clearwater River south of Salmon Lake, which range in size from approximately 115 acres to 280 acres. Salmon Lake's location and easy access make it a popular recreational destination. The price for the Salmon Lake property is $525,000. The prices for the Clearwater River properties are listed at $500,000 and $950,000.
