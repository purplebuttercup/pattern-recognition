Island Lake Property


The Properties Locator Map (click for details)
Island Lake is located in Northwest Montana on the Lincoln County/Flathead County border and includes the southern half of Island Lake. Island Lake is a 225-acre lake and the property itself is approximately 300 acres of land. The Island Lake property offers the unique opportunity to own (with two additional owners) an entire lake. Additional surrounding acreage as well as lake shore may be available if desired. Island Lake is a warm water lake with plentiful wildlife habitat and excellent bass fishing. The price of the Island Lake property is currently being evaluated.
