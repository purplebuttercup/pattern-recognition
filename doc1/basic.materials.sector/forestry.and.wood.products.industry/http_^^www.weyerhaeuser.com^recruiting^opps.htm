Employment Opportunities

Updated November 15, 1997

The next Employment Opportunities update will be December 6 due to the Thanksgiving Holidays.

Applications are accepted for these positions as stated on the posting. There are also Ongoing Opportunities available. To apply for a position:

  1. You may submit your cover letter and resume by email, fax, or postal mail. Duplicate submission is not necessary. Please note the application instructions (located in the contact section) of the position you are interested in:
    • IF:
      The contact is the Staffing Resource Center, carefully follow the Scannable Resume Guidelines to submit your cover letter and resume by email. Do NOT send email attachments to the Staffing Resource Center. By fax, send to 206-924-4151.
    • IF:
      The contact is NOT the Staffing Resource Center, the position is being handled by the individual Business--in these cases it's important to follow the contact section instructions so your application is routed correctly to the appropriate person.
  2. Equally important is to include the job title, geographic location and search number of the position you are applying for.
  3. By postal mail, respond directly to the contact address shown on each posting for either the Staffing Resource Center or the Business.
