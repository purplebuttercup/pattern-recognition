College Relations

Weyerhaeuser recruits college students or graduates in order to identify and introduce to Weyerhaeuser talented entry-level professionals in areas that have been identified as critical to our success.

Opportunities for College Students and Graduates

We hire to fill a variety of intern and full-time positions for students from various academic disciplines, at both the bachelors and graduate degree levels. Please refer to the position description for details on specific requirements and contact information.

  • Internships are usually full-time temporary positions, lasting three to six months, for continuing students working toward completion of their undergraduate or graduate degrees.
  • Associate positions are full-time developmental positions, designed to give recent graduates a head start on their career with Weyerhaeuser.

College Recruiting Process

We identify candidates for our internship and associate opportunities through a variety of avenues.

  • On-campus recruiting: Weyerhaeuser representatives visit leading colleges and universities to participate in career fairs and student events, hold on-campus interviews, and serve as guest lecturers in classrooms. In this way we build awareness of Weyerhaeuser, establish relationships with students and faculty, and identify candidates whose abilities and interests match our needs.
  • Application for specific opportunities: Submit application materials as directed for the specific internship or associate position.
  • If you don't see a position listed that fits your interests: You may submit a resume and cover letter, specifying  career objective, to Weyerhaeuser College Relations. The information is entered in our database, where it is kept for six months to be accessed if an appropriate opportunity becomes available.
  • Please follow Scannable Resume Guidelines to submit your cover letter and resume by mail or email. Do not send email attachments.
