    Investment Information

    Security Information

    Class A Units are listed on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol LOG. They are generally listed by the financial press on composite transactions from the NYSE under the name RayTmbLP.

    There were approximately 1,805 unitholders of record of Class A Units as of February 28, 1997.

    Class A unitholders should expect that the market price of Class A Units should be decreasing substantially as December 31, 2000 approaches.

    Partnership Structure

    It is important for unitholders to understand the terms of the Partnership Agreement and the reduced ownership interest that the Class A unitholders have in the Partnership cash flow after the year 2000. As a result of the Initial Term expiring in that year, the market price of Class A Units should be decreasing substantially as December 31, 2000 approaches.
