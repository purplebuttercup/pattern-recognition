Little Bitterroot Lake Properties


The Properties Locator Map (click for details)
The Little Bitterroot Lake properties consist of several properties on and around Little Bitterroot Lake in Northwest Montana. In total, the properties have approximately six miles of lake shore (over one-half of the total lake shore) and contain more than 2,500 acres of land. The properties include a variety of terrain including no-bank to high-bank waterfront as well as gently rolling hillsides to steep mountain overlooks. The Little Bitterroot Lake Properties provide an opportunity like no other for exclusive lakefront acreage or to develop a significant community around one of Northwest Montana’s well known pristine lakes. The properties in this area are priced from $550,000 to $2,950,000.
