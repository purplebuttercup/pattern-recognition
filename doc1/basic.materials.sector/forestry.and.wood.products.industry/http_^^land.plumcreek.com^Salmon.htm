Salmon Lake/Clearwater River Properties


The Properties Locator Map (click for details)
The Salmon Lake/Clearwater River properties are special parcels in the southern Swan Valley which have lake frontage and/or Clearwater River frontage. There is very little land available on the lake making these parcels even more special. Limited access assures long-term privacy and seclusion for the owner of these properties.The northern parcel is approximately 39 acres with 1,650 feet of frontage along Salmon Lake. The southern parcels run along the Clearwater River and are 115 and 281 acres with 1.5 miles of frontage on the river each. On the east side of the lake, there is a small strip of land which would be appropriate for development as a boathouse to facilitate access to the parcels on the west side of the lake. The pricing for the Salmon Lake/Clearwater River properties ranges from $500,000 to $950,000.
