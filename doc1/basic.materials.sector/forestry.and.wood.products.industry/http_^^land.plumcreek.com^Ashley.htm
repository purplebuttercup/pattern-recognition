Ashley Lake Properties


The Properties Locator Map (click for details)
The Ashley Lake properties consist of properties ranging in size from 100 to 225 acres, with 2600 to 3700 feet of frontage several properties on and around Ashley Lake in Northwest Montana. In total, the properties include over two miles of lake shore and approximately 1,000 acres of land. The properties consist of a variety of terrains including pristine lakefront, forested hillsides and wetland habitats. Currently, the majority of development in the area is on the southern and northeastern shorelines. The properties consist of developable areas on both the north and southwest shores. The location of the properties provide privacy and exclusivity. The pricing for the Ashley Lake properties ranges from $885,000 to $1,170,00.
