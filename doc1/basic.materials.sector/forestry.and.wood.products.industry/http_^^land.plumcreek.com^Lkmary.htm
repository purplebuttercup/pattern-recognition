Lake Mary Ronan Properties


The Properties Locator Map (click for details)
The Lake Mary Ronan properties consist of several properties around Lake Mary Ronan in Northwest Montana. In total, the properties have approximately five miles of lake shore (about two-thirds of the total lake shore) and are made up of several thousand acres of land. The properties consist of a variety of terrain, including prime waterfront as well as gently rolling hillsides and steep mountain overlooks. The properties provide the truly rare opportunity to own the majority of a significant and popular recreational lake in Northwest Montana. The properties in this area are priced from $240,000 to $6,500,000.
