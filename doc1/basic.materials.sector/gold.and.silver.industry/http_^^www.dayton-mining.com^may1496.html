    The Company's 100% owned Andacollo Gold Mine commenced commercial operations on January 1, 1996, and produced 22,159 ounces of gold during the first quarter of 1996. Cash operating costs were slightly under budget at US$163 per ounce of gold. By March 31, 1996 2.7 million tons of ore had been placed on the leach pad containing 62,700 recoverable ounces of gold. Gold production is expected to increase each quarter into 1997 as the 120 day leach cycle approaches a steady state, and current and planned increases in crusher throughput take effect.

    Dayton Mining Corporation is listed on the Toronto Stock Exchange in Canada and the American Stock Exchange in the United States under the trading symbol DAY.
