Piedras Verdes and Suaqui Verde Copper Concessions
Piedras Verdes is located 17 km north of Alamos, Sonora, Mexico. Suaqui Verde is located about 150 km south of Hermosillo, the state capital of Sonora, Mexico.
Mali Gold Concessions
AZCO contracted to acquire 51% of Sanou Mining Corp. which has full working interest in two gold concessions in the Kenieba Gold Mining District. They are the Medinandi and Dandoko concessions.
Indonesian Gold Concessions
AZCO has acquired interests in 3 Indonesian Contracts of Work... B-4 Block, Bengkulu, and Purwokerto.
