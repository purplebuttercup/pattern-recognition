1,914,000 Ounces of Gold

A gram of gold is the size of a shirt button. But it can provide a day's income of $12 to feed the family of an independent hand miner in Venezuela. It's all a matter of scale, but our purpose is the same. At 31 grams per troy ounce, Placer Dome produced 2,210,000 ounces of gold in 1996 of which the Corporation's share was 1,914,000 ounces. Our gold mines generated operating earnings of $208 million in 1996, worldwide. We aim to provide growth for our investors, and improved standards of living for those who inhabit regions of the world where our mining investments take place.
