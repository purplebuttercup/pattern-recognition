About Us

Paul Mueller Company specializes in the fabrication of stainless steel equipment. Mueller quality is entrusted in the operations of the food, dairy, beverage, chemical, pharmaceutical, and aerospace industries. With over 50 years of experience serving national and regional breweries, Paul Mueller Company is also recognized for highly engineered and skillfully crafted brewery equipment.


Brewery Systems Team

Please meet the Mueller Brewery Equipment Team...Harry Barbee, Brewery Team Leader, has been with Mueller for 25 years and is also the Service General Manager. Mitch Brown is the Brewery Team Coordinator and personally oversees customer satisfaction from quote to follow-up after delivery. Randy Warren is Brewery Equipment Designer, Ralf Hoffman is Brewery Equipment Engineer, Tim Dunlap is Brewery System Controls Engineer and Ashton Lewis is Staff Master Brewer. This core team is supported by researchers, craftsmen, service technicians, and sales engineers dedicated to serving the modern brewer.


Mueller Mission

The Brewery Equipment Team's mission is to apply Mueller's knowledge, skill, and experience to the design, production, sales, and installation of reliable cost-effective brewery systems which can be operated by one person and which are capable of producing top-quality brews on a consistent basis for the craft brewing industry while continuing to serve the needs of national brewery customers.


Service

Field Service is a long standing benefit to owning Mueller equipment. The Paul Mueller Company maintains a highly-trained team of service technicians to quickly respond to the service needs of our customers. This is an important consideration when continuous operation is critical.

Beyond field servicing and repair, Mueller also assists customers in modifying previously purchased processing equipment in order to accommodate new process requirements--another way of ensuring that customers receive the most from their investment in Mueller equipment.
