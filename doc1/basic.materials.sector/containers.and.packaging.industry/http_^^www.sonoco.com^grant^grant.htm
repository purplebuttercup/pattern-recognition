Guidelines for Grant Consideration

Sonoco continues, through its philanthropic and business endeavors, to demonstrate its commitment to social responsibility. The corporation views its contribution program as an investment to improve the quality of life, especially in communities where Sonoco has operations.

The Sonoco Foundation, the principal conduit for corporate contributions of Sonoco, is responsive to human needs through its support of quality projects and programs within its priority areas of funding.

The Foundation's grant program currently focuses on education, health and welfare, arts and culture, and the environment. The Foundation supports higher education institutions that supply Sonoco future leaders and technologists. The vast majority of these funds support United State institutions with a local, rather than a national, perspective.
