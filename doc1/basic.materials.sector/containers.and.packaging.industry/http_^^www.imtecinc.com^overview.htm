THE RIGHT LABEL
Shipping, Receiving, Compliance & Packaging
The best choice for cardboard box, container and package labeling. These labels are in compliance with AIAG, LOGMARS, HIBCC, TCIF, MIL-61002, UCC and retail specifications. 		ApplyPrint: a paper label in 14 varieties designed to withstand specific temperatures and environments.

GreenCode: recyclable, it comes in polyethylene, polyolefin and polyester to address your specific requirements.

Outdoor
Labels that are exposed to sunlight, ultraviolet rays, rain, humidity, cold.

DuraCode 7120: a polypropylene label with cold temperature adhesive.

Tags
These materials are suitable in most applications not requiring adhesive.

TuffTag 7114: an excellent tear and moisture resistant polypropylene tag.

Security
These labels provide evidence of tampering or removal and they are designed to deter theft or other unlawful acts.

Guardian7036: polyester label that leaves the word "void" on surface when it's peeled away.

TamperGuard 7016: a destructible tamper-evident asset management label.

Abrasion, Chemical & Solvent Resistance
Select one of these labels when harsh environments are a factor.

StickTuff 7107: excellent for metal and plastic pipe, designed for the most demanding adhesive needs.

ScratchFree 7168: general purpose high-durability polyester with excellent smear, scratch, chemical and moisture resistance.

High-temperature & Electronics
Select these labels when service temperatures exceed 300 degrees Fahrenheit.

StaticSafe: polyimide label designed to have reduced electrostatic discharge properties.

STS 2500 Series: polyimide labels with resistance to a wide variety of harsh environments.

HeatShield: polyester and polymer labels designed for high temperatures and electronic applications.

Laminates
Over laminates provide extra protection against paint, heat, UV rays, abrasion, moisture and exposure to chemicals or solvents.

PaintBlock: polyester label with a peelable layer. A permanent laminate remains on the label surface.

SunScreen: polyvinyl fluoride with UV protection, high-strength, and solvent resistance.

The Labels above are just a few of the many that are available from Imtec's comprehensive label selection guide.
