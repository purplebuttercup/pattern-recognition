Do you need a Label Printing System?

Paxar's Systems Group produces a full line of labeling printers, utilizing: Electronic, Thermal Transfer and Hot Stamp technologies. In addition, the Systems Group provides custom windows and DOS software for creating tags and labels and inputting data. The equipment is supported by a vertical production facility for tags, inks and coated fabric substrates combined with an international service network.

The Systems Group service department provides both on site and telephone support for all Paxar printers and software. In addition, each region provides technical consultants to help with equipment planning, program set up and on site training. These Electronic Systems Specialists (ESS) also provide assistance to your MIS department with computer interface requirements.
