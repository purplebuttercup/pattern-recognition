Cellar Tanks

Mueller Cellar Tanks exemplify Mueller's strict standards for stainless steel equipment design and manufacture. For many years, Paul Mueller Company has manufactured Fermenters, Chip Tanks, Lagering Tanks, Bright Beer Tanks and other vessels for the largest breweries across the nation. Now the same high-quality Cellar Tanks are available to the craft brewing industry.

Each component of Mueller Cellar Tanks is carefully designed and crafted for the application. Heat transfer and refrigeration expertise are applied to ensure that the optimum type and size of temperature control are utilized. Superior material finishing with welds ground flush and ribbon polished ensures that cellar tanks are sanitary and easy to clean. Insulated vessels also have completely welded outer jackets for longer life and enhanced appearance.

Mueller Cellar Tanks are available in standard sizes from 15 to 100 barrels, and Mueller can custom fabricate Cellar Tanks in sizes up to 1,000 Bbls in the plant and sizes over 1,000 Bbls in the field. Mueller Cellar Tanks are built to satisfy the needs of brewers from start-up through expansion.

For the best process control, Bright Beer Tanks meet ASME Code and Fermenters have 60 degree cones.
