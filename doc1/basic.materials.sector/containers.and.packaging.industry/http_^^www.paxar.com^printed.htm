Do you need Printed Labels?

Paxar's Printed Label Group is a vertical manufacturer that offers a variety of printed label products. In addition to producing woven edge narrow fabrics, they have custom tape dyeing capabilities. Labels are supplied cut and processed, or delivered in continous trim formats. This division supports the Quick Care printing program, which produces care and integrated labels within a week.
