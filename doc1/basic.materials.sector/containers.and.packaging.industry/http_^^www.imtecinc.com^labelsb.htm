StickTuff 7107 Designed for the most demanding adhesive needs, StickTuff is a vinyl label that provides durability for many manufacturing processes. It's excellent for metal and plastice pipe marking. And it's resistant to moisture, humidity, water and cold.

ScratchFree 7168 A general purpose, high-durability polyester label with excellent smear and scratch, chemical and moisture resistance. It provides superior print quality with a high-gloss finish. UL recognized.

StaticSafe A white, polyimide label that uses proprietary components to significantly reduce electrostatic discharge (ESD)generated upon removal from the label liner (less than 100v) and circuit board (less than 500v). Studies show that for every $1 spent on ESD prevention, there is a $3 return on investment. Companies with no anti-static precautions experience 20 percent or more product reject rates.

STS 2500 Series A polymide label available in 19 styles to meet environments that range from abrasion, water, solvent, chemicals, cold, tearing, high temperatures, mild cleaners and wave solder. Available in yellow gloss, light yellow matte, white matte, and white gloss.

HeatShield This polymer material is able to survive application temperatures up to 450 degrees Fahrenheit (232 Celsius), at one third the price of polyimide materials. It's ideal for labeling the top side of circuit boards. Without protective over-lamination of the label, HeatShield withstands most of the common circuit board manufacturing processes used today, including high temperatures and cleaning solutions used in IR or vapor phase, and wave soldering. It's compatible with most thermal transfer printers currently in use.

PaintBlock Imtec engineers developed PaintBlock labels for the automotive and appliance industries, where painting is part of the assembly. The PaintBlock label is permanently affixed to the product early in the manufacturing process. Each label has one or more protective layers that cover the bar code information. After the product is painted or has gone through other treatment, the first layer is easily removed. If multiple treatments are applied, then a multiple layer PaintBlock label provides both protection and access to the information needed on the bar code. Simply remove the top layer of protective material after each treatment. PaintBlock lets the product be tracked throughout the manufacturing process, allowing you to take full advantage of the benefits of bar coding. It saves time and money.

SunScreen This clear, printable film provides UV protection, high strength, dimensional stability, and solvent resistance. Ideal for outdoor applications, SunScreen is a polyvinyl fluoride material.

Easy. Imtec makes labels and ribbons specifically engineered for use with applicators. As a result, you can expect peak performance, with minimal applicator down time. We never cut corners by using paper that's a lighter grade, rougher, or has a lighter gauge liner. We never substitute more wax for pigment in the ribbon, or skimp on process control. Companies worldwide have asked us to solve their tough labeling needs. Imtec solutions come with our ironclad guarantee.
