LabelKwik
Our latest Model LD6 label dispensers simplify the removal of preprinted labels from a liner. They are designed with Imtec reliable technology. LabelKwik dispensers quickly and efficiently dispense a variety of pressure sensitive label types and sizes, including clear and butt-cut labels. With LabelKwik, morale improves since frustration associated with removing a label from a liner is minimized. Production is increased as much as 100 percent, resulting in a rapid return on your investment. Money is saved on label costs since labels are less likely to be damaged.

LabelPro
The LabelPro Automated Label Applier is a heavy duty, simple to operate labeler that is capable of accurately applying a wide variety of pressure sensitive labels to the top, side, bottom or corner of almost any shaped product at speeds up to 60 per minute, depending on label size. The LabelPro can operate as a stand-alone labeler or it can be integrated with a wide variety of product handling devices. Sturdy mechanical design and construction assures dependable, accurate label placement and minimum maintenance.
