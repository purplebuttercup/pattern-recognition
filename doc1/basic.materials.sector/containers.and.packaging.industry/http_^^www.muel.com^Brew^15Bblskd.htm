15 Barrel Brewhouse

The 15 Barrel Three Vessel Skid Mounted Brew House is an example of how Mueller innovation enables us to offer customers highly engineered and superbly crafted products on a very cost-effective basis. The skid includes a 15 Bbl Mash Mixer/Brew Kettle, 15 Bbl Lauter Tun, and 15 Bbl Whirlpool in a compact design which includes all valves, temperature sensors, load cells, pumps and computer controls required for operation.

This 15 BBl system is designed as a show piece. The process piping and valving as well as the service piping are placed out of sight beneath the brewers platform. These items are hidden by an outer cladding giving aesthetic appeal. The 15 Bbl Skid is equipped with quiet pumps and valves selected to minimize noise. The exhaust system vents brewery odors directly outside so that it may be displayed in the open and tour groups or restaurant diners can enjoy viewing the brewing process.

The Brewer's platform is large enough for small tour groups to safely view the Brewhouse. Through a sight glass positioned at the work station the wort can be seen. With safety in mind, Mueller also insulates the head of each of the vessels in this skid mounted Brewhouse. All these features are contained in a compact skid which minimizes floor space occupation and can fit into a cargo container.
