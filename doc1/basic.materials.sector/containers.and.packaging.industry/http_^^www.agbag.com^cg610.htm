Ag-Bag Environmental
CG-610
10' x 200' EcoPOD.
Holds 185 Tons of Material.
1 1/2 - 2 Tons per Minute Fill Rate.

     The CG-610 is designed for the mid-sized operator. It is built with a cross-conveyor system to utilize side-delivery wagons or trucks. This is especially efficient when portable mixers are used to haul materials to the composting site. Better blending is accomplished using this method. The CG-610 can also be modified in the hopper to accept front loader filling.


    A 65-plus horsepower tractor with a 540 PTO is needed to power the unit. As the EcoPOD fills, the CG-610 and tractor are automatically moved forward. A braking system controls compaction to maintain porosity. The CG-610 can be a one-person operation by using the mixer-person to fill the EcoPOD as the blended material is unloaded.

    The CG-610 places the aeration piping inside the EcoPOD as it is being filled. An inoculant applicator is installed to apply starter bacteria which quickens the process.
