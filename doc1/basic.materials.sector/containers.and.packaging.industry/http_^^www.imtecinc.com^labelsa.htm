Products Overview

Labels

APPLICATIONS:
Shipping/Receiving, Compliance & Packaging; Outdoor Use; Tags; and Security

ApplyPrint White paper labels available in seven styles and adhesives to address various conditions of moisture, cold and humidity.
GreenCode With GreenCode, you eliminate the hassle of cutting labels off packaging waste prior to its disposal into a recycling bin. Ideal if you apply labels to pallets or packaging such as stretchwrap, shrinkwrap, fiber bundles, or plastic containers. These white labels are available in substrates of polyethylene, polyolefin or polyester.
DuraCode 7120 A general purpose polypropylene label with a cold temperature adhesive. It's commonly used with a SunScreen laminate. Has limited UV resistance for up to one year, and also is resistant to moisture, humidity and tearing.
TuffTag 7114 Excellent for outdoor use, this polypropylene tag has very high tear and moisture resistance. Also resistant to cold and abrasion.
Guardian 7036 A polyester security label that leaves the word "void"on its surface when the label is peeled away. User should test to ensure void message will transfer. Labels can be as small as 0.5"-1.25" and need lamination for chemical resistance.
TamperGuard7016
A destructible, tamper-evident, asset management label constructed of white vinyl. The label tears easily when removed. TamperGuard withstands high humidity, and the user should test surface application.
