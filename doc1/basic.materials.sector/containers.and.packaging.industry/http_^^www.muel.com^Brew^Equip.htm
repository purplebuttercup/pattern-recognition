Brewing Systems

Mueller offers fifteen barrel and larger brewing systems incorporating the most modern technology and Mueller's tradition of quaility craftsmanship. Each piece of equipment is designed with the latest brewing science research results relating to wort production in mind.




Brewhouse

Mueller brewhouse equipment is designed in a variety of capacities to meet the needs of the modern brewer. Grist Hopper, Mash Mixer, Lauter Tun, Brew Kettle, Internal Calandria, Whirlpool, Wort Receiver, Hot Liquor Tank, Cold Liquor Tank, Wort Sampling Station, and Plate Heat Exchanger are all manufactured in accordance with Mueller's strict quality standards.

Mueller also offers a state-of-the-art fully automated brewhouse for the fifteen barrel micro-brewery. This three vessel skid mounted brewhouse includes Mash Mixer/Brew Kettle, Lauter Tun, and Whirlpool.

Cellar Tanks

Mueller cellar tanks provide optimum temperature control using Mueller's Temp-Plate heat transfer surfaces and are available in a range of sizes starting at fifteen barrels. Custom design and construction are available for unique applications and special dimensional needs. Quality engineering and craftsmanship are evident in Mueller cellar tanks.



Controls

Whether manual operation or full automation is preferred, Mueller brewery controls are designed to meet the needs of each brewery. Paul Mueller Company's engineering staff includes specialists who combine PLC control with PC based interfaces to create a brewery control package which is durable, easy-to-use, and consistent with Mueller high-quality standards.



Wort Cooling &
Brewery Refrigeration Systems

Refrigeration is one of the areas where Mueller historically has surpassed other equipment manufacturers. Mueller wort cooling and brewery refrigeration systems are energy and cost efficient. They have proven their value with excellent reliablity and competitive cost.
