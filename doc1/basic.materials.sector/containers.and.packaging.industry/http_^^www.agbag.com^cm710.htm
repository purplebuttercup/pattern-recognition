CM-710
10' x 200' EcoPOD.
Holds 185 Tons of Material.
3 - 5 Tons per Minute Fill Rate.
    The CM-710 is designed for the large site operator and for use with a dump truck, live floor or front-end loader. It is equipped with a 10' wide and 10' long conveyor platform, powered hydraulically to transport material into the rotor packing teeth. The platform has both forward and reverse control, as well as 0' to 90' of travel per minute. The compaction rotor is 8' wide with 1" quadra-staggered fingers. The fingers have replaceable tine caps as wear items for easy replacement.

    Powered by a 120hp John Deere diesel engine, with a 65 gallon hydraulic reservoir and 65 gallon fuel tank. The operator platform provides a complete control panel with an optional cab enclosure.
 
 

As the POD fills, this motorized machine is self-propelled forward and compaction is controlled with a braking system so that porosity is maintained. The CM-710 places the aeration piping inside the EcoPOD as it is being filled. A 50-gallon tank with a 12-volt sprayer evenly applies inoculant (bacteria) to the mix for a quicker start.
