      Kaiser Aluminum International

    he company's commitment to growth in strategic areas worldwide is managed by Kaiser Aluminum International (KAI), which focuses Kaiser Aluminum's complete range of capabilities -- from bauxite through fabricated products -- on selected international ventures. These efforts are particularly targeted on countries with strong fundamentals for aluminum production and/or high potential for growth in demand for aluminum products. KAI also markets and manages the worldwide application of Kaiser's alumina and primary aluminum technology, which is installed in facilities in the U.S., the U.K., Ireland, Sweden, Germany, Sardinia, Russia, Ghana, India, Australia, and New Zealand.

    China -- Kaiser Aluminum China Inc. works with Kaiser's other businesses to develop opportunities in China. Since 1995, Kaiser has formed three joint ventures in primary aluminum and extruded products.

    Russia -- Through its office in Moscow, Kaiser Aluminium Russia, Inc., develops opportunities such as the technology upgrades recently completed at the Krasnoyarsk smelter in Siberia and at the Nadvoitsy smelter near the Finnish border.

      Kaiser Aluminum Commodities: Alumina and Primary Aluminum

    aiser Aluminum Commodities focuses on the company's alumina and primary aluminum businesses. Its marketing arm, Kaiser Commodities Marketing, is charged with ensuring that the company seeks out and maximizes market opportunities that arise from the strategic linkage between those two businesses.
