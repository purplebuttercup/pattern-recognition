About Cyprus Amax

A worker at the El Abra copper mine in Chile
Cyprus Amax Minerals Company employs approximately 10,000 people worldwide. The Company has extensive reserves of copper, molybdenum, coal, gold, and lithium and is exploring for base and precious metals throughout the world. With operations, projects, and programs across the U.S. and on six continents around the globe, Cyprus Amax products help improve the quality of life.

Cyprus Amax is:

A leading copper producer in the United States and South America.
A top American and Australian coal producer.
The world's leading producer, processor, and marketer of molybdenum in the United States and Europe.
A holder of substantial positions in gold in the United States, South America, and Russia.
The world's largest producer of lithium in the United States and Chile.
A leading worldwide exploration company.
A company with a rich historical tradition that can trace its roots back to 1887.
As concerned about the environment as you are.
Proud of our greatest asset--our people!
