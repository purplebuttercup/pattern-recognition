SPCC DECLARES 30 CENT DIVIDEND

NEW YORK, N.Y., April 25, 1996 -- Southern Peru Copper Corporation (NYSE:PCU) today declared a quarterly dividend of 30 cents per share payable June 3, 1996 to shareholders of record at the close of business on May 16, 1996.

SPCC is the largest mining company in Peru and one of the 10 largest copper mining companies in the world. In 1995, it produced 278,600 short tons of copper, 3.0 million ounces of silver and 8.0 million pounds of molybdenum. Shareholders in SPCC are ASARCO Incorporated (NYSE:AR) (54.1%), a subsidiary of the Marmon Corporation (15.0%); Phelps Dodge Overseas Capital Corporation (13.9%) and common shareholders (17.0%).
