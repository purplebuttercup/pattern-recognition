Woodgrain and Other Printed Products

CFC International provides highly-functional coatings to the ready-to-assemble furniture and manufactured housing industries that simulate woodgrain and marble patterns. CFC's newest product offering, ArmoritePlus™, provides the best abrasion,scratch and chemical resistance available.

These products can be applied to either medium density fiberboard or plastic substrates.
