Commitment to Superior Quality and Service

Praxair is committed to providing superior product and service quality: specified purity, on- time delivery, dedicated service and expert support whenever and wherever needed. This diligence enhances our reputation for value-added delivery and service.

At Praxair, quality begins with our full line of gases (below). A full range of purities -- in some cases up to 99.9999 percent -- is available. We offer flexible supply options for both gaseous and liquid products. Praxair experts supervise the selection, installation and start-up of each supply system and assist in establishing delivery arrangements. In some cases, Praxair also offers on-site gas management services for customers. These include receiving, inventorying and monitoring gases and related systems.

To further enhance our delivery systems, we have forged innovative customer partnerships, and maintain sophisticated logistics and monitoring networks that ensure products arrive promptly and safely. Today, the majority of Praxair's production and customer service facilities worldwide have received International Organization for Standardization (ISO) 9002 certification, ensuring product and service consistency.
