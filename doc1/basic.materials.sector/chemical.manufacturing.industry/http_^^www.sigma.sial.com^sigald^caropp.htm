U.S. National Sales Manager - Tissue Culture
for our Fine Chemicals Division, a leading supplier of bulk chemicals and provider of raw materials and custom synthesis services to our leading edge customers in the pharmaceutical, biopharmaceutical, diagnostic and industrial markets.

We are currently seeking a U.S. National Sales Manager of tissue culture products and manufacturing capabilities for Sigma-Aldrich Fine Chemicals. The manager will hire, train and motivate salespeople and supporting staff to drive sales in the Biotech, Biopharmaceutical and Diagnostics Manufacturing markets. Other responsibilities include customer partnering, pricing management and developing sales strategies.

Requirements include:

  • B.S. degree in Biology or other Life Science
  • 3-5 years experience selling to the Biotech or Biopharmaceutical industry - specifically technical products to the process development or manufacturing groups
  • Management or Supervisory experience within a sales or marketing organization
  • Tissue Culture Sales or Sales Management experience a plus
  • Production or Process Development in Biotech, Biopharmaceutical or Diagnostic Industries is beneficial
