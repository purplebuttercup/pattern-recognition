Our People

Contributing to the success of the Sybron Chemicals Inc. specialty businesses are over 750 highly skilled and motivated employees worldwide. Our peoples' knowledge and enthusiasm for the job at hand is the key to the excellent performance of our Company. This, combined with energetic and supportive management, provides the foundation for Sybron Chemicals' performance and profitable growth.

Helping our employees continue to achieve excellence and satisfaction, both in their work and as individuals, is a major Company goal. We place a high priority on in-depth training and growth opportunities for our people, fostering an atmosphere that encourages their best.

Employee Accomplishments

Sybron Chemicals Inc. is proud to recognize the achievements and contributions of employees throughout the organization, through a variety of programs. Criteria for these awards includes one or more of the following: team player, takes initiative to solve problems, provides leadership and support in achieving company goals, displays an attitude that inspires others to do their best, significant cost savings in any area, and building new business. Recognition may be from manager to employee, manager to team, or peer to peer.
