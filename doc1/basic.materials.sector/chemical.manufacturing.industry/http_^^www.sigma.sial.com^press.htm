Sigma-Aldrich Press Releases

April 10, 1997 - Research Biochemicals International joins the Sigma-Aldrich family of companies

May 28, 1997 - AlliedSignal Inc. and Sigma-Aldrich Corporation announced today that they received formal clearance from the Federal Cartel Office of Germany for Sigma-Aldrich’s acquisition of an interest in a new partnership, RdH Laborchemikaliem GmbH & Co., KG.

August 20, 1997 - Sigma-Aldrich Corporation announced today the pending acquisition of Carbolabs Inc., Bethany, Connecticut.
