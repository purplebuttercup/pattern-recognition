DIRECTIONS TO ADM TRONICS, Inc.

BY CAR

From New York City

Take George Washington Bridge to the Palisades Parkway North to Exit 5S which is Route 303 South. Proceed to third traffic light (McDonald's on corner) and make left onto Pegasus Avenue. ADM Tronics is on right.

From South Jersey

Take New Jersey Turnpike North towards George Washington Bridge. Just before Bridge, enter Palisades Parkway North and follow directions above.

From Western New Jersey and Pennsylvania

Take Route 80 or 78 East to NJ Turnpike North and follow directions above.

From New York State and New England

Take Tappan Zee Bridge and follow signs to New Jersey. Proceed to exit for Palisades Parkway South. Take Palisades Parkway South to exit 5S and follow directions above.

BY TRUCK

From New York City

Take George Washington Bridge to Route 9W North. Go 7 miles to Closter Dock Road Exit on left. Make left onto Closter Dock Road and go 3 lights (including Blinkers). Make right onto Piermont Road. Go 5 miles and make left onto Paris Avenue. Go to first traffic light and make right onto Livingston Avenue. Go to first light (McDonald's on corner) and make right onto Pegasus Avenue. ADM Tronics is on right, loading dock in rear.

From South Jersey

Take NJ Turnpike North to Route 9W North (just before the George Washington Bridge) and follow the directions above.

From Western New Jersey and Pennsylvania

Take Tappan Zee Bridge and follow signs to New Jersey. Proceed to exit for Route 303 South. Take Route 303 South (first light in New Jersey, McDonald's on corner). Make left onto Pegasus Avenue. ADM Tronics is on right, loading dock in rear.
