Precision agriculture is usually associated with technology. At Terra, we define Precision In AgricultureTM as one of the ways to become more successful on the farm. It's knowing what tools to use and how to use the information the tools generate. The information, or data, lets you see and better understand the variability in every section of your fields. That data gives you the power to understand and make management decisions to influence those variables.

Precision agriculture could also be called site-specific farming because the data you collect allows you to see what's occurring in smaller sections of your field. With that kind of information, and the ability to see and then influence the variations in your fields, you can make adjustments that benefit you agronomically, economically and environmentally. You can start by learning the current industry terms and working with Terra to understand and use new technology.
