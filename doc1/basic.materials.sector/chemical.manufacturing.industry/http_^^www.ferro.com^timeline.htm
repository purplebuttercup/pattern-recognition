  Ferro Corporation Timeline
 

Ferro was founded at this plant on East 56th Street in Cleveland, Ohio, in 1919 - the year of the first transatlantic flight and the start of Prohibition. The plant, much expanded in both size and capabilities, is still in use today producing porcelain enamel frit, ceramic glaze frit, inorganic color pigments and electronic and specialty glass materials.
