Sybron Chemicals Inc. is the sum of its specialty chemical businesses.
Each is built on a market-oriented team.

Our component businesses operate with an entrepreneurial spirit, always staying close to the customers while focusing on product development, applications, marketing, and sales.

Our people understand their customers' unique needs and are able to respond quickly to changing market demands. For example, we are meeting today's challenges with our advancements in industrial waste treatment, groundspill remediation, and products and methods to produce pure water.

Our manufacturing capabilities encompass a broad range of techniques, from basic mixing and blending to fermentation and sophisticated reaction chemistry. Our varied end-user industries include electronics, utilities, oil refining, textile dyeing and finishing, municipalities, pharmaceuticals, steel, chemicals, and automobile manufacturing.

Our activities fall into two general business areas:

Environmental Products and Services

The Company's environmental products and services and related materials consist of water treatment products, biochemicals and specialty polymers:


Ionac® resins play a major role in providing pure water for industrial and domestic use worldwide. They also are becoming increasingly important in selective metal recovery and chemical processing.

Through our subsidiary, Purification Products Company, we manufacture and supply RO elements for purifying home drinking water and producing high quality water for other applications.


Our specially adapted bacteria are finding increasing use as the active components in consumer and institutional sanitary products, such as the new line of drain build-up removers. Also, we are developing and using these bacteria to degrade chemical wastes generated by industry. A further extension of this knowhow is being utilized to clean up soil that has been contaminated by oil and chemical leaks and spills.

Textile Chemical Specialties

We are a worldwide manufacturer of specialty chemicals for the textile industry with customers in all the major markets: North, South and Central America, Europe, Africa, and the Far East. We have manufacturing sites strategically located throughout the world to service our ever-growing global market. We offer a complete product line that encompasses all areas of the textile market including the preparation, dyeing, finishing, and printing of knit and woven fabrics along with garment, denim, and hosiery processing. We are a leader in innovative chemistries for the textile market and have helped our customers improve in the areas of Quality, Productivity, Environmental Concerns, Safety, and Competitiveness.

We invite you to take a look and see some of the exciting new ideas we have to offer the textile industry.
