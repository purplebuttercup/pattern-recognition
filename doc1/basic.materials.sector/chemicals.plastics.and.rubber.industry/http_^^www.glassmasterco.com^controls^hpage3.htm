Our Versatility is at your Service! 
      
At Glassmaster Controls Co., Inc., we engineer products for specific applications and make them the best that can be made - whether we manufacture five or five million pieces.  Using our Unified Systems Approach, we continue to improve the product and our service to our customers - through constant attention to quality, cost, and delivery. 

Established in 1897 as Shakespeare Controls, and later developing into Speareflex Flexible Controls, Glassmaster Controls Division was purchased from Speareflex in 1988.  After moving from Otsego, Michigan to a larger, 109,000 square foot facility in 1994, Glassmaster Controls Company, Inc. is now located in Kalamazoo, Michigan. 

Glassmaster Controls Company, Inc. is a one-stop source for all controls needs, whether it be for a flexible push/pull cable control, or for an electronic HVAC module.  Customers include automotive, agricultural, heavy-truck, marine, industrial, and specialty markets.   Products include flexible cable controls, heater/air conditioning panels, circuit-board based electronic controls and modules, switches, timers, and automotive diagnostic equipment .  Utilizing our capabilities, we are able to manufacture our own components, cable, and conduit and assemble the controls in-house, resulting in maximum control of quality, cost, and delivery which benefits us, as well as our customers.
