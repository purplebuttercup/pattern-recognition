Advanced Searches

The text search engine allows queries to be formed from arbitrary Boolean expressions containing the keywords AND, OR, and NOT, and grouped with parentheses. For example:

high-impact polystyrene
finds documents containing 'high-impact' or 'polystyrene'

 

high-impact or polystyrene
same as above

 

high-impact and polystyrene
finds documents containing both 'high-impact' and 'polystyrene'

 

high-impact not polystyrene
finds documents containing 'high-impact' but not 'polystyrene'

 

(high-impact not polystyrene) and thermoplastic
finds documents containing 'thermoplastic', plus 'high-impact' but not 'polystyrene'

 

trichloro*
finds documents containing words starting with 'trichloro'
