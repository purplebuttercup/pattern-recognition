To utilize the engineering capabilities, application, expertise, and market knowledge possessed by the employees of our Company to improve and develop products in the fields of monofilaments, reinforced composites, and flexible wire and molded plastic controls.

To explore and develop niche markets that require customized products, quick customer response and quality service and to give priority in the employment of assets and allocation of capital to those product lines which provide the highest return for the Company.
