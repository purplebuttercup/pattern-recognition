Films

AT Plastics is a fully integrated manufacturer of polyethylene products with over 35 years of experience in specialty grade ethylene vinyl acetate (EVA) polymers and compounds, agricultural/horticultural films. Advanced technology in polymers and films extrusion and over 20 years commitment to agricultural films research has made AT Plastics the leader in development of innovative products for agriculture. Experienced personnel operating modern extrusion and process control equipment and utilizing the most advanced polymers and additive technology ensure AT Plastics maintains the highest level of quality in the industry
AT Plastics co-extruded silage bags and silage sheets are utilized by leading farms around the world to preserve the highest quality haylage and silage. Quality bale tubes and stretch bale tubes are gaining acceptance worldwide as the preferred means of preserving bale haylage and hay. Continuing innovation and the application of new technologies ensures that farmers around the world can depend on AT Plastics to introduce the latest in quality films for advanced feed storage.

Durafilm ® greenhouse films products from AT Plastics Inc. have been the leader in the horticultural industry for over 20 years. Offered in a range of products suited to every use from over wintering nursery stock to Multi-season greenhouse covers. Durafilm ® products are renowned for their strength, durability and reliability. Proven in years of use by growers the world over Durafilm ® products have weathered extremes from Northern Canada to South Florida and Saudi Arabia to New Zealand.

Dura-Therm® Clear HVA has been designed specifically for green house growers in Canada and Northern United States. Countries with specific problems with heavy snow loads, hail, blowing sheets of ice and low light levels during winter months. This special blend of resins will give that added security, excellent light transmission and I.R. retaining properties. You can expect trouble free film for 4 seasons or more if used as a double layer

Features

  • Uniform 200 um thickness (8mil)
  • Will fit 95% of current poly locks
  • 92%-93% light transmission
  • State of the art UV stabiliser
  • Excellent IR retaining properties
  • Each roll identified for quality control purposes
  • Guaranteed replacement against manufacturing defects
AT Plastics Inc. Dura-Therm® Clear HVA is backed by a North American greenhouse film distributor network and by a research development program designed to keep AT Plastics Inc. constantly aware of regional greenhouse growers' needs.

Dura-Therm® Clear HVA is available in a wide range of custom sizes, including layflat tubing in lengths of up to 300 feet.
