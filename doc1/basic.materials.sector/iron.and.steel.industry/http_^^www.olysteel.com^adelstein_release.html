Olympic Steel Management Change

CLEVELAND, Ohio - (September 2, 1997) Bruce S. Adelstein, Vice President-Operations of Olympic Steel, Inc. (Nasdaq: ZEUS) has ceased to be an Officer and employee of the Company. Mr. Adelstein's Cleveland Division operational responsibilities will be assumed by Raymond A. Walker, General Manager of the Cleveland Division.
