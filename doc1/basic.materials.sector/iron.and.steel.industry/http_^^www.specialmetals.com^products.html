PRODUCTS

 

BILLET PRODUCTS

SMC produces a wide variety of Superalloy Reforging Billet including standard rotor quality UDIMET alloy 718, Fine Grain UDIMET alloy 718 for Direct Age applications, Waspaloy, UDIMET alloy 901, UDIMET alloy 706, UDIMET alloy D 979 as well as the popular MarAging Steels.

SMC Billet has been approved by all major Gas Turbine manufacturers for critical rotor applications. Billet sizes are available from 5 inches in diameter up to 26 inches in diameter in rounds, squares, and octagons. Product is available as Random Length Billets, Multiples, or Cut increments traceable back to original ingot locations. Peeled product is available up to 14 inches in diameter. Lathe turned product is available over 12 inch diameter. In-house Immersion Ultrasonic Inspection Capability to #1FBH, #2FBH or #3FBH standards (0.4 mm dia. or 0.8 mm dia., or 1.2mm dia.). 10,000 or 45,000 pound Vacuum Induction heat sizes available (13,000 or 20,000 kgs).

 

BAR PRODUCTS

SMC supplies the highest quality Rolled Bar to the world-wide aerospace industry. Centerless Ground or Peeled Machining and Reforging Bar is available in over 30 alloy systems encompassing the entire Superalloy market.


Rolled Bar products in the 0.40 inch to 5 inch diameter range (10 mm to 127mm). Superior quality producer of the difficult to process Nimonic Alloys including UDIMET alloys 80A, 90, 105 and 115. High quality MarAging steels for aerospace and military markets. Developer of UDIMET alloy 720 for Land Gas Turbine blading and Aircraft Gas Turbine high strength disc applications. Annealing and Precipitation hardening heat treatments available from in-house equipment. Immersion Ultrasonic Inspection available up to 5" diameter.

 

DENTAL PRODUCTS

SMC is established as an industry leader in the research, development and manufacture of today's advanced dental amalgams. Our silver base almagam alloys, first introduced in 1975., are now recognized throughout the world under various well known trademarks. Each alloy is produced under strict quality control standards by a proprietary atomization procedure developed by Special Metals.
