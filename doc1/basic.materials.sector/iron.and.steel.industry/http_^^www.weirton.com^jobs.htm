Weirton Steel Corporation is an equal opportunity employer and offers competitive salaries and a benefits package that are among the best in the industry.
Benefits Package

Located in Weirton, W.Va., Weirton Steel Corporation is the state's largest single employer. The city of Weirton, now home to more than 22,000 people and 20 industries, was founded by industrialist Ernest T. Weir, who in 1909 founded Weirton Steel. Located in the state's panhandle and spreading through Hancock and Brooke counties, the town of Weirton now consists of five communities: Holidays Cove, Weircrest, Weirton Heights, Marland Heights and Downtown.

Weirton residents also enjoy close proximity to Pittsburgh, Pa., as Weirton is located just 25 miles west of the Pittsburgh International Airport. The city of Pittsburgh offers a combination of diverse cultural, night life, arts, educational, sports and business opportunities.
Official City Of Pittsburgh Homepage

Consistently rated as one of the safest cities in the country, Weirton offers a neighborhood atmosphere just miles away from the advantages of a larger metropolitan area like Pittsburgh. And, Weirton averages below the national average for the cost of living, according to the American Chamber of Commerce Researchers Association.

In addition, Weirton offers:
  • Two country clubs.
  • Recreation facilities including swimming pools, public parks, playgrounds and more.
  • More than 100 clubs and organizations including service clubs, lodges, fraternal organizations, auxiliaries, hobby groups, youth groups and other associations.
  • Thirty-four places of worship representing 40 different nationalities.
    • Both as a place to work and live, Weirton's cultural, educational and economic benefits complement nicely the warmth and security found among its people.
