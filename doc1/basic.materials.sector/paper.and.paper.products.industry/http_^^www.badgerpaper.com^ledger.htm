Badger Ledger

Badger Ledger is recommended for office applications requiring high strength and durability. This grade is manufactured in 20, 24, 28, and 32 lb. weights ( 17 X 22 - 500 ) to meet the needs of the most exacting customers.

Badger’s unique process capabilities make private watermark available to customers requiring this feature. Standard colors are white, buff, and green tint.
