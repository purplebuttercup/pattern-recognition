Whether growing and harvesting trees or manufacturing paper and wood products, at Boise Cascade, we continuously look for ways to build on our past environmental successes. The air and water around our manufacturing sites are cleaner. We're reducing manufacturing wastes and developing new ways to reuse and recycle the waste we do generate. Our foresters and biologists are demonstrating that you can harvest timber to produce the wood and paper products our society needs and maintain and enhance fish and wildlife habitat at the same time. With sound science as our guide, we will continue to work toward achieving meaningful and measurable environmental results.
