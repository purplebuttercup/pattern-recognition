Badger Paper Mills, Inc., a publicly owned company with stock traded on the NASDAQ was formed in 1929. The company produces packaging, printing, writing, and specialty papers. Facilities include processes to produce machine glazed paper, coated paper, and machine finished papers. Capabilities for printing, waxing, roll and sheet converting and packaging complement the manufacturing capabilities.

Products are distributed through merchant sales organizations, commission brokers, and company sales representatives. Proprietary products are sold under the Ta-Non-Ka®, Copyrite®, BPM, Envirographic®, Northern Brights and FRESHRAP® labels.

A wholly owned subsidiary, Plas-Techs Inc. provides flexographic printing capabilities and produces polyethylene bags.
