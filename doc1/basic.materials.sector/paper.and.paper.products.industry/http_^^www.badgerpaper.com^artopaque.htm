Artopaque

Artopaque is a premium No. 2 opaque that has a brightness of 88 and superior opacity. This combination makes it the perfect selection for two sided printing applications. Artopaque is available in a range of 40 lb. to 70 lb. basis weights in both smooth and vellum finishes.

Badger’s roll stocking with various sizes allows for quick response for your web printing needs.

 

Artopaque specifications are on a 25 X 38 - 500 ream size are:

Basis Weight   40 lb.   50 lb.   60 lb.   70lb.
Brightness   88   88   88   88
Opacity   89   93   95   96
Caliper   3.1   3.8   4.6   5.2
