Flexible Packaging Products

Waxed and packaging papers are made to specifications of the customer. Badger Paper Mills Inc. has 65 years experience providing products for a wide range of packaging equipment and applications. This experience provides Badger Paper Mills Inc. with a wide array of proven packaging products that can be directly applied to customer needs or be used as a basis for modifying custom made products for unique applications.

Machine glazed and coated products can be produced for packaging, barrier, and other industrial applications on the Yankee Paper Machine . Proprietary grades for packaging applications are sold under the FRESHRAP® name.

Paper products can be enhanced by Badger through waxing, printing, slitting, sheeting and delivered with various forms of packaging.
