Wausau Papers doth proclaim a contest of utmost skill and artistry to be named, "Designs of the Round Table." All designers feeling worthy of the challenge are invited to come hither with their finest works for Royal review.

Wausau Papers would like to invite you to be a part of our online design gallery, "Designs of the Round Table." Submit designs printed on Wausau Papers' stock and you could earn the Graphic Excellence Award and be featured in our new Web site gallery. The Grand Prize, designing a printed piece for Wausau Papers, will be announced on October 1, 1998. Entries will be accepted throughout the year and Graphic Excellence winners for each category will be announced on December 1, March 1, June 1 and September 1. Categories being judged include:

  • Annual Reports
  • Letterhead - may include envelopes and business cards when available
  • Text and Cover - newsletters, brochures, catalogs, posters, books, folders and miscellaneous materials


Rules:
  • Designs must be printed on Wausau Papers' paper. (Combinations with coated stock is acceptable.) Entries will not be returned.
  • Winners will be announced on December 1, March 1, June 1 and September 1.
  • Winning designs will appear on Wausau Papers' web site until the following quarterly judging.
  • All winners receive a "Graphic Excellence Award" plaque.
  • Entry constitutes end user's permission for usage.
  • Additional entries may be obtained through your local authorized Wausau Papers' Distributor or by calling 1-800-950-9764.
  • Grand Prize - The winner will be announced October 1, 1998, and will design a piece for Wausau Papers.


How to Enter:

Complete the entry form in full and include five printed samples. (Do not mount, fold, staple or mark on any samples.)

Send Entry to:    Wausau Papers
Attn: Designs of the Round Table
