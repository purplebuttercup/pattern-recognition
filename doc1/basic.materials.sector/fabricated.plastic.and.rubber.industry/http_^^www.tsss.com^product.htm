At Triple S, our production facilities feature industry-leading equipment designed for consistent quality and accuracy. Because they're standardized, these machines help eliminate performance variables that can slow or stop production runs. So, with Triple S, you get maximum efficiency and productivity.

Our array of equipment includes JSW molding machines ranging from 55 tons to 720 tons. A vertical injection press with a rotary turntable is also available for insert molding.

The JSW machines are equipped with controller-profilers that monitor seven molding parameters. An upper and lower limit are programmed for each of these parameters. Whenever one of the limits is exceeded, a visible and audible alarm sounds, alerting the operator to the nature of the problem and/or the corrective action that must be taken. True process control.

And the JSW machines are linked to the Mattec® ProHelp™ production monitoring system which monitors and collects data on a real-time basis. The result is a true shop-floor control system.

Isolated drying and handling for optimum results

In order to maintain ideal conditions, Triple S performs all material handling and drying in a central location isolated from the molding machines. A central drying system from Motan, Inc. maintains precise moisture and temperature controls at each drying hopper. And a Motan automated vacuum conveying system utilizes dry air to ensure ambient moisture doesn't reach the dried resins. _____
