Aluminum Extrusions

Tredegar's aluminum extrusions are used in a variety of residential building products. Examples include tub and shower enclosures, windows and doors, and carpet thresholds.

Tredegar produces a wide range of extrusions for the commercial construction market. Examples include door and window components, columns, curtain walls and storefronts. Depending on customer needs, extrusions can be painted or anodized in a variety of finishes.
