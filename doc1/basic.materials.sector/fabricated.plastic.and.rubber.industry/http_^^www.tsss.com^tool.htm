When you work with Triple S Plastics, there's no need for a third-party tool builder. We have complete tool building capabilities, including 48 journeymen toolmakers on-site. That cuts the time it takes to get your product to market. And it gives you quick and easy access to every step in its design and development.

Your part is assigned to one toolmaker. Together with a Triple S project engineer, that lead toolmaker is responsible for the tool building process, from start to finish.

Tap our diverse capabilities

Triple S has the equipment and expertise to ensure your mold is made accurately and efficiently.

We'll perform a computer simulation of the cutter paths prior to machining. Then we'll download the final part geometry, tool design and cutter paths to our automated CNC milling equipment or EDM machines. Final details are then meticulously crafted under the practiced eye and skilled hand of a journeyman toolmaker. We then run samples on one of our five on-site sampling presses, ensuring trouble-free production.

Whether your part requires a complex, multi-cavity, high-volume production tool, an aluminum prototype or a limited-life mold, your Triple S team will use the latest technology to ensure your parts meet or exceed specifications. The first time and every time.
