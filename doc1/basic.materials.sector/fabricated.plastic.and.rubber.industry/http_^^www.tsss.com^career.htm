Spotlight on the Tooling & Technology Centre:

World class tooling/molding facility looking for experienced, motivated people to fill five positions. We have two positions available for Computer Aided Designers. Three years experience in tooling and plastics processing, two years experience in mechanical drafting (blue print reading, geometric tolerancing, etc.), two years experience on 3D and/or solids based CAD system required. Multi-shift availability a plus. Unigraphics and/or Cimatron 90 experience a plus.

We have one opening for a Product Data Management Technician. Five years experience in tooling and plastics processing, three years experience in mechanical drafting (blue print reading, geometric tolerancing, etc.) two years experience on 3D and/or solids based version of either Unigraphics, Cimatron 90, or Pro Engineer required.

We have one position available for CNC Mill Operator for 2nd Shift. Experience on Fadal and Fanuc controls and programming experience a plus.

We have one position available for EDM operator for 2nd or 3rd shift. Experience on Charmilles sinker and wire machines preferred.

Applicants must be able to work independantly as well as within a team environment. We offer competitive salary, clean, comfortable, and smoke free environment, and state of the art technology. Our competitive benefits package includes paid vacation, paid holidays, 401k, stock purchase, health/dental/vision insurance, life/disability insurance, tuition reimbursement and bonuses based on divisional/corporate goals.

Salaries for all positions will be based on experience. Triple S Plastics dynamic team environments believe in providing potential for growth. Please submit salary requirements with resume to:
