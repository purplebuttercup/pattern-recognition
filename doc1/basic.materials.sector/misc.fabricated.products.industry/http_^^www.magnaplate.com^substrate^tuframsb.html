TUFRAM®

 For aluminum alloys.
Creates a steel-hard, dry-lubricated surface on aluminum
Reduces abrasion and corrosion
Resists wear and galling
Outwears and outperforms case-hardened steel, stainless steel and hard chrome plating
Smooth, slippery surface is ideal for superior mold release
Eliminates sticking and product "hangup"
Facilitates material flow and sanitary maintenance
Some coatings are USDA-approved/FDA-compliant
Permits use of lighter-weight aluminum as a replacement for heavier metals (e.g., steel)
