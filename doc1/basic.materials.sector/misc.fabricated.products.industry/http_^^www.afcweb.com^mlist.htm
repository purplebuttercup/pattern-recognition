Join the AFCWEB Electronic Mailing List!

AFC Cable Systems is excited to introduce a new way for you to obtain information about AFC and its products. Anyone who joins the electronic mailing list will receive periodic e-mail messages which include:

  • Quarterly Earnings Releases
  • New Product Releases
  • Exciting new updates to the AFCWEB Internet site

Join the mailing list today, and you'll be the first to know about the latest news at AFC.

To join the AFCWEB electronic mailing list, simply send e-mail to info@afcweb.com. In the body of the message type the following:

SUBSCRIBE AFCWEB (first name last name) (your e-mail address)

That's it! Periodically, you will receive an e-mail message from AFC.

If you would like to unsubscribe from the list at any time, simply send e-mail to the above address with the body of the message reading:

UNSUBSCRIBE AFCWEB (first name last name) (your e-mail address)
