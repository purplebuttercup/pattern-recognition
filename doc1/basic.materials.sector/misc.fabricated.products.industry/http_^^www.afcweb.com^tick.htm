Download the AFC News Ticker!
The AFC News Ticker provides a direct feed of the latest headline news, stock quotes, sports scores, and weather reports from Yahoo! for any Windows 95 or Windows NT 4.0 machine connected to the Internet.

This is what the ticker looks like


The above demo is using sample (but current) data. The real news ticker allows you to select your own custom data. Double-click any story, stock, or weather report to see the full text of the story.

The ticker resides directly on your Windows 95 or Windows NT 4.0 taskbar or runs as a window "glued" to your desktop. It even runs inside your favorite Windows' screen saver.

It uses preferences established in your My Yahoo! account to provide custom news and information that you select.

It requires Netscape Navigator or Microsoft Internet Explorer (version 3.0) and either Windows 95 or Windows NT 4.0.
