Below are some helpful search tips for locating the distributor nearest you!:

  • Please note that most of our distributors are located in outlying areas.
  • In order to locate the one nearest you enter the:
    • area code (you can enter from 1 to 3 digits of the area code)
    • zip code (you can enter from 1 to 5 digits of the zip code)
    • state (rather than a specific city name)
  • To start another search, click the "Reset" button on the Distributor Locator screen.
  • To find a specific product type click one of the following selections on the Distributor Locator screen:
    • General Line
    • Audio/Video
    • Cords
    • Broadband/CATV
    • Industrial
    • Alarm/Security
    • Motor/Lead wire/Hook-up
    • Premise/Data
